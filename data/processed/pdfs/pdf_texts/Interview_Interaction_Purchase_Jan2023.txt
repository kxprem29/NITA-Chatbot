All the Heads of Departments/Sections are hereby informed that display the number of

candidates in the prescribed format to the Administration by 15/12/2022 positively for further
necessary action.

For Teaching positions, mandatory qualifications to be adhered by the Committee. Faculty
members, who do not full fill qualification can apply for Technical-cum-Teaching Assistant
having qualification of Master Degree at a monthly consolidated salary of Rs.40,000/- & the
Committee can recommend their case based on their competence. .
