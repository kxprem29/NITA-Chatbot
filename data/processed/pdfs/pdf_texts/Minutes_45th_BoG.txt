The 45" meeting of Board of Governors of National Institute of Technology Agartala was held
on November 10, 2018 at 10:30 am in the Board Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma : Chairperson of BoG and Director, NIT Agartala.

(2) Shri Amit Shukla : IFS, Director, Higher Education Dept., Govt. of
Tripura (nominee of Principal Secretary,
Education)

(3) Prof. Sharad Gokhle : Associate Dean of Infrastructure, Planning &

Management, IIT Guwahati (nominee of the
Director, IITG)

(4) Shri Alok Ranjan Roy : Member, Addl. Chief Engineer, PWD,
Choudhury Government of Tripura, Agartala.
(5) Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha : Member, Professor, M. E. Dept., NIT Agartala.
(6) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharya : Member, Asstt. Professor, E.E. Dept., NIT
Agartala.
(7) Dr. Govind Bhargava : Secretary, Registrar, NIT, Agartala

The Joint Secretary and Financial Advisor, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India; the Special Secretary /
Additional Secretary / Joint Secretary dealing with Technical Education, Dept. of Higher
Education, MHRD; Prof. Sanghamitra Bandyopadhyay, Director, Indian Statistical Institute,
Kolkata and Prof. Vijayalakshmi Ravindranath, IISc, Bangalore could not attend the meeting.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Board of Governors and Director of NIT Agartala
presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors.

The Chairperson invited the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 45.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 44‘ Meeting of Board of Governors,
NIT Agartala.

The minutes of 44" Meeting of the Board of Governors held on August 09, 2018 were confirmed
after deliberations on the comments submitted by the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India vide letter

Item No. 45.7: Consideration and approval for allowing interdepartmental flexibility
for allowing scholarship to Ph.D scholars of Engineering Departments
from the fund of TEQIP-III.

The BoG approved and confirmed interdepartmental flexibility for allowing scholarship to Ph.D

scholars from the fund of TEQIP-III, without any restriction of numbers.

Item No. 45.8: TDS liabilities during the financial year 2007-08 to 2010-11.

The Board deferred the agenda for next meeting.

Item No. 45.9: Any other issue with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 45.9.1: To re-appoint Dr. Bidyut Bhattacharya as Professor in the Dept. of
ECE and Dr. N. K. Kole as OSD to Director at NIT, Agartala “On
Contract”.

The Board approved and confirmed the contractual appointment of Dr. N.K. Kole as OSD
against the post of Professor on consolidated pay of Rs 175000/ per month for a period of
initially one year, which can further be extended to two more years on the annual satisfactory
performance.

For Dr. Bidyut Bhattacharya, one of the Board member pointed out that BOG, in one of the
meeting, has put up restrictions for his further extension of services at NIT Agartala. In view of

this, the Board was of the opinion that all the facts be brought to the forthcoming BOG meeting.

Item No. 45.9.2: To consider the name of Sports Complex as “Bharat Ratna Atal
Bihari Vajpayee Sports Complex”.

The Board approved and confirmed the naming of Sports Complex as “Bharat Ratna Atal Bihari
Vajpayee Sports Complex”.

The meeting ended with the vote of thanks to the Chair.
