MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF
TECHNOLOGY AGARTALA (NIT AGARTALA) AND TRIPURA STATE ELECTRICCITY
CORPORATION LIMITED AGARTALA (TSECL AGARTALA) FOR KNOWLEDGE
SHARING AND CAPACITY BUILDING IN THE CONCERNED AREAS.

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is made on the between
NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AGARTALA (NIT AGARTALA), established by
an Act of Parliament in 2006; hereinafter referred to as First Party.

AND

TRIPURA STATE ELECTRICITY CORPORATION LIMITED AGARTALA (TSECL
AGARTALA), a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 1956, having its Registered Office
at R7RM, Banamalipur, Agartala, Tripura-799001 (hereinafter referred to as “TSECL” which
expression shall unless repugnant to the context herein include its administrators, successors,
executors and permitted assigns) hereinafter referred to as Second Party

(NIT Agartala and TSECL are hereinafter individually referred to as the ‘Party’ and collectively as
‘Parties’ )

Strengthen academia-industry interaction for knowledge sharing and capacity building through

collaboration.

CONFIDENTIALITY

a) During and for a period of five year from the date of disclosure, each party agree to consider as
confidential all information identified as confidential by the disclosing party in written or
tangible form or, if orally disclosed and confirmed in writing within thirty days of disclosure.

b) The obligation above shall not extend to any confidential information for which the receiving
party can prove that this information

e is in the public domain at the time of disclosure or comes within the public domain
without fault of the receiving party.

e is already known or become known to the receiving party.

e is received from a third party having no obligation of confidentiality to the disclosing

party.
¢ is independently developed by the receiving party; or
e is required to be disclosed by law or court order.

c) All data not identified as confidential can be used for technical and research publication. For
use of confidential data in publications written permission must be obtained.

d) Each Party agrees to use the confidential information of the other Party only for the Purpose of
this MoU.

TERMINATION

This MoU may be terminated by mutual consent of the parties. However, either party shall have
the right to unilaterally terminate this MoU giving 30 days prior written notice to the other party.
However, no such early termination of this MoU, whether mutual or unilateral, shall affect the
obligation of the participants under any agreement, Confidentiality clause as referenced in para 5
above, or any other agreement, entered into pursuant to this MoU shall survive any such
termination.

REPRESENTATION
Nothing in this MOU shall be construed to make a party a partner, an agent or legal representative

of other for any purpose. This MoU shall not create a legally binding agreement between the
parties.

CONDUCT OF MEETING

The coordinators of TSECL and NIT Agartala shall meet at least once in every six months at a
mutually convenient location for reviewing/discussing the progress of activities undertaken as per
this MoU.

ASSIGNMENT & TRANSFER

It is understood by the Parties herein that this MoU is based on the professional competence and
experience of each party and hence neither Party shall transfer or assign this MoU, or rights nor
obligation arising hereunder, either wholly or in part, to any third party without the other’s prior
written consent. Any attempted assignment or delegation without such consent shall be null and
void.

. COST & EXPENSES

TSECL shall sponsor the activities and Research/consultancy Project(s) based on the modalities
decided between TSECL and NIT Agartala in coordination and discussion with each other. This
will be subject to decision by TSECL Agartala. The details of the activities and Research Project
shall be worked out separately between TSECL and NIT Agartala based on discussion with each
other.

COUNTER PARTS

This MoU and each amendment, modification and waiver in respect of it may be executed and
delivered in counterparts, each of which will be deemed an original.

FURTHER ASSURANCES
Each Party shall execute such agreements, deeds and documents and do or cause to be executed or
done all such acts and things as shall be necessary to give effect to the terms and intention of this

MoU.

SETTLEMENT OF DISPUTE

Any dispute or differences arising out of or connection with this MOU, in the first place to the
extent possible, be resolved amicably between the Parties failing which the same shall be settled in
accordance with the provisions of Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 and its amendments
thereafter by a Sole Arbitrator to be agreed by both Parties.

Arbitration proceedings shall be held at Agartala and the language of the arbitration proceedings
and that of all documents and communications between the Parties shall be in English.

The Courts of Agartala shall have the jurisdiction in case of any legal dispute.
