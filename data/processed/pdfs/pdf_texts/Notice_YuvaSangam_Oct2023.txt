This is for information to all the students (UG, PG & PhD, Aged-18 to 30 years) of NIT Agartala that
Yuva Sangam (ahr) Phase-3 under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) is an initiative by
Government of India to strengthen people to people connect between youth belonging to different ;
states of India. Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat Yuva Sangam (aRFTA) Phase-3 will focus on
conducting exposure tours of youth between the one state to another other states & vice versa. It
will provide an immersive experience of various facets of life, development landmarks, recent
achievements and youth connect in the host state. During their visits, the youth will have a multi-
dimensional exposure under five broad areas — Paryatan (Tourism), Parampara (Traditions), Pragati
(Development), Prodyogik (Technology) and Paraspar Sampark (People-to-people connect). In this

connection all the interested students may register for this event using the link given below.
