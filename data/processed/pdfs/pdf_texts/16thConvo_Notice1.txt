16th CONVOCATION NOTICE
The 16th Convocation of National Institute of Technology, Agartala, will be held on, 4th November,
2023 (Saturday), at NIT Agartalafor conferring degrees to the graduates of AcademicSession 2022-23.
On behalf of National Institute of Technology Agartala, we invite all the degree recipients (UG, Dual
Degree, PG and Ph.D) to attend the 16th Convocation Ceremonyfor receiving degree. This notice details the
processof registration for the event.
A-1. Registration
For participating in the 16th Convocation, it is mandatory for all the degree recipients to register
themselves through an online registration portal (mis.nita.ac.in) and then click on the convocation
portal which is active from 4th September, 2023 to 12th Q0ctober, 2023. Digi-locker ID needs to be
mentioned mandatorily during registration. Individuals without Digi-locker ID need to register in
Digi-locker portalf irst. No registration fee is required during registration as fee is already paid.
To access the Online Registration Portal System, all the degree recipients are hereby informed to
follow the link i.e.https://mis.nita.ac.in.
A-2 Distribution of Robes:
The robes will be distributed to the registered recipients only, on the day prior to the Convocation,
i.e, on 3rd November. 2023 from 10:00 AM to 1:00 pm in respective Departments. Thereafter,
no robe will be issued. Degree recipients will not be allowed to enter into the Convocation
Hall/premises and they will receive their degree in absentia in absence of the robe.
It is also informed that, those degree recipients who are unable to complete their online
registration may collect their degree certificate in absentia from the Examination Section during
workinghoursafterthe Convocation.
A-3 Rehearsal:
The rehearsal of the Convocation programme has been scheduled on 3rd November, 2023 at 3:30
pm. The degree recipients who have registered and collected robes within the above mentioned
deadline, will only be allowed for the rehearsal and main event. Except rehearsal, no degree
recipients will be allowed toe nter the venue during Convocation.
