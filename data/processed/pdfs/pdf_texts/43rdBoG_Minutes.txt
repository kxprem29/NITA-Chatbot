Item No. 43.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 42" Meeting of Board of Governors,
NIT Agartala.

The Board of Governors (BoG) confirmed the minutes of 42" Meeting of the Board of
Governors held on December 27, 2017.

Item No. 43.2: Actions taken on decisions of the 42"’ BOG meeting of NIT, Agartala
held on December 27, 2017.

The Board of Governors noted the actions taken on decisions of the 42™ Meeting of the Board of
Governors, NIT, Agartala.

Item No. 43.3: To consider various recommendations and decisions taken by the
Senate at its 26" Meeting held on 14-03-2017.

The BoG ratified and confirmed Item No. S/26/18 regarding ‘Consideration and approval of
reduction of seats of B. Tech Civil Engineering and distribution of those seats to other
Departments’ and Item No. S/26/15 regarding ‘Consideration and approval of adoption of credit
system of Indian Institute of Technology, Madras under the exchange programme between
NITA and IITM’ of 26" Senate Meeting. Remaining decisions to be placed in the forthcoming
BoG.

Item No. 43.4: To consider various recommendations and decisions taken by the
Buildings & Works Committee (BWC) at its 40" Meeting held on 27-
03-2018.

The BoG suggested to place the recommendations/ decisions of the 40" BWC meeting to the
next Finance Committee meeting.

Item No. 43.5: To consider various recommendations and decisions taken by the
Finance Committee (FC) at its 29™ Meeting held on 24-03-2017.

The recommendations and decisions of 29"" Finance Committee meeting held on 24-03-2017 are
confirmed.

Item No. 43.6: To consider nomination of members by BOG to BWC and FC.

The BoG ratified the decision of nomination of two members as the Member of Finance
Committee viz. (1) Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha, Mechanical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala
and (2) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee, Asstt. Professor, Electrical Engineering Department, NIT
Agartala. BoG also ratified the nomination of Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha, Mechanical Engineering
Department, NIT Agartala as the Member of Building & Works Committee.

Item No. 43.7: Any other issue with the permission of the Chair.
Item No. 43.7.1: To consider the guidelines for utilization of Cumulative Professional

Development Allowance (CPDA) in NITs.

The BoG adopted and confirmed the guidelines for utilization of Cumulative Professional
Development Allowance (CPDA) for the faculty members of the Institute to perform the
activities i.e. (A) Presenting of Papers and attending National & International Conferences/
workshops, (B) Membership Fee for Professional Bodies, & (C) Contingent expenses, as
communicated by the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India, vide letter F. No. 35-6/ 2016-TS.III,
dated: 8" June 2017.

Item No. 43.7.2: To consider various recommendations and decisions taken by the
Finance Committee (FC) at its 30" Meeting held on 27-03-2018.

BoG ratified the recommendations of 30" Finance Committee meeting of NIT Agartala held on
27-03-2018.

The meeting ended with the vote of thanks to the Chair.
