At the very beginning Prof. Gopal Mugeraya, Director & Chairman, SENATE, NIT Agartala
welcomed all the members present in the meeting. He specially welcomed Prof. A.
and Prof. Saji
Vasudeva Adhikari, Prof. of Chemistry, NITK, Surathkal Gopinath,
Indian Institute of Management, Kozhikode, Kerala for attending the 20th meeting of
SENATE of this Institute. He introduced both the new members to the SENATE and gave
an account of the contribution they are making in their respective Institution. He also
expressed hope that their participation in the meeting of SENATE of this Institute will go a
long way in developing academic activities of this Institute. Prof. Mugeraya made special
mention of Prof. Umesh Mishra who is running newly established Centre for Carrier
Development (CCD) as Chairman. He then requested all the members to take part in the
discussion.
After the opening remarks of the Chairman, SENATE, Dr. Debasish Bhattacharya,
Secretary, SENATE welcomed all the members and took part in the discussion on all the
agenda points of the meeting. Dr. Bhattacharya then placed certain Agenda points before the
house for discussion.
