Item No. 50.5: To consider the proposal to recruit a Counsellor for the students of
NIT Agartala.

The BoG ratified the recruitment of Counsellor at NIT Agartala in addition to sanctioned post of
Non-Teaching staff.

Item No. 50.6: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of 43" Building &
Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Board noted Item No. 43.1 to 43.14, 43.17 and 43.19 to 43.23 of 43" Building & Works
Committee. The recommendation of 43 Building & Works Committee meeting held on June
21, 2019 with regard to Item No. 43.15, 43.16, 43.18 and 43.24.1 were approved.

Item No. 50.7: To consider the decision of temporary engagement of different
Teaching and Non-Teaching employees at NIT, Agartala on
contractual basis.

 

The Board noted the information.

Item No. 50.8: To consider the Guidelines for engagement of Visiting Faculty at NIT
Agartala.

The Board approved the guidelines for engagement of Visiting Faculty. Since the student ration

has taken quantum jump as compared to sanctioned faculty strength therefore, engagement will
be made additional to the sanctioned posts.

Item No. 50.9: To consider the Concordance Tables in respect of pensioners of NIT
Agartala.

The Board noted the status and decided to process the matter at Institute level, separate approval

in this regard is not required.

Item No. 50.10: To consider the nomination of Member to the Board of Governors.

The Board ratified the nomination of Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi), Assistant Professor, Chemical

Engineering Department, NIT Agartala as the Member of Board of Governors.

Item No. 50.11: Discontinuation of Interviews at Junior Level Post carrying the
Grade Pay of Rs. 4600/- or less [upto Level 7 in 7 CPC].

The Board adopted the guidelines in accordance with instructions issued by DoPT vide their

Office Memorandum No. 39029/ 01/ 2013- Estt (B)-Part dated 29" December 2015, which has
been communicated by the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India vide letter F. No. 35-4/ 2016-TS.III,

dated: 11" December 2019.

Item No. 50.12: Adoption of the guidelines for settlement of claims for compensation
as enunciated in various judgments of Hon’ble Supreme Court in
case of death/ permanent incapacitation due to unintended/
unforeseen occurrences during maintenance, operation and
provisioning of public services.

The guidelines of DoT vide Memorandum No. 36-11/ 2015-SR, dated: 5" December, 2016 and
Institute committee are approved. However, point no. 2 of the institute’s committee report may

be read as, the guidelines will be applicable for all employees who are not covered under Group
Insurance. The superannuated employees from any sector will not be covered under this scheme.

Item No. 50.13: To consider the issue mentioned below pertaining to the ongoing
TEQIP-III programme at NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the 4"" phase Procurement Plan of Rs. 49,89,440.00 (Rupees forty nine lakh

eighty nine thousand four hundred forty) only under TEQIP-III as per rules.

Item No. 50.14: To consider the report of the external review committee for
academic audit that visited NIT Agartala during October 21-22,
2019.

The Board perused and approved the report. Action taken report of the last external review

committee may be put up in the forthcoming BoG meeting.

Item No. 50.15: Any other issue with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 50.15.1: To consider the Annual Report for the year 2018-19 of the Institute.
The Board approved and confirmed the Annual Report 2018-19 prepared by the Institute
incorporating the accounts of the Institute for 2018-19 audited by Indian Audit and Accounts
Department, Office of the Accountant General (Audit), Tripura.

The meeting ended with vote of thanks to the Chair.
