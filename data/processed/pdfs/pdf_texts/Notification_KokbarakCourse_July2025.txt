NOTIFICATION
Sub: Admission in Kokborok (Tripura language) Certificate Course for the Academic
Session 2025-2026
Applications are invited from eligible candidates for admission in Kokborok (Tripura
language) Certificate Course (two semesters) for the Academic Session 2025-2026 of NIT
Agartala.
1. Intake capacity of students is 50, which will comprise mainly teaching and non-
teaching staff and students of NIT Agartala; however external students may also be
admitted if vacant seats are available.
2. Admission will be given to the students having requisite qualification on first come first
serve basis according to merit.
3. Application will be received from 09.07.2025 to 25.07.2025. The Application Form is
appended in Annexure – I, which is to be submitted to the office of the undersigned.
Selected applicants will be notified in the institute website at the earliest. Selection,
Admission and registration process will be completed within 25.07.2025. The classes
will begin from 26.07.2025 onwards.
4. Admission fee for the course is Rs. 1000.00 (Rupees one thousand only) per semester
(exempted for regular students of NIT Agartala). The fee should be paid in favour of
“REGISTRAR, NIT AGARTALA” in the form of crossed Demand Draft payable at
SBI, NIT Agartala Branch OR deposited in the Institute A/c. No. 30369892838 through
online. Payment receipt should be submitted along with the application form.
Minimum Educational Qualification for admission to the programme: -
1. Minimum required educational qualification is H.S. (10+2) passed with at least 40%
marks.
2. The selection will be made based on marks obtained in H.S. (10+2) and higher degrees
if any. Entrance exam may also be conducted if required (for external applicants only).
3. This notification has been issued as per the approval of the competent authority.
