NOTICE

 

   

Students who have taken admission in Ph.D program under the category of “full time with
scholarship” during academic session July-December 2024-25 are hereby instructed to apply for
MHRD (now MoE) scholarship by following steps given below.

1) Scholars should fill up the scholarship form online through the stipend module link of their
respective account in the MIS at mis.nita.ac.in (Administrative—Stipend—Transactions—
Apply Stipend) and take a print of the report generated after online submission.

2) After that they should fill up the undertaking form (as per prescribed format at Annexure-
A) and submit both the reports (hard copy) to the Office of the Dean(AA) latest by 20/09/2024,
duly recommended by the concerned Head of Department.
