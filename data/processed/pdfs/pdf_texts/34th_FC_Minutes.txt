Item No. 34.4: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.

The present status of TEQIP — III was noted.

Item No. 34.5: To consider the Assets & liabilities dues for an amount of Rs.
56,49,968 to the Govt. of Tripura.

The Finance Committee appreciated the efforts to resolve the long pending matter and
recommended to pay Rs.56,49,968/- to DHE, Government of Tripura to settle the assets &
liabilities dues. However, it was opined that a letter can be sent to MHRD to get the position
cleared.

Item No. 34.6: To consider fixation of license fee of different building to be
charged by NIT Agartala for use of Office Buildings, Academic
Buildings, Market Complex by Contractors/ Agencies/
Shopkeepers etc.

The Finance Committee approved the license fee, recommended by the committee.

Item No. 34.7: Absorption of Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar w.e.f. 05-05-2017
and counting of his Past Service for the purpose of Pension, Leave
etc. as per CCS Rule 1972.

The Finance Committee declined the proposal and made it clear that the absorption at NIT
Agartala is in violation of NIT statutes/ Recruitment rules. The FC directed to Registrar to get
his superannuation cleared from his parent department and inform to the NIT Agartala within
a month so that his services for the remaining period could be decided till the age of 62 years
or for a total tenure of 5 years, whichever is earlier.

The FC decided that his lien in his parent organization may be treated as extended upto
31.7.2019 (the date of his superannuation in his parent organization) and his pension
contribution and leave salary contribution during the period of his service at NIT, Agartala
till the date of his superannuation in his parent organization may be transferred to his parent
organization. The BoG also decided that w.e.f. 1.8.2019, his remuneration as Registrar, NIT,
Agartala may be re-fixed at his last pay drawn minus pension in compliance with DoPT O.M.
No. 3/3/ 2016-Estt. (Pay II) dated 1.05.2017 as amended from time to time. Also, in view of
Rule 55-A of CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972, he shall not be eligible to draw Dearness Relief on
Pension during the period of his re-employment as Registrar, NIT, Agartala. During the
proceedings of this discussion, the Registrar went outside and did not participate.

Item No. 34.8: To consider GPF balance in respect of Dr. Vidyut Dey, Assistant
Professor, Production Engineering Department.

The agenda will be put up in the forthcoming FC.
