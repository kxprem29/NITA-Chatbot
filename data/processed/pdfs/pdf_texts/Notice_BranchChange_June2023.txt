NOTICE

It is notified for information to all 1st year B. Tech & BT-MT (Dual degree programme)
students who are promoted to 3™ semester of various branches during the next academic session,
i.e. 2023-24 that Branch Change will be made based on the result of 1st Year (1st & 2"4
semesters) of the academic session 2022-23 and as per Rule No.6.7 of B.Tech regulation of NIT
Agartala.

6.7 The applicants may be allowed a change in branch, strictly in order of inter se merit,
subject to the limitation that the strength of a branch should not fall below the existing
strength by more than ten percent and should not go above the sanctioned strength by
more than ten percent.

Students who has secured a CGP +5 and above out of 10 (in the 1st Year) and also
completed all the common credits requirement in the first two semesters, in their first

attempt without having course requirement in the summer term examination are eligible and
if willing for branch change are hereby instructed to apply through the Online Portal

(https://mis.nita.ac.in ) from 23" June, 2023 . The students are advised to use the Username and
Password of their Institute MIS account.

The Guidelin lying Branch Sliding through MIS may be seen as follws:

LINK URL: - https://mis.nita.ac.in

1) STEP 1:- Students need to login with their MIS Username and Password after entering the above
URL

2) STEP 2:-After login Student will go to the Academic, after that transaction, after that Internal
Sliding Form.

3) STEP 3:-After that student will enter his/her AIR in the provided textbox for AIR, he/she also
give his / her branch preferences, upload his / her JEE Main Scorecard after that he / she will
check the declarations available for him / her and after doing all the task he / she will be able to
apply for branch sliding by clicking on Submit button.

The last date filling up the form is 5 July, 2023. Students are instructed to apply only once in
the portal.

This is issued with the approval of the Competent Authority.
