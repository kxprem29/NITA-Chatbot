To upkeep the standard and quality of teaching it is not possible

29 faculty members where as the minimum requirement is 53
nos. of faculties. An institute of eminence cannot afford to
dilute the quality of teaching.
The members of SENATE opined that the Institute should not
take the risk of enhancing of seat to 150. It is better to reduce it
to 120 or in extreme case it may go up to 150 if situation
demands.

each paper of B.Tech in the mid-term examination would be of 2.00
