WHEREAS, Sri Anirban Dutta, Associate Professor, Department of MESS, National Institute of
Technology, Jirania, West Tripura District has proposed the Medical Superintendent, Dr BRAM
Teaching Hospital for providing treatment to the employees / staffs and their dependable family
members as per rates of the CGHS Scheme;

AND WHEREAS, The Medical Superintendent, Dr BRAM Teaching Hospital has agreed under
approval of the management of the Society for Tripura Medical College & Dr. BRAM Teaching
Hospital to provide treatment as per rate of CGHS Scheme. Now, therefore, the parties here
above have agreed to execute this MOU on the Terms & Condition detailed below:-

 

1) The cost of treatment to be provided to the regular employees and their dependable
family members for medical care in OPD, cost of Investigation, cost of pathological
test, Cost of Dialysis and cost of other test done in Radiology Department ete. shall
be cash payment basis and bills will be generated as per CGHS Shillong rate which will
be reimbursed by the National Institute of Technology, Jirania, West Tripura District to
their regular employees.

2) The cost of IPD treatment and others to regular employees and their dependent
family members will be cashless based on CGHS Shillong rate and shall have to be
reimbursed by NITA within a period of 30 days as per bill to be raised by the Medical

ve Superintendent, Dr. BRAM Teaching Hospital. Any delay in making payment shall bring
hindrance in this MOU and to continue to provide Health Care Services.

3) The cost of services in the Radiology Department, C.T. Scan in particular shall have
to be ‘paid by the patients on spot because C.T. Scan Service is outsourced fo a third
party and the third party is not in a position to provide service on credit, Similarly,
Dialysis Service of Dr. BRAM Teaching Hospital is also outsourced to a Third Party and
the third party as per term of Contract with Society for Tripura Medical College & Dr.
BRAM Teaching Hospital, will not provide any service on credit. If needed the bills can
be certified by the doctors of Dr. BRAM Teaching Hospital.

4) All kind of treatments and pathological or radiology tests for contractual employees
and their family members will be cash payment basis based on CGHS Shillong rate on
production of NITA ID card, family ration card and Aadhar card,

5) For avoiding complicacy in defining the family members of the regular employees of the
NITA, it shall be the responsibility of the National Institute of Technology, J irania, West
Tripura District to define and identify the eligible family members and to provide
necessary certificate as to eligibility of the family members to receive treatment.

15) For in-door patient, after treatment and discharge of the patients from hospital, the
Medical Superintended shall raise bill towards cost of treatment as per CGHS rates and
