NOTICE

This is for general information to all concerned students that NIT Agartala signed
academic/research MOUs with IIT Madras, IIT Delhi, IIT Hyderabad and IIT Guwahati
where a scope for the eligible students is facilitated for a Direct Entry to PhD program
(surpassing the GATE) in the respective Institution of choice by following selection
procedure as per MoUs concerned. Eventually, all these programs are running well and
the MoUs are displayed in the website of NIT Agartala for better clarity and
understanding:

Interested students who are positioned among the top 10% in regards of the CGPA after
fifth semester having more than 8.5 CGPA are hereby requested to submit an
application along with their updated CV, marks statement and tentative research
plan through their Head of the Dept so as to reach this office by 27 of March 2023, 5
pm to this office. No application/ request would be entertained after the due date.

A student can apply maximum for two institutes of choice and name of the selected
students after scrutiny would be referred for summer internship/induction etc. after a
formal scrutiny at this end.
