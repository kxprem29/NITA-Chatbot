-IP 239: EP and AW tests for lubricants

Operational range

Speed: (300 -3000) + 100 rpm (High speed option is available)

1. Determine extreme pressure (EP), wear preventive (WP), frictional and

2. Test the quality of produced oil and verify the quality of oil procured
from suppliers.

3. Test wear preventive properties of nanoparticles derived from carbon
metal oxide, sulfides and nanocomposites.

4, Compare and estimate the deterioration of lubricant quality by

5. Test polymeric viscosity modifiers to achieve better shear stability and
