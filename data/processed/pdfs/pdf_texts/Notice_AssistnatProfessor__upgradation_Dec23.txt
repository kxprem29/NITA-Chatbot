Sub: Upgradation of existing Assistant Professor Grade-II (OC*) Pay Level-10 to Assistant
Professor Grade-II (OC*) Pay Level-11

With reference to Schedule ‘E’ of NIT Statutes (as amended vide Clause No. 5 (g) - (I)) of The
Gazette of India Notification regarding National Institute of Technology, Agartala, dated-17th
July 2023, applications are invited from faculty members of the Institute serving as Assistant
Professor ( Grade-II) in the Pay Level-10 (OC*) for upgradation to Assistant Professor Grade-II
Pay Level-11(OC*).

How to Apply;

1) Faculty members willing to apply for upgradation are to apply through enclosed
application form duly forwarded by HoD concerned and submit it in the Office of
Registrar latest by 05/01/2024, Friday. The completed application form is also required to
be emailed at registrarnita@rediffmail.com .

2) Application will not be received after the last date ie. 05/01 /2024 under any
circumstances.

3) It is to be ensured that the application is to be completed in all respect. No columns of the
application form be left blank. It should be properly indexed and spiral bound.

Enclosed: Application Form.
