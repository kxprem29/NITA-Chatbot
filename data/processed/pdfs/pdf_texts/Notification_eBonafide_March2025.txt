NOTIFICATION

Subject: Application for e-Bonafide Certificate through Institute MIS

This is to inform all UG, PG & Ph.D students that the e-Bonafide Certificate can now be
applied through the Institute's Management Information System (MIS).

Student needs to follow the below mentioned steps to apply for the certificate:

1. Log in to the Institute's MIS portal.
2. Click on "Online Payment" > "SBI Epay" > "Common Payment Interface".
3. Select "Bonafide Certificate".

Once student complete these steps, application for the e-Bonafide Certificate will be
processed and student will get the e-Bonafide certificate within the 5 working days after
applying in the MIS portal.
