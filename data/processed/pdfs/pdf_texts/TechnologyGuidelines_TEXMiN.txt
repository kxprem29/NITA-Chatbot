Technology Development (Td) Project Guidelines
Eligibility Criteria:
1. Applicants (Principal Investigator (PI) and Co-PIs): At least one of
the applicants (PI or Co-PIs) must belong to a Scheduled Caste (SC)
or Scheduled Tribe (ST).
2. Academic Position: Applicants must hold a regular academic
position at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Agartala.
3. Qualification: The PI and Co-PIs must hold a Ph.D. degree in
Engineering or Science.
Nature and Duration of Support:
1. Scope of Support: The TEXMiN-NIT Agartala Mining CPS Centre of
Excellence (CoE) will support individual researchers or research
groups working exclusively at NIT Agartala.
2. Project Duration: The project duration is strictly two (02) years.
3. Funding Limit: The maximum funding provided for the entire
project is â‚¹13 lakhs (Indian Rupees Thirteen Lakhs only).
4. Proposal Limit: An applicant may submit only one proposal per
call as a PI.
Selection of Application:
Selection will be based on the recommendations of a Program Advisory
Committee constituted by the competent authority (Head of the Institute).
Plagiarism:
1. Submitted proposals must present original ideas and content.
2. Plagiarism in any form will not be tolerated.
3. All proposals will undergo a similarity check, and any found to be
plagiarized will be rejected.
