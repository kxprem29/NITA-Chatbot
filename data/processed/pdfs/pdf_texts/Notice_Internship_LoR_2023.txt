Notice

Subject: Procedures to be followed for issuing Letter of Recommendation (LoR) with respect to
Training/Internship for the Undergraduate and Post Graduate students of NIT Agartala.

Based on the approval of the Competent Authority, vide File No- F.NITA.72 (61-CCD)/Internship
Coordinator/2020-21, students will apply for Online/Summer/Winter Internships/Training Program to
the CCD through proper channel. In this regard, approved format of Letter of Recommendation
(LoR), Student Declaration Form and Forwarding Letter have been included in MIS Portal of the
Institute. In this connection, the following procedures to be followed by the students for getting
Letter of Recommendation (LoR) with respect to Training/Internship for the Undergraduate and Post
Graduate students of NIT Agartala.

1) The individual student will fill up the Letter of Recommendations (LoR), Student Declaration
Form and Forwarding Letter alongwith student database information through their respective
MIS Account for Online/Summer/Winter Internships/Training Program.

2) The signed copies of two sets of the documents (LoR, and Student Declaration Form)
alongwith the Forwarding letter need to be submitted to the respective Student Internship
Coordinator by the student.

3) The above mentioned documents need to be submitted to the Departmental Faculty Placement

Coordinator by the Student Internship Coordinator of the respective Department/Branch. All
the cases will be sent to CCD for necessary approval.

4) Finally, the LoR will be signed by the Faculty Coordinators after getting necessary approval
and will be issued by the CCD.
