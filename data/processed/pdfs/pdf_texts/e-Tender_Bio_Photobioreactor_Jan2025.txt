Rotatable Glass Tubular Reactor (Horizontal type)

The tubular reactor will be connected to the jacketed reactor in both the sides, one side connected through

Brackets and base should be provided to place the tubes intact.

The LED panel will be placed at the back side with luminous intensity of 4000 — 10000 Lux, with multiple LED

lights across the length of the tubular reactor.

The LED lights should have adjustable wavelength (red, blue, green, white) and ON/OFF timer.

Cooling Media: distilled water, :
