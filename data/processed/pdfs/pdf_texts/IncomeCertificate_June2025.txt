valid Annual Family Income Certificate alon with affidavit by 30-06-2025 he/she will be ineligible for

shall pay full tuition fee.

9. In the event of failure of submission of above mentioned documents, on or before 30th June, 2025 and
rejected if any, their claim for fee remission will not be entertained for the Odd semester (2025-26).
