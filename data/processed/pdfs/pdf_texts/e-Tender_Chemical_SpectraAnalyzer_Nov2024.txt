QUANTITY

Color Spectra Analyzer

Specification should be as below or better

Spectrophotometer to color measure samples in many forms such as pellets,
solids, gelatins, powders, and pastes

Specification:

1. Illumination/ viewing system: di: ~8°, de: ~8° (diffuse illumination: 8°
viewing), SCI (specular component included) / SCE (specular
component excluded) switchable

. Integrating sphere: ~ 054 mm
. Light source: Pulsed xenon lamp or better
. Detector: Dual 32-element silicon photodiode arrays or better
. Spectral separation device: Planar diffraction grating or better
. Measurement wavelength range: ~ 400 to 700 nm or better

7. Measurement Wavelength pitch: ~10 nm

8.” Half bandwidth: Approx. 10 nm or better

9. Reflectance Measurement range: ~ 0 to 175%; Display resolution: ~
0.01

10.IIlumination area: ~ MAV: @12 mm

11.Measurement area: ~MAV: 08 mm

12. Repeatability: ~Standard deviation within AE*ab 0.04;

13.Inter-instrument agreement: ~ Within AE*ab 0.20

14.UV adjustment: ~ 0% only; 400 nm UV cutoff filter

15.Observer: ~ 2° observer angle, ~ 10° observer angle

16.Colorimetric values: L*a*b*, L*C*h, Hunter Lab, Yxy, XYZ, and color
difference in these spaces; Munsell (C)

17.Color difference equations: AE*ab (CIE1976) / AE94 (CIE1994) / AEOO
(CIE2000) / CMC (l:c) / Hunter AE / DIN990

18.Measurement time: Approx. 0.7 sec. (Measurement mode: SCI or SCE)

19.Minimum measurement interval: Approx. 1.5 sec (Measurement mode:
SCI or SCE)

20.Data memory: ~ 1,000 target data + 5,100 sample data

21.Battery performance: Approx. 3,000 measurements when
measurements are taken at 10-second intervals at 23°C with the
dedicated lithium battery

22. Viewfinder function: Yes

23.Display: color TFT-LCD with reversible portrait viewing mode or better

24. Interface: USB 2.0; Bluetooth

25.Power: Dedicated lithium-ion battery (removable), USB bus power
(with lithium-ion battery installed), Dedicated AC adapter (with
lithium-ion battery installed)

26.Charging time: Approx. 6 h or better

27.Operating temperature/ humidity range: Temperature: 5 to 40°C,
Relative humidity: 80% or less (at 35°C) with no condensation

 

Warranty: one year
