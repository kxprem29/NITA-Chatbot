RENEWAL OF MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING
BETWEEN

NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AGARTALA
TRIPURA, INDIA

AND

INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY MADRAS
CHENNAI, INDIA

In order to contihue to promote collaboration between National Institute of Technology, Agartala,
India and The Indian Institute of Technology Madras, India, the two universities desire hereby to
record their consent to the extension of the Memorandum of Understanding signed on 6" April 2018.

The extended effective term of the Memorandum of Understanding shall be for a period of five years
effective as of the date at execution by the appropriate officers of each signatory institution. The
Clauses of the Agreement remain unchanged.

In witness hereof, the duly authorized representative of each institution agrees to the provisions of this
document.
