Minutes of the 35% Meeting of the
Board of Governors

At Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura

The 35" meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala
was scheduled to be held on November 14, 2015 at 11.00 am in the Conference Hall of NIT
Agartala, Tripura. The venue of the meeting has been changed at Virtual Class Room of NIT
Agartala. Accordingly the meeting was held on November 14, 2015 at 11.00 am in the Virtual
Classs Room of NIT Agartala.

The following members were present in the meeting:

Prof. Deepak B. Phatak, Chairperson, Board of Governors, NIT Agartala, introduced Shri
Jagdish Singh, Secretary to the Government of Tripura, Dept. of Higher Education, Agartala, to
all Members of the Board of Governors and welcomed him to the meeting of Board of
Governors of NIT Agartala. *

Prof. Deepak B. Phatak, Chairperson, Board of Governors, NIT Agartala, presided over the
meeting and welcomed all the Members of the Board of Governors.

The Chairperson then invited the Director and the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda
items.

Item No. 35.1: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of 18" Senate
meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 09, 2015.

The BoG approved the minutes of the 18" Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 09,

2015.

Item No. 35.2: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of 19" Senate
meeting of NIT Agartala held on November 14, 2015.

The BoG approved the minutes of the 19" Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on Noverber 14,

2015. —

The meeting ended with the vote of thanks to the Chair.
