NIT Agartala is conducting a Computer-based Test in different centers across India, and the syllabus for
the test is as per GATE/NET/Specializations. The test will be conducted at HIT Guwahati, Cotton
University (Guwahati, Assam), NIT Durgapur, Techno India University (Kolkata, West Bengal), NIT
Delhi, NIT Trichy, NIT Patna, MANIT Bhopal, NIT Warangal, NIT Jamshedpur, IET Lucknow

1
Test Date: 22-Mar-2025
Time: 11 :00 AM (1ST)
No. of Questions: 60 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)
Max.Marks: 120
Negative Marks: I (for wrong answer only)
Duration: 90 minutes
The overseas candidates will be allowed to appear for the Proctor-based Online Test as per the above
details. They must send an email (mention all the details (Registration ID, Name, Department Name, and
Applied Post)) to registrar@nita.ac.in from the~r current Institutional Email ID (not from Gmail, Yahoo,
etc.), and attach the current Institute Identity Card, Offer Letter of the Post s/he is presently holding
abroad, and relevant Passport Page related to employment abroad. The overseas candidates must submit
all these documents during their physical reporting, if any. They must send an email by 14-Mar-2025 to
appear for the Proctor-based Online Test. Otherwise, their candidature will not be considered.
All the .shortlisted candidates (except overseas candidates), whose names are published, are advised to
visit https://hr.nita.ac.in/ChooseWrittenTestCentre/lndex. The shortlisted candidates can select the
examination centers and download the Hall Tickets. The activity can be performed between 12-Mar-2025
to 14-Mar-2025. Non-provision of center choice by 14-Mar-2025 will be assumed that the candidate is
not interested in appearing in the test and the candidature will not be considered for further processing.
It is to be noted that there are a limited number of seats at each center. Thus, center allocation will be
done on the first come first serve basis. No T AIDA will be provided to appear in the test. The Institute
reserves its right to change of venue, date, time, etc., in case of any unavoidable circumstances and the
Institute will not be liable to pay any compensation to candidates on this account.
