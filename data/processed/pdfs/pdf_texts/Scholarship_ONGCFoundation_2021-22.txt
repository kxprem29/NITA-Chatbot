ONGC Foundation offers the following
Scholarship scheme for the academic year 2021-22:

I. Scholarship Scheme for SC/ST Category Students - 1000 Nos.
ll. Scholarship Scheme for OBC Category Students - 500 Nos.
lll. Scholarship Scheme for General/EWS Category Students - 500 Nos.

Scholarship in each scheme for an amount of = 48,000/- per annum
each to meritorious SC/ST, OBC and General/EWS category students
pursuing professional courses in Engineering, MBBS and Master's
degree courses in MBA, Geology and Geophysics. 50% of
scholarships are reserved for girl students.

This scholarship is eligible only for the students who have
got admission in the academic year 2021-22

Selection of candidates will be made on the basis of aggregate marks
obtained in the qualifying examination prescribed for each specified
course of study.

The last date of receipt of the application is 06.03.2023.

For more details and application format, please refer to the ONGC
Foundation website www.ongcscholar.org
