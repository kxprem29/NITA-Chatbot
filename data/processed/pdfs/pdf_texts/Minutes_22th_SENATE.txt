Professor Gopal Mugeraya, Chairman SENATE has welcomed all the members who have
attended the 22"d meeting of SENATE of the Institute. He has informed the house that the 22nd
meeting of SENATE is a special one in connection with 9th Convocation of the Institute where apart
from 2l Ph.D scholars who have been provisionally awarded Ph.D degree and which has been
approved by the 2i't SENATE, 6 (six) other scholars have meanwhile completed their Ph.D. Award
of Ph.D degree to these scholars is required to be approved and confirmed by the SENATE to place
to the BOG for consideration. After discussion all the members have unanimously confirmed the
minutes of the 21't meeting of SENATE. The Chairman SENATE has then requested the members
to take part in the discussion on the agenda notes placed in the meeting and give their considered
views.

Secretary, SENATE has also welcomed all the members present in the meeting. He has placed all
the agenda points including awarding of the degree of Doctor of Engineering (Honoris Causa) to Sri
Tathagata Roy and the degree of Doctor of Letters (Honoris Causa) to Padma Shri Nana Gajanan
Patekar in the 9th convocation of NIT Agartala. Members present in the meeting have taken part in
the discussion. Their contribution in their respective field has also been discussed at length. After
