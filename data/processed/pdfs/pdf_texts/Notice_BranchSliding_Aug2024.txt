The undersigned is directed to inform that, 51 nos of students indicated in Annexure-I have

branches in order of preference and the availability of seats in the respective Departments, subject to
the limitation that the strength of a branch should not fall below the existing strength by more than
ten percent and should not go above the sanctioned strength by more than ten percent.

3. Based on the recommendation of committee and subsequesnt approval of the Authority, all
the allotments in respect of branch change in Annexure-I is final and binding on the applicants. No
student will be permitted, under any circumstances, to refuse the change of branch offered.

4. Examination Section should allocate the schemes to the branch slided students for their new
branch.
5. The branch slided students are instructed to use their new enrolment number and reset the

password from the home page of the MIS.

6. The students as mentioned in Annexure-1 should report to the UG coordinator of the newly
allocated department. The co-ordinator should allocate appropriate section and Faculty Advisor to
the branch slided students.

7. Students should choose subjects for the new branch and submit the same. After this they should
report for Physical Verification to the Faculty Advisor of the newly allotted branch .

8. The branch change is final and binding to all those who have been allotted a new branch. No
request for reconsideration will be entertained There will be no branch change throughout the entire
_course of study and also under any circumstances.
