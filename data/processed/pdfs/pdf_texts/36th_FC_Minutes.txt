The Finance Committee, 36 meeting of National Institute of Technology, Agartala was held
on June 24, 2020 at 11:00 am in the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma : Chairperson of Finance Committee and Director
of NIT Agartala.

(2) Mr. Madan Mohan : Addl. Director General (HE), MHRD,
Govt. of India

(4) Dr. D. Bhattacharya : Professor, Math. Dept., NIT Agartala.

(5) Dr. Govind Bhargava : Member Secretary of FC and Registrar of NIT
Agartala.

Mr. Madan Mohan and Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral attended the meeting through video
conference.

«Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson of Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala
presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors. The
Chairperson also delivered his best wishes to stay safe during the COVID-19 pandemic
situation.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 36.7: To consider freezing of Dearness Allowance to the employees &
Dearness Relief to pensioners at current rates till July 2021 and the
authority of NIT Agartala may be authorized to allow to implement
further DA order as declared by Govt. of India.

The Finance Committee approved the proposal for freezing of DA to employees and DR to

pensioners and authorized the Director of NIT Agartala to allow to implement further DA
orders as and when Govt. of India notified.
