At the very beginning Prof. Gopal Mugeraya, Director & Chairman, SENATE,
NIT Agartala welcomed all the members present in the meeting. He specially
welcomed Prof. Manoj Mitra for attending the 15ft meeting of SENATE of this
Institute in spite of his busy schedule. He informed the meeting that after the 14tr
meeting of SENATE held on 09105/2014 there was no meeting of Board of Governors
(BoG) and therefore the minutes of the 14tr meeting of the SENATE could not be got
approved. He however added that the matter was under the acticve consideration of
the Govt. of India and decision of appointment of Chairman, Board of Governors
(BoG) of this Institute would be taken shortly. He then requested all the members to
take part in the discussion.
After the opening remarks of the Chairman, SENATE, Mr. Sumanta
Chakrabarti, Secretary, SENATE requested Prof. R. P. Sharma, Dean (AA) to place
the agenda points before the house for discussion.
Prof. R.P. Sharma, Dean (AA) started discussion on all the agenda points one
by one after welcoming the members of the meeting.
