NOTICE
This is for information to all concerned that the National Institute of Technology Agartala
and the Indian Institute of Information Technology Agartala will jointly celebrate the 79th
Independence Day on 15th August 2025. AS a part of the celebration, a March Past by the
students will be one of the key events to honour the occasion.
All Undergraduate (UG) students, both boys and girls of NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala,
are intormed to report at the Sports Complex from 4th August 2025 at 5:30P M for March
Past practice.
All Gymkhana members are instructed to coordinate with the students during the practice
sessions.
Students are directed to attend the practice sessions sincerely and with full dedication.
