At the outset the Chairman welcomed all the members of the SENATE to its 18th meeting.
Leave of absence was granted to members who could not attend the meeting. The Chairman Senate
expressed his satisfaction by informing all the members that the Ninety percent (90%) seats were
filled up in the Undergraduate programs. He revealed that the Institute achieved an excellent record
on campus placement from the very beginning of the academic session 2015-16. About fifty percent
(50%) of the total 470 registered and eligible students from various departments were already
placed in the reputed organizations. The maximum number of 82 jobs were offered to the students
from IBM. He also highlighted about the inauguration of new building of Computer Science &
Engineering Department. For effective growth, development and strengthening of academics,
research and innovation in the institute, the Chairman informed that each department would have an
iconic programme where eminent academician related to that department would deliver lectures.
The Chairman SENATE apprised the members about the highlights of the decisions taken in
the meeting of previous SENATE. He informed the house that the 17th minutes of the meeting of
SENATE held on 22"d April,2015 was approved by the Board of Governors in its 34th meeting held
on24th September 2015.
