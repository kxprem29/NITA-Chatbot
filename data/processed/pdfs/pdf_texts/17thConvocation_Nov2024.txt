NOTICE

This is for information to all concerned that the 17 Convocation of the National Institute of
Technology Agartala will be held on 18 January, 2025 (Saturday) at NIT Agartala for conferring degrees
to the graduates of various programmes (UG, Dual Degree, PG and Ph.D) for A.Y 2024-25.

A-1. Online Registration

1. Online registration is mandatory for all the degree recipients for participating in the Convocation
ceremony.

2. Graduates to log on to the online registration portal -https://mis.nita.ac.in. The registration portal
will active between 02/12/2024to 02/01/2025.

3. Digi-locker ID need to be mentioned mandatorily during registration. Individual without Digi-
locker ID, need to register he/she register in Digi-locker portal first. No registration fee is required
during registration as fee is already paid.

A-2 Dress Code:

1. It is to be noted that following dress code is to be strictly maintained by the Degree recipients
on the day of Convocation, else they will not be allowed to enter in the Convocation area.

Boys: White Kurta Pajama, preferably cotton.
Girls: Off-white Salwar suit, preferably cotton.

A-3 Help Desk:
For any Technical Issues during Online Registration, please contact Mr. Pramathesh Banik
(nitmisagt@gmail.com) Phone Number : 0381-2548537, 10:00am. - 5:00pm(In Working days)

All are advised to visit Institute website regularly for further updates related to the Convocation.
