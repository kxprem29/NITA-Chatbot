Prof. (Dr.) P. K. Bose, Director & Chairman, SENATE, NIT Agartala initiated the
discussion by welcoming all the members of SENATE. He specially welcomed Prof. K.
P. Ghatak of University of Calcutta and Prof. Nilanjana Gupta of Jadavpur University. He
invited all the members present in the meeting to take part in the discussion on the issues
placed in the Agenda Note.

After the opening remarks of Chairman, SENATE, Dr. N. K. Kole, Secretary, SENATE
briefly presented the Agenda Items and Dr. R. P. Sharma, Dean, Academic Affair, then
discussed all the issues of the Agenda in details.

PART -A

Item No. S/10/01 Conferring of the Honoris Causa- Doctor of Science to
Padma Bhushan Udupi Ramachandra Rao, former
Chairman, Indian Space Research Organisation, (ISRO)
Bangalore in the 4 Convocation of NIT Agartala on 10"
February, 2012.

The SENATE approved Conferring of the Honoris Causa-
Doctor of Science to Padma Bhushan Udupi Ramachandra Rao,
former Chairman, Indian Space Research Organisation, (ISRO)
Bangalore, for his outstanding contribution in the field of
Science.

Item No. S/10/02 Consideration and approval of printing of B.Tech Certificates
in English.

The SENATE approved printing of B.Tech Certificates in
English with provision that bilingual certificates will be issued to
the candidates on demand.

Item No. S/10/03 Consideration and approval of organizing 4" Convocation in
2 (two) sessions (from 4.10 pm to 5.35 pm and from 6.15 pm
to 7.30 pm)

The SENATE approved organization of 4" Convocation in two
(2) sessions (from 4.10 pm to 5.35 pm and from 6.15 pm to 7.30
pm). In the first session Chief Guest, Special Guests of Honour,
Guest of Honour, Chairman BOG, Director and Registrar will be
on the Dias. In the second session all participants of the academic
procession will be on the Dias.

Item No. S/10/04 Consideration and approval of awarding Ph.D degree to Smt.
Sima Ghosh, a Ph.D Scholar in Civil Engineering Department
for the first time in the history of NIT, Agartala.

The SENATE approved awarding Ph.D degree in Engineering to
Smt. Sima Ghosh, a Ph.D Scholar in Civil Engineering
Department.

Item No. S/10/05 Consideration and approval of starting MBA Programme in
NIT, Agartala from the academic session 2012-13.

The SENATE approved starting of MBA Programme in NIT,
Agartala from the academic session 2012-13 with an intake
capacity of 30.

Item No. S/10/06 Consideration and approval of awarding Ph.D degree to the
institute faculty members of NIT Manipur.

The SENATE approved the MoU to be signed between NIT
Manipur and NIT Agartala for collaborative Ph.D Programme to
benefit the Faculty members of NIT Manipur.

The Meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
