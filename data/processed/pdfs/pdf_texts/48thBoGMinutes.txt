Item No 48 9 Absorption of Dr Govind Bhargava, Registrar wef 05-05-2017 and
counting of his Past Service for the purpose of Pension, Leave etc as
per CCS Rule 1972

The Board declined the proposal and made it clear that the absorption at NIT Agartala ts in
violation of NIT statutes/ Recruitment rules The Board directed to Registrar to get his
Superannuation cleared from his parent department and inform to the NIT Agartala within a
month so that his services for the remaining period could be decided till the age of 62 years or
for a total tenure of 5 years whichever ts earlier

The BoG decided that his lien in his parent Organization may be treated as extended upto
3172019 (the date of his superannuation in his parent organization) and his pension
contribution and leave salary contribution during the period of his service at NIT Agartala till
the date of his superannuation in his parent organization may be transferred to his parent
organization The BoG also decided that wef 182019 his remuneration as Registrar NIT
Agartala may be re-fixed at his last pay drawn minus pension in compliance with DoPT OM
No 3/3/ 2016-Estt (Pay 11) dated 1 05 2017 as amended from time to time Also in view of
Rule 55-A ot CCS (Pension) Rules 1972, he shall not be eligible to draw Dearness Reliet on
Pension during the period of his re-employment as Registrar, NIT Agartala During the
proceedings of this discussion, the Registrar went outside and did not participate
