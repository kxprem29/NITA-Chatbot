NOTIFICATION

 

Subject: List of short-listed candidates for Interview-cum-Interaction after the written test/ written test
and computer proficiency consisting of typing test for recruitment of Group-B and Group-C in
the various non-teaching positions.

Ref. : Advertisement vide No. F, NITA. 2 (519-Estt)/ 2019/ 7717, dt. 02-11-2019 and No. F. NITA. 2
(519-Estt)/ 2019/ Vol-II/ 3335, dt. 15-09-2021.

With reference to the subject cited above, the list of short-listed candidates for Interview-cum-Interaction after
the written test/ written test and computer proficiency test consisting of typing test for recruitment of Group-B
and Group-C in the various non-teaching positions for the direct recruitment at NIT Agartala are appended
(Annexure-I) herewith for information to all candidates. All the candidates are hereby communicated that the
letters for Interview-cum-Interaction has been sent to your registered e-mail. Candidates should satisfy the
eligibility criteria as per Recruitment Rules / terms & conditions of the advertisement / NIT Act & Statutes and
its amendment time to time.

Shortlisting Criteria for Interview-cum-Interaction:

(1) The cut off marks in written examination / written & CPT consisting of typing examination for different
categories of posts under Gr.-B and Gr.-C is declared as follows:

Cut-off marks (%)
UR (Unreserved 40
R(Reserved)

(2) From the panel of qualified candidates in written test/ written test and CPT consisting of typing test are
to be called to appear before the Selection Committee for Interview-cum-Interaction maintaining the
ratio 1:6 category wise preferably for each post. In any stage, the number of candidatesis less than for
maintaining the ratioi.e. 1:6 then all the candidates who full fill minimum cut-off marks are called for
Interview-cum-Interaction. The weightage of written test and Interview-cum-Interaction is 80:20.

    

  
  

  
   

(3) In case you fail to attend the Interview-cum-Interaction, it will be presumed that you are not interested
for the post and your candidature will be treated as ‘cancelled’. No further correspondence will be
entertained in this regard.
