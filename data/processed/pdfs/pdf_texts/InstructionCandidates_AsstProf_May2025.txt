INSTRUCTIONS TO CANDIDATES REGARDING PPT PRESENTATIONS BEFORE THE SELECTION
COMMITTEE FOR RECRUITMENT TO THE POSTS OF ASSISTANT PROFESSOR (LEVEL 10 AND
LEVEL 12).
All the Candidates appearing for personal interview before the Selection Committee for
recruitment to the posts of Assistant Professor (level 10 and Level 12) are required to delivera
five minutes presentation consisting of maximum of six slides covering the following details:
Slide 1-Personal details: Name, Current affiliation.
Slide 2-Academic credentials from Graduation onwards with name of the degree awarded,
Institute/University, Grade points/%, title of Post graduate and/or Ph.D. thesis and special
mention of Institute (Like QS/NIRF etc.).
Slide 3- Details of experience attained till date in chronological order (for Level 12 candidates)
from current to start position with important achievement w.r.t. RR for NITs and IlEST.
Slide 4- Details of publications covering publications in journals, conferences, Ph.D. supervised
as Principal/Other supervisor, Books/Chapter contributed and important academic and
administrative responsibilities. To be provided in tabular form with credit point claimed.
Slide 5- Sponsored projects handled and short term courses organized. For project include
project granting agency, amount of funding, PI/Co-Pl details etc.
slide 6- Any additional information as relevant, candidates to cover the contribution made.
(NITA faculty to provide special mention of achievement in the period served in NIT Agartala).
This can include excellence in teaching, contribution to teaching learning and academic system
in general etc.
