With the approval of the competent authority, a workshop on Happiness Strategy' will be conducted by
Prof. Rajesh K. Pillania for NIT Agartala. Participants will gain valuable insights and practical

knowledge into daily life to enhance overall well-being.
In this regard, all students, faculty members and staff of NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala are encouraged
to attend the workshop, scheduled on 24th October 2024 at 6:00 PM onwards in the Virtual Classroom
of NIT Agartala.
