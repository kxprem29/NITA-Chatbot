Minutes of the 334 Meeting of the

The 33 meeting of the Finance Committee of National Institute of Technology, Agartala
was held on June 26, 2019 at 10:30 am in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House. New
Delhi.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. H. K. Sharma : Chairperson, Finance Committee, NIT Agartala.
(2) Mrs. Darshna M. Dabral : JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India.
(3) Mr. K. Rajan : US, MHRD, Govt. of India [Representative of

Joint Secretary (dealing with NITs), MHRD,
Govt. of India].

(4) Prof. S. C. Saha : Professor, NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee =: Assoc. Professor, NIT Agartala.
(6) Dr. Govind Bhargava : Member Secretary, Finance Committee and

Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Prof. H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala presided
over the meeting. He welcomed all members of the Finance Committee.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 33.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 32" Meeting of Finance
Committee held on February 13, 2019.

The minutes of 32" Finance Committee meeting held on February 13, 2019 were confirmed
after deliberations. To avoid complications due to earlier orders/ decisions of the Institute
with regard to payment of sitting fee Rs. 5000.00 for attending meetings or other official
affairs, the decision taken vide Agenda Item No. 32.16 of 32™ Finance Committee will
suppress all the earlier decisions.

Item No. 33.2: Action taken on the decision of 31" Finance Committee meeting
held on August 09, 2018.

The Finance Committee noted the action taken report. FC was apprised about the Legal
opinion with regard to action taken on TDS liabilities vide agenda 31.12, in which Legal
Counsel advised that it will not be wise to go for litigation because of deposition of money to
Central Government only.

Item No. 33.3: To consider the Annual Budget proposal of the Institute for the
Financial Year 2019-20.

The Finance Committee accepted and confirmed the Annual Budget of the Institute for the
Financial Year 2019-20.

Item No. 33.4: To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as
on June 15, 2019,

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute.
Item No. 33.5: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.
The Finance Committee noted the status of TEQIP-III project.

Item No. 33.6: To consider the Physical & Financial Statement of the ongoing
construction works/ projects.

The Finance Committee noted the physical & financial statement of the ongoing construction
works/ projects.

Item No. 33.7: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of 41° Building &
Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee accepted the recommendations of the minutes of 41° Building &
Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

Item No. 33.8: To consider the Medical Attendance Guidelines for NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee suggested to follow the CS (MA) guidelines for medical benefit to
the regular employees of NIT Agartala. The Finance Committee also approved the list of the
referral hospitals, approved by the Govt. of Tripura vide No. F. | (9)-Secy/ HFW/ 13 (1), dt.
08-06-2016 (Annexure — A).

Item No. 33.9: To consider the adoption of Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
for the funds received from individuals/ Private Bodies/ Non-
Government Organizations for giving Memorial Awards/
Fellowships.

The Finance Committee approved to adopt the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP).

However, the Finance Committee suggested to open a Current Account with Collect Facility.

so that the donor may donate directly in this account.

Item No. 33.10: To consider the Stipend for Rs. 5000.00 may be given to Mr. Amit
Debnath, Research Scholar of CSE Department as order passed by
the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura vide WA 49 of 2018.

The Finance Committee ratified the decision and suggested to issue an Advisory Memo to the

members/ chairman of the Doctoral Committee.

Item No. 33.11: To consider user charges of the students to meet the minimum
expenses of the facilities at NIT Agartala w.e.f. 2019-20 Academic
year.

The Finance Committee noted the information.

Item No. 33.12: National Pension System (NPS), the monthly contribution of
employer @ 14% of the Basic Pay plus DA w.e.f. 01-04-2019.

The Finance Committee approved to adopt contribution of NPS from 10% to 14% in respect
of Employer's share in line with the Office Memorandum of Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India
vide F. No. 13-4/ 2019-TC, dated: 16-04-2019.

Item No. 33.13: To consider consolidated fees for conference and hearing of Sr.
Advocate for defending NIT Agartala at Hon’ble Supreme Court of
India.

The Finance Committee ratified the decision.

Item No. 33.14: To consider the work of “Special Repair, Retrofitting and
Upgradation of Library Building at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs.
71,55,558.00.

Due to earth quakes, creaks have been developed in different parts of the Building and the
structure is lying idle without any use. Therefore. to make use of the structure in the interest
of students and to prevent it from further deterioration. Special Repair, Retrofitting and
Upgradation of Library Building is essential and is to be taken up immediately.

The Finance Committee therefore approved the estimated cost of Rs. 71.55,558.00 (Rupees
Seventy One Lakh Fifty Five Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty Eight) for the works and
recommended to place it to the Board.

Item No, 33.15: To consider the work of “Special Repair, Retrofitting and
Upgradation of Dhalai Hostel building at NIT Agartala” for Rs.
98,94,302.00.

This hostel building is old and therefore gradual deterioration has been taken place in the
building with respect to time. To keep this building functional for the use of students, special
repair, retrofitting and up-gradation is urgently needed.

The Finance Committee approved the estimated cost of Rs. 98.94,302.00 (Rupees Ninety
Eight Lakh Ninety Four Thousand Three Hundred and Two) for the works and recommended
to place it to the Board.

Item No. 33.16: To consider the work of “Upgradation of 2-storey building besides
Knowledge Centre at NIT Agartala” for Rs. 60,77,405.00.

A built up and incomplete Structure is lying idle adjacent to the Knowledge Centre (library
building) since last few years. A committee was made to look at the efficacy of the structure
and suggested its use. The committee recommended to make use of the existing incomplete
