NOTICE FOR IMPROVEMENT EXAMINATION 2024

This is to inform all the students of the Institute that the registration of
improvement examination for subjects of UG, Dual & PG will be from 23.05.2024 to
27.05.2024. The students need to pay Rs. 500/- (Rupees five hundred only) per subject in
the Institute account (A/c. No. 30369892838) online through MIS. Student need to
register the subject in MIS for the said examination. The examination will be conducted
by the respective departments.

Eligibility for appearing in the Improvement Examination:

1. A student who obtains a CGPA less the 6.5 with grade “P” in some subjects may be
permitted to appear at the examination for improvement in 3 (three) subject(s) to
the maximum in which the student has got “P” grade.

2. The students will be allowed to appear for improvement examination in the subjects
for the current year only.

3. A student who have “F” or “FA” grade in any subject(s) of the current year will not
be allowed to appear in the improvement examination.
