NOTICE FOR SUPPLYMENTARY & IMPROVEMENT EXAMINATION 2024 (Only for BSc. BEd.

 

This is to inform all the students of BSc. BEd. that the registration of Supplementary
Examination 2024 and Improvement Examination 2024 will be from 06.11.2024 to 14.11.2024,
The students need to pay Rs, 500/- (Rupees five hundred only) per subject in the Institute
account online through MIS. Student need to register the subject in MIS for the said
examination. The examination will be conducted by the respective departments within 05th — O6th
December 2024.

Eligibility for appearing in the Improvement Examination:

1. A student who obtains a CGPA less the 6.5 with grade “P” in some subjects may be permitted to
appear at the examination for improvement in 3 (three) subject(s) to the maximum in which the
student has got “P” grade.

2. The students will be allowed to appear for improvement examination in the subjects for the current
year only.

3. A student who have “F” or “FA” grade in any subject(s) of the current year will not be allowed to
appear in the improvement examination,
