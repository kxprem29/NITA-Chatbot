INSTITUTE PLACEMENT POLICY
A student can avail a maximum of three Job offers from the campus, the details are as follows:
1. NORMAL TIER: Registered and eligible interested students can participate in the placement
drive under the Normal Tier. (COMPULSORY or OPTIONAL t0 be decided by the CCD)
2. STANDARD TIER: All registered eligible students are required to participate in the plucement
drive under the Standard Tier. (COMPULSORY o r OPTIONAL to be decided by the CCD)
3. DREAM TIER: Registered eligible students interested in the job can participate in the
placement drive under the Dream Tier. (OPTIONAL)
Any company that will offer an internship followed by a Performance-Based Offer will be
allocated under the STANDARD TIER, But the drive will be optional for the eligible students.
Any students selected for an internship, followed by performance-based PPO /FTE, will be
allowed to participate in DREAM AND NORMAL TIER placement drives.
The attocation of tiers fNORMAL, STANDARD, &D REAM) will be decided by the CCD, NIT
Agartala. based on factors such as the institute's industry relationships, prospects, company
prospects, institutional prospects, and other parameters.
All PSUs will be considered under the DREAM Tier.
If a student is placed in the DREAM Tier, they will not be allowed to participate in any other
campus placement drives.
If a student is placed in the STANDARD Tier, they may not be allowed to participate in the
NORMAL Tier campus placement drive further. However, they will be allowed to participate in
the DREAM Tier campus placement drive.
If a student is placed in the NORMAL Tier, they may be allowed to participate in both the
STANDARD Tier and DREAM Tier campus placement drives.
If as tudent is placed in any DREAM TIER company, they will not be eligible for any other
Internship/ Intern FTE/I ntern followed by PPO through on-campus and off-campus.
If a student is placed in any DREAM TIER company, other tier offers (normal and or standard)
will be rendered null and void.
If as tudent registers for a company's placement or internship process but fails to participate in any
stage of the selection process (e.g., PPT, test, GD, or interView), they will be considered PLACED
in the respective tier of that drive.

INSTITUTE PLACEMENT RULES AND REGULATIONS
A. ELIGIBILITY & REGISTRATION:
1 All the final year student interested in campus placement (0n campus and off campus) should
register for campus placements with the Centre for Career Development (CCD). Placement
Registration is for the final placement session only.
2. Registration for placement will be done during the month of June.
3. Campus placement is a facility provided for the students. Students who are not interested in
placement are advised not to register for placement. Students who do not register for placement
have to compulsorily give a declaration for not participating in the placement drives while
completing the placement registration proces. Students who have not registered for placement
will not be allowed to participate in any of the placement processes (even in Public Sector Units)
that occur through CCD, NIT Agartala.
4. Students have to fill up a Google form to register for the placement session. The student who does
not register will not be enlisted in the students available for placement. It is the responsibility of
the student coordinators of the respective department to complete the registration process in time
as decided by the CCD.
5. In the Google form, there should be a declaration that "All the information provided in the Google
form is true to my knowledge and any violation in the information provided will be punishable by
the authority" and an acceptance of the terms and conditions.
B. TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

registering for placements with a declaration as follows:
