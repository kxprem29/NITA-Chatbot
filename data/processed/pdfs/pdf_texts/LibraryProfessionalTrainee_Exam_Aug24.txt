General Instructions

I. Provisionally eligible candidates are required to appear for the written test/interview to be conducted on
September 20, 2024.

2. Candidates need to report sharp at 9.30 am at Central Library, National Institute of Technology Agartala.

3. Bring filled in Application Form (3 copies) along with all the original and self-attested photocopy of the
certificates and mark sheets.

4. Carry a blue / black ball point pen for the test.

5. You need to bring the copy of this email for the test

6. Please confirm your participation for the written test by replying to this mail by September 10, 2024.

Eligibility criteria —

|. First class (60%) in MLIS.

2. Completed MLIS in 2024 or 2023.

3. Age below 27 years as on 25.08.2024.

Candidates may send their representations/grievances, related to eligibility and not eligibility, if any within 08.09.2024

positively in the email id nitacentrallibrary@ogmail.com.

You must be prepared to stay one more day after the scheduled date of interview,

*Note-

1. Applications that do not meet the eligibility criteria given in this advertisement and/or are incomplete in any respect
shall be summarily rejected.

2. In case the applicant gets screened/shortlisted/selected on the basis of the credentials furnished by him/her which
are, on scrutiny, found to be incorrect/inadmissible /forged /fabricated/falsified, his/her candidature shall be liable to
be cancelled at any stage/at any time during the tenure of the trainee,
