NOTICE

All the B. Tech students of the Institute belonging to Gen/EWS/OBC(NCL) categories and want to avail the

benefit of tuition fee concession based on the notification issued by the Ministry of Human Resource and Development
(MHRD (now MOE)), Govt. of India, vide order no. F .No.33 - 4 / 2014- TS.III, dated: 24" June, 2016, are hereby instructed
to upload the following documents from 01-06-2022 to 15-06-2022:

1.

A valid “Family Income Certificate (in Hindi/English) from all sources” issued on or after 1st April, 2023
strictly by the Competent Authority of the District of students’ residence, as per Annexure-A, attached

with the notice. (A format of income certificate is also attached with the notice, in case the issuing

authority desires one).

 

 

 

2. An Affidavit in the format attached with the notice is to be submitted by the student along with his/her
parents.

3. The student should write their Name, Roll no, and sign the documents mentioned in sl, no. 1.

4. After signing, the documents of sl. 1 and 2 should be converted into a single pdf file (original color
scanned only) and upload in the MIS as per guidelines given in point 5.

ai

Step 1 Log in through the link https://mis.nita.ac.in.
Step 2 After log in, use the path Academic >>> Admission >>>Upload Document

6.

7. In connection with the above, any students belonging to the aforementioned categories, if fails to upload the
valid Family Income Certificate from 01-06-2023 to 15-06-2023, he/she will be ineligible for getting the
benefit of the tuition fee remission for the ODD semester of the academic session 2023-24, and shall pay full
tuition fee.

Note:
i. For SC, ST & PwD students, no income Certificate is required to claim tuition fee waiver.
ii. Students are also instructed to retain the original copy of the Family Income Certificate and affidavit &
submit the original to the Academic Section when the college reopens.
