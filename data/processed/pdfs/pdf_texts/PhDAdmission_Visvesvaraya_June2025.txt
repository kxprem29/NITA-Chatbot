Applications are invited from the eligible candidates for admission in the Ph.D
Programme under Visvesvaraya Ph.D scheme for Electronics and IT: Phase II of the
Ministry of Communication and IT for July-December 2025 academic session in the
Electronics System Design and Manufacturing (ESDM) and IT / IT Enabled Services
(ITES) at NIT Agartala.

Minimum Qualification for admission in Ph.D programme under
Visvesvaraya Ph.D scheme:-

Candidates for admission in Ph.D programme under Visvesvaraya Ph.D
scheme must satisfy any of the following criteria:-

I. Master’s Degree in Engineering/ Technology in Electronics & Communication
Engineering /Electrical Engineering/ Electronics & Instrumentation
Engineering/Computer science and Engineering/ Mechanical Engineering/ broad
area of specialization in ESDM and ITES or equivalent with a minimum CGPA of
6.5 out of 10 or equivalent (60% of marks). GATE is mandatory for admission
in full-time Ph.D program under this scheme.

II. Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering/ Technology in Electronics & Communication
Engineering / Mechanical Engineering/ broad area of specialization in ESDM and
ITES or equivalent, with an excellent academic record as 70% or 7.5 CGPA ina 10
point scale from an Institute/ University with average NIRF ranking of within 100
(Engineering category) over last 3 years, and Institutes of National Importance.
Candidates must have valid GATE score.

*** Conversion of CGPA to percentage and vice-versa will not be allowed.

Selection Procedure:-

The candidates fulfilling the eligibility conditions will be short-listed and the list will
be published in the institute website. Selection will be made on the basis of academic
creditiantials, publication, performance of the candidates in the interview and/ or written test
and availability of seats. The short-listed candidates should appear for interview and/ or

written test.

Scholarship Facility:-

The candidates who will be selected for admission under the scheme in Ph.D
programme as full-time scholars will be entitled for scholarship @ Rs. 38,750/- per month in
1% & 2"4 year and @ Rs. 43,750/- per month in 3 to 5" year as per applicable norms of the

scheme.

Note for Part-time Ph.D seats:

1. Part-time Ph.D seats’ is provisioned for professionals working at a reputed place/
organization.

2. The selected candidates should not be availing any Ph.D Fellowship/ scholarship from
any other scheme of Central or State Government.

3. Part-time candidates who do not avail of any Ph.D Fellowship/scholarship/stipend
from any Central or State Government shall be provided a one-time incentive of Rs. 3
Lakh on successful completion of the Ph.D degree.

4. NOC (in prescribed format) from the employer is mandatory for part-time candidates.
