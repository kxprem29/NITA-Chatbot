CIRCULAR
Sub: Entry/Updation of Family details in MIS Portal
It is hereby informed that all the regular Faculty and Non-faculty employees of
NIT Agartala are required to update their family details in MIS Portal of NITA for
availing smooth treatment facilities including referrals at ILS hospital. The
updation should be completed within 15 days from the date of issue of this
circular.
It is to note that the data so filled by the Institute employees should be strictly as
per details of family submitted by them in their service record.
A11 due precaution should be exercised in updating the said details on MIS as the
data so captured may be used for extending other services benefits after due
verification of the same from the respective service records by Establishment
Section.
This is issued with the approval of the competent Authority
