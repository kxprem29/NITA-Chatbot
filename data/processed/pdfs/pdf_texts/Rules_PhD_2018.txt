General
The National Institute of Technology, Agartala,(NITA) awards the degree of Doctor of
Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Engineering/ Science/ Humanities and Social Sciences/ Management to a
candidate who successfully completes the stipulated Program of Research.
The Program of Research and the governing regulations are formulated by the Senate and
duly approved by the Board of Governors of the Institute. The Board can modify or change the
structure, the governing regulations etc. from time to time.
A candidate to be awarded Ph.D. degree has to submit a thesis embodying the findings of
his/her research work carried out in this program. The thesis should make an original
contribution of high quality to the advancement of knowledge as judged by the experts in the
relevant area.
A candidate becomes eligible for the award of the Ph.D. degiee after fulfilling all the
academic requirements prescribed by the SENATE of the Institute.
The award of the Ph.D. degree shall be made upon the recommendation of the SENATE
of the Institute and after approval by the Board of Governors of the Institute.
