Prof. Gopal Mugeraya, Director & Chairman, SENATE, NIT Agartala inititated the
discussion by welcoming all the members present in the meeting. He expressed his
satisfaction by informing the members that the number of Ph.D recipients from this Institutes
was increasing gradually and in the 8ft convocation of the institute it would be possible to
award Ph.D degree to more than 10 scholars. He called upon the Heads of the Departments to
encourage good candidates to apply for the faculty posts for which advertisement was made
recently so that it could go a long way in improving academic activities of the institute. He
requested the faculty members who would be going outside the country to various
Universities under CPDA programme to spend one or more days in the University and
interact with one or two professors on the academic improvement of this Institute. They
might also be invited to visit our Institute.
After the opening remarks of the Chairman, SENATE, Mr. Sumanta Chakrabarti,
Secretary, SENATE conveyed good wishes to all for the Bengali New Year and welcomed all
the members of the meeting. He requested Prof. R. P. Sharma, Dean (A) to place the agenda
points before the house for discussion.
Prof. R.P. Sharma, Dean (A) started discussion on all the agenda points one by one
after welcoming the members of the meeting.
