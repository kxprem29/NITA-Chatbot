NOTICE FOR ENGAGEMENT ON CONTRACT BASIS

 

Applications are invited for engagement on contractual basis for the post of Facility Manager (contractual).

1.

Name of the Post : Facility; Manager (contractual),

Upto 62 years as on 31-12-2024.

Monthly Rs.50,000/- per month (consolidated). ( Remuneration will not be a constraint for

Remuneration deserving candidate). For official tour/visit, TA/DA will be admissible as per rule.

Job Profile a) Management/Supervision of maintenance, gardening, housekeeping team in
the Institute to required standard,
obtaining maximum productivity, efficiency, quality service and observe all
aspects of compliance and quality standard by adhering to budget and
required criteria.

b) Supervise day to day maintenance of all the aspects of the estate including

maintenance of building, tree cutting, horticulture, housekeeping, waste

management etc.

Supervise utility staff and empanelled vendors.

      

   
  
 

   

       
     
   
    
    
   
   
  
 

  

Contract Tenure

  

The contract will be Initially for a period of one year and to be considered for further
extension up to 3 terms subject to requirement of the institute and satisfactory work
performance.

The original terms & renewal together is limited to 3 years.

 
 
    

Essential Qualification : Must be a Graduate from a recognized University in any discipline.

Essential Experience: The applicant should have relevant experience as referred against job profile. Proof of
experience must be enclosed with application form.

The applicant having experience in educational institute will be preferred. However, the same can be relaxed for

deserving candidates. Retired persons from Govt. Depts./ Autonomous bodies/PSU/Central/State University having
required experience in the given job profile will be preferred.

No. of Post : 01 (one)
Eligible candidates, interested to appear in interview should send their application addressed to the Registrar, NIT

Agartala mentioning (1) Post applied for, (2)Name (in Block letters), (3) Father’s /Husband’s Name (4) Present &
Permanent address (5) Date of birth and Age (as on 31-12-2024) (6) Email address, Mobile No. (7) Whether
SC/ST/OBC/PH/Ex-S, (8) Educational qualification (9) Experience (if any) (10) Category (11) Other Information (if
any) (12) whether all the terms & condition of the advt. is agreed or not (YES/NO) along with self attested
photocopy of all certificates in a single PDF for proof of education/ experience /age etc latest by 25-02-2025 trough
email at establishment@nita.ac.in

Shortlisted candidates will be informed by return email indicating tentative date & time to attend the interview.

The shortlisted candidates are required to bring copy of the filled-in Application form, recent passport size
photograph, all original documents along with a set of self-attested copies of the documents at the time of
interview for verification.

For updates (if any) please visit the institute website http://www. nita.ac.in

TERMS & CONDITIONS:

The appointment against the above mentioned position shall be purely on contract basis on a fixed pay initially for
a period one year and to be considered for further extension upto 3 terms subject to requirement of the institute
and satisfactory work performance. The original terms & renewal together is limited to 3 years.
