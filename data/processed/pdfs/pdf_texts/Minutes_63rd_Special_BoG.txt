Item No. 63(A).1: Incorporation of Section 8 company at NIT Agartala for opening of
Atal Incubation Centre (AIC).

The Board approved the proposal for incorporation of Section 8 Company by the name of “AIC

~NITA” at NIT Agartala for establishing the Atal Innovation Mission (AIC) as per guidelines of

the Atal Innovation Mission issued by sponsoring Agency i.e. NITI Aayog.

Item No. 63(A).2: Incorporate CA firm for the process of establishment/ SPV.

The Board approved to hire a CA firm following due procedures as per GFR-2017.
