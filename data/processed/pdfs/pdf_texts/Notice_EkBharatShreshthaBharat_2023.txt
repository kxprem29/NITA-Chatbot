NOTIFICATION

This is inform All the native Students (UG, PG & PhD, Aged-18 to 30 years) of
Tripura that Yuva Sangam (aT) under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB)
is an initiative by Government of India to strengthen people to people connect
especially between youth belonging to North Eastern India and other States.Ek
Bharat Shreshtha Bharat Yuva Sangam (YdRVTA) will focus on conducting
exposure tours of youth between the 8 North Eastern States and other States. It
will provide an immersive experience of various facets of life, development
landmarks, recent achievements and youth connect in the host state. During
their visits, the youth will have a multi-dimensional exposure under five broad
areas —Paryatan (Tourism), Parampara (Traditions), Pragati (Development),
Prodyogik (Technology) and ParasparSampark (People-to-people connect). In this
connection NIT Agartala (Tripura) paired with IIT Madras (Tamil Nadu),
interested native students from Tripura State may apply for this event.
