GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
MINISTRY OF TRIBAL AFFAIRS
[Scholarship Section]
National Fellowship for Higher Education of ST Students for the year 2023-24.
The Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India invites online applications for
National Fellowship under the scheme “National Fellowship & Scholarship for Higher
Education of ST Students” for the year 2023-24. The fellowship is awarded to candidates
selected for pursuing Ph.D/integrated M.Phil+Ph.D programmes.
Number of slots: 750
Interested candidates may apply online through
https://fellowship.tribal.gov.in/
The last date of submitting applications online is: 31.08.2023. For more
details, candidates may visit Ministry’s portal: - https://fellowship.tribal.gov.in
