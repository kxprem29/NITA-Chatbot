Minutes of the 35** Meeting of the
Finance Committee
held on January 21, 2020

 

The Finance Committee, 35" meeting of National Institute of Technology, Agartala was held
on January 21, 2020 at 11:00 am in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, New Delhi.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. H. K. Sharma : Chairperson, Finance Committee, NIT Agartala.
(2) Shri D. K. Singh : Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD.
(Representative of JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt.
of India)
(3) Shri Indrajit Kuri : Under Secretary, MHRD.

(Representative of Joint Secretary (NITs),
MHRD, Govt. of India)

(4) Prof. S.C. Saha : Professor, NIT Agartala.

(5) Dr. Govind Bhargava : Member Secretary, Finance Committee and
Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee, Associate Professor, NIT Agartala could not attend the
meeting.

Prof. H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala presided
over the meeting. He welcomed all the members of the Finance Committee.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 35.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 34" Meeting of Finance
Committee held on October 03, 2019.

The minutes of 34" Finance Committee meeting held on October 03, 2019 were confirmed

after deliberation. The decision of 48" BoG meeting with regard to re-employment in the post
of Registrar of Dr. Govind Bhargava is to be incorporated.

Item No. 35.2: Action taken on the decision of 33" Finance Committee meeting
held on June 26, 2019.

The Finance Committee noted the action taken.

Item No. 35.3: To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as
on January 09, 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute.
Item No. 35.4: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.
The Finance committee noted the present status under the TEQIP — III project.

Item No. 35.5: To consider the Physical & Financial Statement of the ongoing
construction works/ projects.

The Finance Committee noted the Physical & Financial statement for ongoing construction
works/ projects of the Institute.

Item No. 35.6: To consider the Separate Audit Report on the accounts of the
Institute for the financial year 2018-19.

The Finance Committee approved the Separate Audit Report for the year 2018-19. However,
the committee was of the opinion, that the reply submitted to CAG along with the
observation, are to be henceforth brought to the notice of FC.

Item No. 35.7: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of 43" Building
& Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted Item No. 43.1 to 43.14, 43.17 and 43.19 to 43.23 of 43%
Building & Works Committee. The recommendations of 43" Building & Works Committee
meeting held on June 21, 2019 with regard to Item No. 43.15, 43.16, 43.18 and 43.24.1 were
accepted and recommended to place it before the BoG.

Item No. 35.8: Adoption of the guidelines for settlement of claims for
compensation as enunciated in various judgments of Hon’ble
Supreme Court in case of death/ permanent incapacitation due to
unintended/ unforessen occurrences during maintenance,
operation and provisioning of public services.

The guidelines of DoT vide Memorandum No. 36-11/ 2015-SR, dated: 5" December, 2016
and Institute committee are approved. However, point no. 2 of the institute’s committee
report may be read as, the guidelines will be applicable for all employees who are not
covered under Group Insurance. The superannuated employees from any sector will not be
covered under this scheme.

Item No. 35.9: To consider GPF balance in respect of Dr. Vidyut Dey, Assistant
Professor, Production Engineering Department.

The Finance Committee noted the proposal and suggested to recover the interest from Dr.
Vidyut Dey during the period of withholding the amount/ fund.
