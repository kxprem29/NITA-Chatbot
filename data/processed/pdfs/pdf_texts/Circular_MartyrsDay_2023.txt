CIRCULAR
Subject: Observance of silence for two minutes from 11.00 AM to 11.02 AM on 30th January,
2023 (Martyrs 'day) in the memory of those who sacrificed their lives during struggle tor
India's freedom.
In pursuance of Order no. F. No. 29-212019-S&S, dated: 16th January, 2023 issued by Govt.of
India, MoE, Department of Higher Education New Delhi, National Institute of Technology
Agartala will commemorate by observing silence for two minutes from 11.00 AM to 11.02 AM
on 30th January, 2023 in as a mark of respect of those who sacrificed their lives during
struggle for India's freedom. All departments Heads are requested to ensure the same in their
respective departments at a suitable location. The Staff of admin block will assemble at
knowledge park.
This is issues with the approval of the Competent Authority.
