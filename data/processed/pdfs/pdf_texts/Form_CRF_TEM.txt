* Use of any kind of Food stuff or Shoes are not allowed inside the working area of the lab.
* Sample will be done on first cum first serve basis as per the slot provided.
* Carry your own Carbon coated grid (mesh size 300 ~ 400) for sample analysis
* Magnetic, Conducting, Corrosive or any kind of unsuitable sample will not be accepted.

circumstances.
