NOTICE
As per letter received from North Eastern Handicrafts and Handlooms Development
Corporation Limited (NEHHDC) Limited, (A Government of India Enterprise) dated
14.08.2023, it is notified that the Purbashree - the brand promoted by NEHHDC for
encouraging local crafts in handicrafts and handlooms in the different roots of North East
Region, will park their Mobile Retail Shop: Purbashree on Wheels' at NIT Agartala
near Food Court opposite side of Auditorium, NIT Agartala on 24" Aug, 2023 from
11 a.m. to 5 p.m. to honor their noble cause of marketing the products of artisans and
weavers of North Eastern States and for further empowering the artisans and Weavers of
the North Eastern region.
This is issued as per instruction of the authority.
