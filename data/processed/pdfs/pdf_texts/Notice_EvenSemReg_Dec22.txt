Sub: - Registration for students of intermediate years in UG, Dual Degree, PG & Ph. D programmes (except

1st year UG & Dual Degree) for Even Semester (Jan - Jun 2023).

1.

The students of intermediate semesters of UG, Dual Degree, PG & Ph. D programmes are hereby informed
to pay their semester fee in full to make them eligible for subject registration during Jan - Jun 2023
semester. Students/Scholars are instructed to pay their semester fees based on the Type mentioned in
Table-1 and then register themselves in MIS..

The fees for the programs, semesters and type are mentioned in Table 2 & 3, are applicable from
26/12/2022 to 04/01/2023 Beyond this period fine will be applicable as per Institute Rule.

The semester fees have to paid mandatorily using the online payment portal at https://mis.nita.ac.in. The
link will be available from 26/12/2022. The students are advised to use the Username and Password of

their Institute MIS account (Online Payment SBI E-PAY>Pay Tuition Fee).

Students, after completing the formality indicated in Sl. No. 1, will be allowed to register the subjects
provisionally through the same online portal (Academic> Registration> Semester Registration). All
required subjects are to be selected by each student before clicking on submit button to complete their
provisional registration and print 3 (three) sets of provisional registration slip. All three registration slips
are to be signed physically by the respective students and concerned faculty advisor/supervisors of the
students/scholars and to be submitted to UG/PG co-ordinators of the respective Departments. One signed
copy of the registration slip is to be handed over to concerned student later on..

There will be no subject registration under any circumstances after 4" January, 2023, except special
cases considered by the Authority.

Students availing loan through bank/ other sources are instructed to update the details of their loan
including the transaction details and upload relevant supporting documents in their MIS account
(Academic Transaction Bank Loan Details).

Regarding hostel related matters, students may contact the Office of the Chief Warden.

As per Academic calendar circulated earlier, classes will be commencing on 02/01/2023(Monday) and a
student should have at least 75% attendance to appear Mid-Term and End-Term Examination in any
subjects/labs.
