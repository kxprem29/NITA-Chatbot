qualifying exam, minimum marks in the qualifying exam, relaxation thereof,
reservation, admission procedure, etc before applying for the NCET 2024, as
Universities have their own specific requirements for admission to specified
courses (discipline/stage). NTA will not entertain the corrections sent by the
