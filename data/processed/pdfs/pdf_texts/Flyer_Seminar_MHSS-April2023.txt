Digital Humanities is an interdisciplinary and
emerging field of studies that combines aspects
of digital technologies/computing and
humanities/culture. Prof. Aleksandar Petrovic is
a leading scholar, working on the intersection of
technology and humanities, and he will be
delivering the keynote address in this seminar. A
few papers will be presented that will explore the
central theme of the seminar from different
perspectives.
