The Chairman of the SENATE, NIT Agartala, at the outset informed the SENATE
that the tenure of the Chairman of the Board of Governors, NIT Agartala was over
on May 09,2014 and no further communication was received from the MHRD
regarding selection of the new Chairman or continuation of the Chairman, Prof. P.N.
Singh till final nomination of the new Chairman. However, it is confirmed by
MHRD that the communication regbrding appointment of the new Chairman may be
sent by MHRD atany point of time today.
,
As per provision of Section 16(l) of the NIT Act 2007 -"The Chairman shall
ordinarily preside at the meeting of the Board and at the Convocation of the
Institute".
The 7tr Convocation ofNIT Agartalais scheduled to be held on November 08, 2014.
The First Statutes of NIT 2009 vide Section 17(15) states that "In the event of the
occurrence of any vacancy in the office of the Chairman by reason of his death,
resignation or otherwise or in the event of the Chairman being unable to discharge
his functions owing to absence, illness or any other cause, the Director may
discharge the functions assigned to the Chairman under section 16 of the Act".
After detailed discussions, the SENATE authorized the Director, NIT Agartala, who
is discharging the functions of the Chairman as per provision of the NIT Act 2007
and First Statute of NIT 2009 due to vacancy in the post of Chairman, to preside
over the Convocation, confer Degrces to the degree recipients, sign the Degree
Certificates both as the Director and the Chairman, BoG (in absence of Chairman)
and perform all other duties assigned to the Chairman of BoG, NIT Agartala as per
Act and Statute of NIT.
