Professor H. K. Sharma, Chairman SENATE welcomed all the members who
attended the 28th meeting of SENATE of the Institute. He then requested all the
members to take part in the discussion on the limited agenda points of the meeting.
After this the Chairman SENATE requested Dean (Academic) to place all the agenda
points one by one for open discussion.
The Dean (Academic) then welcomed all the members of the SENATE who were
present in the meeting and requested to come forward with their valuable suggestions on
the agenda points. After that he placed all the 06 agenda points one by one. Members
present in the meeting took part in the discussion and gave their considered views. After
detailed discussion the following decisions were adopted:
Item No. 5/28/01 Confirmation of the minutes of 27th meeting of SENATE held on 26th
September 2018.
The SENATE approved and confirmed the minutes of 27th meeting of
SENATE held on 26th September 2018 after deliberations.

alumnus and an entrepreneur in the 1lth convocation of NIT Agartala.

Governor of Tripura in the 1lth Convocation of this Institute.
The SENATE also approved and confirmed the proposal to award the degree
of Doctor of Engineering (Honoris Causa) to Sri Smaran Choudhury an
alumnus and an entrepreneur in the 1lft Convocation of this Institute.
