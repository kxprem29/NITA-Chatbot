NOTICE

It has been observed that due to technical reasons of the SBI ePay payment gateway, a large
number of students have failed to pay tuition fees and thereby could not carry out physical registration.
Thus, the last date for paying semester registration fees as mentioned in notice No. NITA.5/(22-
Acad) /Registration Fee (UG, PG, Ph.D)/2024-25 (Vol. IX)/A-1698, dated 04-07-2024 is being extended to
28/07/2024 at 5:00 PM.

Physical registration should be completed by 5.00 PM on 29% July 2024 without fail.

If Physical registration is not completed by the above-mentioned date and time, fines will be levied
from 30% July 2024 as per notice even no. dated 04-07-2024
All Classes will commence on 19/07/2024 (Friday). Students must maintain at least 75%

attendance to be eligible for Mid-Term and End-Term Examinations in any subjects/labs from 19-07-
2024, or else they will be debarred from those subjects.

Attendance will be monitored through an online MIS system.
All other information provided in the above-mentioned notice remains unchanged.

This notice is issued with the approval of the Authority.
