Item No. 51.4: .Service matter of Shri Sumanta Chakraborty, Ex-Dy. Registrar in
connection with case filed by him vide case number W.P. (C) 1298/
2017, pending before the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura.

Shri Sumanta Chakrabarti joined NIT Agartala on 12.08.2011 as Deputy Registrar vide
appointment letter no. F.NITA.2(166-Estt)/11/2478-82, dated 21" July 2011 from Tripura State
Pollution Control Board (A Govt. of Tripura Organization). As per terms and conditions of
appointment letter vide clause no.11 (Retirement Benefits) states that “the Institute has the New
Pension Scheme (NPS), Govt. of India for the employees joining after 01.04.2004. For in service

~~ "candidates appointment under the Institute will be duly supported with pay protection and will

be governed by Rules as defined in the FRSR”, which was accepted by Shri. Chakrabarti while
joining at NIT Agartala. The LPC (Last Pay Certificate) dated 11.08.2011 submitted to NITA by
Tripura State Pollution Control Board (TSPCB) (his previous organization), indicated that he
was subscribed to EPF vide EPF Account No. AS/TR/2205/10 which was being maintained by
the Commissioner, Employee Provident Fund Organisation, Bholagiri, Agartala, Tripura (Copy
enclosed). But somehow, his service condition was changed to GPF, which violates the
rules/provisions of NITSER Act & Statutes and Govt. of India and also the terms and conditions
of above mentioned appointment letter.

Based on the facts, Board decided that

(a) Shri Sumanta Chakraorti, who joined NIT Agartala on 12-08-2011 can not be granted the
benefit of GPF as per the Clause 29 of NIT Statutes and appointment letter because he
did not have GPF in his previous service at the time of joining at NIT Agartala.
~(b) Board decided to keep the actions on hold till the verdict of Hon’ble High Court of
Tripura (W.P (c) 1298/ 2017).

The above decisions are taken subject to the verdict of the Writ Petition (C) 1298/2017 filed in
the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura.
