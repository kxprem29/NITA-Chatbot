NOTICE

1) Ph.D research scholars, who took admission in the academic session January-June, are
instructed to apply for renewal of yearly scholarship.

2) Every Scholar should fill up the scholarship from online through the stipend module link to
their respective account in the MIS at https://mis.nita.ac.in/mis and should submit a hard
copy of the online application. The scholar should also submit a progress report which should
be printed after generating online through Institute MIS by the a/c of his/her supervisor. The
documents should be submitted latest by 17/01/2025.

gradation along with the yearly scholarship renewal application, progress report which should
be printed after generating online through Institute MIS by the a/c of his/her supervisor and the
assessment report issued by the Research Promotional Committee (RPC) of their
respective departments.

4) Scholars are instructed to apply for scholarship form in the MIS only after completing their

semester registration.
