NOTIFICATION

This is to inform all the 2 & 3" year students of NIT Agartala that, the International Youth
Exchange Programme (IYEP) is going to be conducted for the exchange of youth delegates
with various countries, to promote mutual understanding of values and culture among them and
to develop better relations. In this regard Department of Youth Affairs, Ministry of Youth
Affairs & Sports is seeking nominations of youth for International Youth Exchange Programme
to be conducted during 2024-25. In this connection all interested 2" and 3™ year students of

NIT Agartala may apply for this programme through google form link given below by
20/04/2024.

https://forms.gle/HrJM2NrN5PAJG77t7

N.B

a) The proposed youth must be an Indian Citizen.

b) The nominated Youth should be in the age group of 15 to 29 years.

c) The nominated youth should be a regular student/volunteer/artist of your organization with
excellent track record.

d) The proposed youth should not have been sent abroad in any of the International Youth
Exchange Programme organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports before.

e) The Youth should have no past history of criminal conviction and no Criminal case must be
pending against him/her in any court of law.

f) The nominated youth must possess a valid Indian passport with expiry date on or beyond

30/09/2025 as on the date of forwarding of nomination.
