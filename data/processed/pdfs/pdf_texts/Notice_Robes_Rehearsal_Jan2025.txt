NOTICE

This is in continuation of this office notice No. NITA.5/(19-Acad)/17th Convocation/2024-25/A-
3851-62 dated 27/11/2024. It is hereby informed to all concerned that:

A-1: Distribution of Robes:

e Robes will be distributed to registered degree recipients on 17th January 2025, from 10:00 AM to
1:00 PM, in their respective departments.

e Norobes will be issued after the deadline.

e Latecomers or those without robes will not be allowed entry into the Convocation Venue and must
collect their degrees in absentia after the Convocation from the Examination Section during office
hours.

A-2: Rehearsal:

e The rehearsal for the Convocation is scheduled on 17th January 2025 at 3:30 PM.
e Only recipients who have registered and collected their robes by the deadline can participate in the
rehearsal and the main event.

e Norehearsal, no entry. Those who miss the rehearsal will not be allowed into the Convocation
venue on the event day.

All recipients are advised to regularly check the Institute website for updates regarding the Convocation.
