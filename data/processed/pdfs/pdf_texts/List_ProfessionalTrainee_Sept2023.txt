Gencral Instructions

1. Provisionally eligible candidates are required to appear for the written test/interview to be
conducted on October 13, 2023.

2. Candidates need to report sharp at 9.30 am at Central Library, National Institute of Technology
Agartala.

2. Bring filled in Application Form (3 copies) along with all the original and self-attested
photocopy of the certificates and mark sheets.

3. Carry a blue/ black ball point pen for the test.

4. You need to bring the copy of this email for the test.

5. Please confirm your participation for the written test by replying to this mail by October 05,
2023.

Eligibility Criteria -

|. First Class (60%) in MLIS

2. Completed MLIS in 2023 or 2022

3. Age below 27 years as on 25.09.2023

Candidates may send their representations/ grievances, related to eligibility and not eligibility, if
any within 05.10.2023 positively in the email id nitacentrallibrary@gmail.com

You must be prepared to stay one more day after the scheduled date of interview.

*Note —

1, Applications that do not meet the eligibility criteria given in this advertisement and/or are
incomplete in any respect shall be summarily rejected.

2. In case the applicant gets screened/shortlisted/selected on the basis of the credentials furnished
by him/her which are, on scrutiny, found to be ‘ncorrect/inadmissible /forged /fabricated/falsified,
his/her candidature shall be liable to be cancelled at any stage/at any time during the tenure of the
trainee.
