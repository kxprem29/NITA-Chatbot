NOTICE

 

 

It is notified for information to all 1*t year B. Tech(With all the branches), BT-MT (Dual
degree programme) & BS-MS(Dual degree programme) (with in the branches) students who are
promoted to 3" semester of various branches during the next academic session, i.e. 2024-25 that
Branch sliding will be made based on the result of 1st Year (1st & 2"4 semesters) of the academic
session 2023-24 and as per Rule No.6.7 of B.Tech regulation of NIT Agartala.

6.7 The applicants may be allowed a change in branch, strictly in order of inter se merit,
subject to the limitation that the strength of a branch should not fall below the existing
strength by more than ten percent and should not go above the sanctioned strength by
more than ten percent.

Students who has secured a CGPA of 8.5 and above out of 10 (in the 1st Year) and also

completed all the common credits requirement in the first two semesters, in their first
attempt without having course requirement in the summer term examination are eligible and
if willing for branch sliding are hereby instructed to apply through the Online Portal
(https://mis.nita.ac.in ) from 4 July, 2024 to 10 July, 2024 . The students to use the Username

and Password of their Institute MIS account for have submission of preferances of branches.

The Guideline for app lying Branch Sliding through MIS may be seen as follws:

 

1) STEP 1:- Students need to login with their MIS Username and Password after entering the above
URL

2) STEP 2:-After login Student will go to the Academic, then transaction and after that Internal
Sliding Form.

3) STEP 3:-After that student will enter his/her AIR of JEE Main Exam held on 2023 in the textbox
provided for AIR. He/she also give his / her branch preferences, upload his / her JEE Main
Scorecard after that he / she will check the declarations available for him / her and after doing
all the task he / she will be able to apply for branch sliding by clicking on Submit button.

The last date filling up the form is 10% July, 2024. Students are instructed to apply
accordingly.
This is issued with the approval of the Competent Authority.
