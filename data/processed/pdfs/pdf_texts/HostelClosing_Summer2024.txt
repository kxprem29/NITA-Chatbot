NOTICE

This is to notify to all the hostellers of NIT Agartala for the following information regarding closer
of hostel and the upcoming summer vacation period: -

Closer Date: The hostel will be closed for 2 4" and 6" semester UG (both Boys & Girls) from 13"
May 2024 and for final year UG students from 20 May2024.

Mess Operation: The regular mess will be closed from 13th May 2024, However, one mess each in
Boys and Girls hostels will remain open during summer vacation on payment basis for PG and PhD
students, and for final year until 20" May 2024. It is mandatory for all hosteller whoever will be
residing during vacation will have to take food in the mess considering hygiene and safety.

Leave application: Mess leave and hostel leave can be applied only upto 12" May 2024.During
summer vacation period (13" May to 14" July2024) leave applications will not be required.

Maintenance work: The hostels will undergo maintenance works during the summer vacation. Except
for PG and PhD students, no other students will be allowed to stay in the hostel premises. Student opting
for internship in and around Agartala are advised to make alternative accommodation arrangement
during this period.

Supplementary Exam: Students with supplementary exam will be permitted to stay in the hostel one
day prior to the exam, during the exam, and one day after exam. Relevant documents must be submitted
to the hostel office via email (chiefwardennita@gmail.com) for approval.

Room Vacation and belongings: Continuing hostellers have to vacate their respective room and deposit
all belongings in a specified common room after properly ID labeling them with ID tags before leaving
the hostel. Belonging must be collected by 20" July 2024.

Reopening information: The hostel and mess will be reopened on 13â„¢ July 2024. The Payment portal
for hostel fees will be accessible from 1* July 2024 till 15" July 2024 only. Students must complete the
requisite hostel payment before returning to the hostel, regardless of availing loans.

Loan document submission: Students who have availed loan and received disbursement prior to 15"
July 2024 will be allotted room provided relevant documents are sent to chief Warden email
(Chiefwardennita@gmail.com) as well as upload the same in MIS.
