Central/State Universities/Institutions including IITs, NITs, RIEs, and Government
Colleges etc. for the Academic Session 2025-26.

specialization and the second major is in an opted discipline.

[NCET] 2025 in 178 Cities (Annexure 1) and 13 mediums across India in Computer Based
