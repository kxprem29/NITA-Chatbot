ltem No.30.32.1: To consider the estimate for the work “Special repair of
Gomati Hostel during the year 2013-14 at NIT Agartala”, at a
cost of Rs. 50,21,082.00.

Due to sad demise of Sri Rahul Kumar, a 2nd year student of ECE Department, who
was residing In Gomati Hostel, a total student unrest took place at NIT Agartala campus
from December 04, 2013 onwards Students gheraoed the administration and demanded
Immediate renovation of all three old Hostels viz Gomati, Dhalai and Howrah Hostels at
par with the new Hostel (Aryabhatta) within a month's time District Administration also
intervened in this matter and advised the Institute authority to accept one of the
demands to renovate all three old Hostels namely Gomati, Dhalai and Howrah within this
time The decision of renovation of three old hostels viz, Gomati, Dhalai and Howrah
was taken in presence of District Magistrate Superintendent of Police and other state
government Officials to subside the agitation of the students

The BWC members discussed in details about the prevailing situation of student
unrest and commitment given by Institute authority and District Administration The
BWC also decided to give the work to M/s NCCL ,a resourceful Contractor who has
been working in other construction works of NIT Agartala campus and has the potential
to complete the work within the time frame as mentioned above
Considering the emergent situation, the B WC approved to execute the works by M/S
NCCL as extra items / deviated quantities of works on the agreement of Administrative
Block | & Il and Seminar Hall at NIT Agartala, with the following conditions at Gomati
Hostel only as an exceptional case
