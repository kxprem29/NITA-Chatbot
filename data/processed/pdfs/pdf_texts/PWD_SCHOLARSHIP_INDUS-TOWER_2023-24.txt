Indus Towers Scholarship Program (For persons with Disabilities) is inviting applications for 2023-
24
Indus Towers Scholarship Program is giving young students with disabilities an opportunity to pursue
their career aspirations by providing them with scholarships for higher education.
The Scholarship Program aims to provide foundational support to students with disabilities who are
deserving but may lack the resources to pursue their dreams. This scholarship aims to empower
them to become successful professionals unlocking their potential to uplift themselves and their
communities up and promote their socio-economic development.
Indus Towers Scholarship Program is a CSR Initiative of Indus Towers Ltd under Flagship program
<SAKSHAM=, implemented by Shishu Sarothi Centre for Rehabilitation and Training for Multiple
Disability.
Eligibility:
 The Applicants are Indian Nationals.
Category I: Students are state subjects/Domicile from NE States, i.e., Arunachal Pradesh,
Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura.
Category II: Students belong to other states in India and are studying in Indian Institute of
National Importance (INIs), Visit: https://www.education.gov.in/institutions-national-
importance
 The Applicants are persons with benchmark disabilities, i.e., 40 % and above.
 The Age of the applicants is between 14-30 Years.
 The Applicants must have completed HSLC/ Class 10 and or/ preceding examination with at
least 55% or equivalent CGPA, i.e., 6.25
 Annual Income of the family falls under BPL or Economic Weaker Section.
 Candidates must be willing to pursue/are pursuing:
1. Academic & Professional Courses from Affiliated Institutions: Higher Secondary/
Graduate/Post-Graduate/Master/ Diploma etc
2. Short-Term Skill Building & Certification Courses: Computer, Vocational, Hospitality etc.
3. Coaching for Employment Oriented Govt Exams
 Course duration not extending beyond 31st March 2025.
Prizes & Rewards: Fee/s (On actuals) paid to the educational institution directly
Application Mode: Online through www.ssscholarship.org
Last date to Apply: 21st August 2023
For any queries, please reach out to us at +91 7086268549
