How to reach NIT Agartala:

 

1. NIT Agartala Campus is around 59 min (26.5km) away from the Maharaja Bir
Bikram Kishore Manikya Airport, Agartala.
Public vehicles are available from airport to reach the campus.

2. NIT Agartala Campus is around 7 min (3.2 km) away from Jirania railway station
and 37 min (21.1 km) away from the Agartala (Badharghat) railway station.
Public vehicles are available from both the railway stations to reach the campus.
