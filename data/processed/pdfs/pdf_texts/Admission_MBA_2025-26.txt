Eligibility: Graduates of fulltime regular course in Engineering discipline with 60% of marks

Institute Level Test (which will be conducted by NIT Agartala). A relaxation of 5% in
marks or 0.5 in CGPA/CPI in 10 point scale in graduation marks may be extended to the SC/ST
candidate. Final year appearing candidates may also apply.

 

Institute Level Test for MBA Admission-2025 will be conducted by NIT Agartala through
online mode.

Group Discussion and Personal Interview process (GD & PI) will be held online mode.

Applicants, who have valid CAT/CMAT/GMAT/MAT score, are not required to appear in the
Institute Level Test (ILT). Either a valid score of CAT/CMAT/GMAT/MAT or Institute Level
Test (ILT) (which will be conducted by NIT Agartala) will be considered for Personal
Assessment (PA) process.

Decision of NIT Agartala regarding date for PI & Institute Level Test will be final.

. No request for changes for dates regarding PI & Institute Level Test will be entertained.

. All the prescribed format of certificates needs to be downloaded from the official website of NIT

Agartala.

. Online portal for application will be closed as per schedule.

. Applications of the candidates may be rejected if supporting testimonials etc. are not uploaded

properly. Applications may also be rejected if any misleading documents or wrong or incomplete
information are given.

The fees payable at the time of admission will be notified on the Institute website in due course
of time.

All selected candidates must stay in the institute hostel.

details are available on the Institute website.
