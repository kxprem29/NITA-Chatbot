It was informed to the BWC that as suggested by Mr. Rajesh Singh, Director NITs in the 30"
BWC meeting held on 09/12/2013 a decision was taken to include one member from CPWD
Agartala in the BWC of NIT Agartala. Accordingly, NIT Agartala invited Chief Engineer,
CPWD Agartala to be a member of the BWC of NIT Agartala and requested to attend the
31° BWC Meeting to be held on 07/02/2014. In response, the CPWD conveyed that as a
policy decision of the Directorate the officer of CPWD will not be participating as a member
of the BWC Meetings for works not executed by the CPWD and expressed their inability to
depute any of the CPWD Officer to attend the BWC Meeting at NIT Agartala which was
communicated by Mr. M. Balachandran, Superintending Engineer (P cum ACC) office of the
Chief Engineer, NEZ-II, GPRA Complex, Gandhigram, Agartala vide letter no. 23(6)(1)/NEZ-
11/2014/109, dated 28/01/2014.

The rates quoted by the lowest bidder have been analyzed for justification by the Estate
Section and found to be reasonable, also in consideration of rates for similar nature of works
received in the near past. The tender opening, evaluation & recommendation committee
scrutinized all the bids and recommended the lowest bid of M/S NCC Limited at a cost of
Rs.152,42,54,361.00(Rupees One Hundred Fifty Two Crore Forty Two Lakh Fifty Four
Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty One only) for award of work.

After threadbare discussion on rate justification and considering all aspect of trends on price
escalation on major construction materials and labour, the BWC arrived at a decision to
award the work to M/S NCC Limited at a cost of Rs.152,42,54,361.00(Rupees One

Hundred Fifty Two Crore Forty Two Lakh Fifty Four Thousand Three Hundred and
Sixty One only).

Item_No.31.4: To consider approval of estimate for the work “Supply, Installation,
Testing & Commissioning of 15 (fifteen) lifts in Administrative
Building 1 & 2, School of Management, International Guest House,
Academic Block (ECE Deptt. & DoNER), Central Library and

Director’s Bungalow at NIT Agartala., at a cost of Rs. 2,86,51,140.00.

The detailed estimate submitted by the Estate Engineer for the work “Supply, Installation,
Testing & Commissioning of 15 (fifteen) lifts in Administrative Building 1 & 2, School of
Management, International Guest House, Academic Block (ECE Deptt. & DoNER), Central
Library and Director's Bungalow at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 2,86,51,140.00 has been
examined by the Building & Works Committee.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal with a total estimated cost of Rs.

2,86,51,140.00 (Rupees Two Crore Eighty Six Lakh Fifty One Thousand One Hundred
and Forty Only) for inviting tender and decided the makes should be restricted to 5(five)
vendors namely (1) OTIS, (2) Thyssen Krupp, (3) Johnson, (4) Kone and (5) Mitsubishi.

The meeting ended with vote of thanks to the chair.
