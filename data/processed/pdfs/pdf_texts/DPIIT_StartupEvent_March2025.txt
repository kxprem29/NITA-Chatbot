Announcing the 2nd edition of
Startup Mahakumbh
Building on this resounding success, we are now pleased to announce the second edition of Startup Mahakumbh – themed,
Startup India @ 2047: Unfolding the Bharat Story.’ This edition will be held from April 3–5, 2025 at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi. The event
is designed to ignite entrepreneurship, foster innovation, and establish itself as a transformative platform for the startup ecosystem.
The Journey Continues- Bigger, Bolder, and More Impactful!
The Startup Mahakumbh program is thoughtfully curated by a distinguished committee of leaders from India’s startup ecosystem. The
event is led by the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI). It is supported by key industry stakeholders such as
ASSOCHAM, TiE, IVCA, and Bootstrap Foundation. The event is further well-supported by the National Startup Advisory Council, DPIIT,
and Startup India.
StartupIndia@2047-UnfoldingtheBharatStory
Startup Mahakumbh is not just an event; it is the beginning of a movement, a conuence of visionaries, change-makers, and
disruptors. It has proved that India is not just participating in the global innovation race but is leading it with a vision as ambitious
as the nation itself.
As India approaches its 100-year milestone of independence in 2047, the Startup Mahakumbh theme, ‘Startup India@ 2047- Unfolding
the Bharat Story,’ celebrates the spirit of innovation rooted in India’s heritage and fueled by cutting-edge technology. It embodies a
bold vision for empowering grassroots entrepreneurs, integrating tradition with technology, and building a sustainable, inclusive
ecosystem. Together, we are shaping a Bharat where startups drive change, inspire progress, and create a legacy of innovation for
generations to come.
