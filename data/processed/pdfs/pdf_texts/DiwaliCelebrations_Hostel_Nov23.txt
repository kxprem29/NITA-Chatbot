NOTICE
This is for general information to all Hostellers that Diwali celebrations are strictly
prohibited inside the hcistel Block. This includes the burning of crackers, lighting of diyas
and candles, and any other activities that could pose a fire hazard.
Diwali is a festival of lights and joy, but it is also important to remember that it is a time
when fire hazards are at their highest. Burning crackers and lighting diyas and candles
inside the hostel Block can easily lead to a fire, which could have devastating
consequences.
For the safety of all hostel residents, students are instructed to refrain from celebrating
Diwali inside the hostel Blocks.
If students wish to celebrate Diwali, do so outside of the hostel premises. There are many
places outside the hostel Block where students can safely enjoy the festivities.
