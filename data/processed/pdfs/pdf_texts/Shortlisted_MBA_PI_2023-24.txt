Instructions to Candidates:

1.

All the candidates provisionally shortlisted must produce all relevant original documents along
with one set of self-attested Photocopy copy and two passport size photographs on the date of
Personal Appearance (GD & PI).

All the candidates are hereby instructed to bring any one National Identity Proof (Aadhaar
Card/Voter ID Card/Pan Card/Driving License) during Institute Level Test (ILT) and Personal
Appearance (GD & PI).

The EWS/ OBC-NCL certificate is required to be submitted during Personal Appearance (GD &
PI). The OBC-NCL/ EWS certificate, wherever applicable, must be issued by the
Competent Authority as per guidelines and norms of GOI.

The applicants, who have not submitted the original final year appearing certificate, need to
submit during Personal Appearance (GD & PI).

All the applicants will have to fulfil the eligibility criteria as notified for admission to MBA
programme 2023-24 (Vide Notification No.NITA.5/(55-Acad)/MBA/2022-23/A.-2268 dated
13.03.2023.

Applicants, if selected after PA process may be admitted provisionally and the candidates will
have to submit certificate of completion of graduation degree and original migration, conduct/
character certificate from the last Institution attended, on the date of provisional admission at
NIT Agartala.

The admission will be provisional subject to physical verification of the documents submitted
and information provided. If at any point of time, any of the information and/ or document(s) are
found incorrect/ false, the provisional admission of the applicant(s) will be immediately

cancelled without any refund.

Candidates should keep on visiting Institute website regularly for any updates.

This is issued with the approval of Authority.
