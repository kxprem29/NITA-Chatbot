Minutes of the 31 Meeting of the
Finance Committee
held on August 09, 2018

 

The 31* meeting of the Finance Committee of National Institute of Technology, Agartala was
held on August 09, 2018 at 10:30 am in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, New
Delhi.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. H. K. Sharma : Chairperson, Finance Committee, NIT Agartala.

(2) Mr. D.K. Singh : Under Secretary (Fin), MHRD, Govt. of India
(Representative of Joint Secretary & Financial
Advisor, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India).

(3) Mr. K. Rajan : Under Secretary, MHRD, Govt. of India.

(4) Prof. S. C. Saha : Member, Professor, NIT Agartala.

(5) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee : Member, Asstt. Professor, NIT Agartala.

(6) Dr. Govind Bhargava : Member Secretary, Finance Committee and

Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Prof. H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala presided
over the meeting. He welcomed all members of the Finance Committee.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 31.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 30 Meeting of the Finance
Committee held on March 27, 2018.

The Minutes of 30™ Finance Committee meeting were confirmed.

Item No. 31.2: Action taken on the decision of the 28 and 29" Meeting of the
Finance Committee held on July 01, 2016 and March 24, 2017
respectively.

Actions taken were noted by the Finance Committee.

Item No. 31.3: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of 38" and 39"
Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee accepted the recommendations of 38" and 39" Building & Works

Committee meeting. The Finance Committee recommended that Item No. 39.13 (Horticulture
and Beautification) of 39" BWC meeting, if not taken up, can be dropped or to be completed

Item No. 31.4: To consider the Annual Budget proposal of the Institute for the
Financial Year 2018-19.

The Annual Budget for financial year 2018-19 was accepted by the Finance Committee and

The Finance Committee received the information.

construction works/ projects.

The Finance Committee noted the statements.

Item No. 31.8: To consider for creation of Corpus Fund of National Institute of
Technology, Agartala.

The Finance Committee approved the proposal with the following modifications:

i) One time payment to Institute Development Fund which will be renamed to “Corpus
Fund” by the students of B. Tech, BT-MT, M. Tech., MCA, MBA & BS-MS,
shall remain unchanged. Such payment for M.Sc. and Ph.D. students shall be Rs.
5000/- and Rs. 15000/-, respectively.

ii) Any interest earned from “Hostel Charges” may be transferred to IRG.

iii) No loan from IRG can be given to Staff.

iv) Corpus Fund may be utilized in emergent situation and due to extreme urgency, as
decided by the Director.

v) IRG Account may be opened separately.

Item No. 31.10: To consider the Research Operating Grant.

The Finance Committee accepted the proposal and recommended the same to BOG for
approval.

Item No. 31.11: Design, Supply, Construction, Erection, trial run and

The Finance Committee received the information and recommended to BOG for approval.

Item No. 31.12: To apprise about Agenda Item No. 30.3 of 30 Finance Committee
meeting in respect of TDS liabilities during the financial year
2007-08 to 2010-11.

The Finance Committee noted that a Committee has been constituted to look into the issue
and therefore, the FC decided that the TDS matter should be put up to the next Finance
Committee meeting along with the Committee report.

Item No. 31.13: To consider the proposal for commencement of ‘Professor A. K.
Misra Memorial Lecture’ along with the opening of Account in the
name of Professor A. K. Misra Memorial Lecture Fund.

The Finance Committee accepted the proposal and recommended the same for approval of
the BoG.

Item No. 31.14: To consider procurement of Desktop PCs and Printers through
GEM portal of the Govt. of India.

The Finance Committee accorded administrative approval and financial sanction for the

phase wise procurement. The FC recommended that old computers to be disposed off by
following Scrap Disposal System/ Rules.

Item No. 31.15: To consider the issue of operation of Mechanical Engineering
Department Professional Promotion Fund (MEDPPF).

The Finance Committee accepted the proposal subject to the condition that no Institute fund
will be provided and such resources generated by all Departments may be kept in this
Account. The available fund in the separate Account in the name of DPPF (Department
Professional Promotion Fund), may be utilised by all departments, at Institute level. Such
Account should be part of Institute Annual Accounts.

Item No. 31.16: To receive information regarding the new PAN of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee received the information.

Item No. 31.17: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.
Item No. 31.17.1: To consider the Annual Accounts of the Institute for the financial

year 2017-18.

The Finance Committee ratified the decision of the Chairperson of Finance Committee and
recommended to BoG for ratification also.

Item No. 31.17.2: | To consider pending audit points
In accordance with the decision already communicated by the Govt. of India to all NITs, the
Finance Committee suggested that the pending audit points which have not been dropped by

the CAG, should be reported to the Finance Committee at its next meeting.

The meeting ended with the vote of thanks to the Chair.
