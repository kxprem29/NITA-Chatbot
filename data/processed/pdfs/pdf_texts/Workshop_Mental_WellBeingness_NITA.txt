About the Institution:

Founded in 1965 as Tripura Engineering College, NIT
Agartala became a National Institute of Technology in
2006. It offers a range of undergraduate,
postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various
disciplines, with a focus on academic excellence,
research, and industry collaborations. The institute
features modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art
facilities.
