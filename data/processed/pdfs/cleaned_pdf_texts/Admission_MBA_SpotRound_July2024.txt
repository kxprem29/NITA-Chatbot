NOTIFICATION

Subject: Notification regarding admission in MBA Programme through Spot Round for the academic session 2024-25.

All candidates from the waiting list (as notified vide Notification No. NITA.5(55-Acad)MBA/2024-25/A-1702, dated 12/07/2024) are hereby instructed to apply with physical appearance on 05/08/2024 at the Institute for admission in the MBA Programme for the academic session 2024-25 in the vacant seats as per the vacant seat matrix given below:

Category | No. of Seats
------- | --------
OP      | 03

1. Candidates must bring the requisite admission fee of Rs. 41,825/- (Forty-One Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty-Five Rupees Only) through Demand Draft in favor of REGISTRAR, NIT AGARTALA, STATE BANK OF INDIA, NIT AGARTALA BRANCH, IFSC Code: SBIN0004491.

2. All interested candidates from the waiting list need to apply to the Dean (AA) in plain paper.

3. Candidates are also instructed to bring ID proof.

4. The candidates will submit their application along with DD and other documents latest by 1 PM of 05/08/2024.

5. The selection will be done strictly as per the merit list.

6. 1) The self-attested photocopies of all documents must be submitted to the MBA Department during the physical reporting on 05/08/2024 from 10:05 AM to 06/08/2024

   2) Candidates must bring all original documents for verification.

7. Classes will commence from 7th August, 2024 (Wednesday) for MBA students admitted during the 2024-25 academic session.

This is issued with the approval of the Competent Authority.
