WEST TRIPURA, PIN: 799046

**Minutes of the 40th-A (Special) Meeting of the Finance Committee**
(Through Circulation)

The Agenda for the 40th-A (Special) meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was circulated among the following members vide Notification No. F.NITA-28(144-REG)/FC/2021-22/1410-14, dated March 24, 2022, and the meeting was convened through circulation.

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Chairperson of BoG & FC and Director, NIT Agartala.
(2) Mrs. Saumya Gupta, Joint Secretary (NITs), MoE, Govt. of India.
(3) Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral, JS & FA, IFD, MoE, Govt. of India.
(4) Prof. (Dr.) A.K. Chakraborty, Professor, Electrical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. (Mrs.) Sumita Deb, Assistant Professor, Electrical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala.
(6) Prof. (Dr.) D. Bhattacharya, Member Secretary & Registrar (I/C), NIT Agartala.

**Item No. 40-A/1:** Regarding approval of the Budget Estimates (Revised) of the Government of India & the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2022-23 as per requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Finance Committee accepted the proposal as per suggestions received from MoE, Govt. of India pertaining to Office Memorandum F.No.3-3/2013-IFD II (e: 9041) dated 04.02.2022, regarding Hiring of High Value Equipment between Department of Higher Education, IFD, Ministry of Education, Government of India, and the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2022-23 as per requirement of GFR 229 (xi). The committee recommended placing it before the Board for approval, incorporating necessary corrections as suggested by a member of the FC.

(Prof. (Dr.) D. Bhattacharya)
