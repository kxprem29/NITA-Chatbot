Minutes of the 52nd Meeting of the
Board of Governors
held on September 29, 2020

The 52nd meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on September 29, 2020, at 12:05 pm in the Virtual Class Room, NIT Agartala, Tripura.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati: Chairperson of BoG, NIT Agartala and Former Director General (Naval Systems & Materials), DRDO

(2) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma: Director, NIT Agartala.

(3) Prof. (Dr.) T. G. Sitharam: Director, IIT Guwahati

(4) Mr. Madan Mohan: Addl. Director General (HE), MHRD, Govt. of India

(5) Mr. D.K. Singh: Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD (Representative of JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India)

(6) Mr. Saju Vaheed A: Director, DHE, Govt. of Tripura.

(7) Dr. D. Bhattacharya: Professor, Math. Dept., NIT Agartala.

(8) Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi): Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Dept., NIT Agartala.

(9) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Secretary, Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

The Chairperson, Prof. (Dr.) T. G. Sitharam, Mr. Madan Mohan, Mr. D. K. Singh, and Mr. Saju Vaheed A attended the meeting through video conference.

Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati, Chairperson of the Board of Governors of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors. The Chairperson invited the Director and Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 52.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 51st Meeting of the Board of Governors, NIT Agartala.

The minutes of the 51st meeting of the Board of Governors held on June 24, 2020, were confirmed.

Item No. 52.2: Actions taken on decisions of the 51st BOG meeting of NIT, Agartala held on January 21, 2020.

The Board noted the actions taken report.

Item No. 52.3: To consider the level of Conferences/ Workshops/ Seminars etc. as Tier-I for attending a program under Cumulative Professional Development Allowance (CPDA) by the regular faculty members of NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the proposal to decide the level of Tier-I as recommended below:

1. Conferences/ Workshops/ Seminars etc. organized by any reputed professional bodies (National/ International) that have existed regularly for the last 30 years in India may be considered as Tier-I level.

2. For attending Conferences/Workshops/Seminars etc. abroad, internationally reputed professional bodies that have existed regularly for the last 20 years may be considered as Tier-I level.

Item No. 52.4: To consider the Audit Fee in respect of Transaction/Accounts Audit of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala for the years 2013-14 to 2018-19.

The Board approved the proposal. Henceforth, the fee raised by AG (Audit) will be paid with the approval of the Director, NIT Agartala.

Item No. 52.6: To consider Amendment in the guidelines for utilization of Cumulative Professional Development Allowance (CPDA).

The Board approved the amendment to the guidelines of CPDA.

Item No. 52.7: To consider the proposal of Fee and User Charges for the July-December semester of 2020.

The Board approved and confirmed the proposal and authorized the Director to take a decision for charging fees and other user charges in consultation with the committee of Deans. The decision may be communicated to the Board.

Item No. 52.8: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 44th Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the recommendation of the 44th Building & Works Committee meeting held on November 15, 2019.

Item No. 52.9: To consider the recommendation of the minutes of the 35th Finance Committee meeting of NIT Agartala held on January 21, 2020.

The Board approved the recommendation of the minutes of the 35th Finance Committee meeting of NIT Agartala held on January 21, 2020.

Item No. 52.10: To consider the issue pertaining to the ongoing TEQIP-III program at NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the revised final phase procurement plan of Rs. 1,15,56,500 (Rupees One Crore Fifteen Lakhs Fifty-Six Thousand Five Hundred only) under TEQIP-III as per prevailing rules. Since the B.O.G. has accorded approval for the procurement plan as above, the required necessary Financial Powers are delegated to the Director to exercise future transactions for this specific case within the Govt. rules/ TEQIP procedures as applicable.

Item No. 52.11: To consider the shifting of MIS servers to the Cloud system of the Govt. of India.

The Board approved the shifting of the Institute's MIS servers to the National Informatics Centre (NIC).

Item No. 52.12: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 52.12.1: To consider the inclusion of Convocation Fee in the final Semester registration fee.

The Board approved the deposit of Rs. 1500.00 as convocation fee by the students/ scholars at the time of submission of the seventh-semester registration fee of UG programs and final semester registration fee of PG/ Ph.D. programs. This will be implemented from the 2020-21 session.

Item No. 52.13.2: To consider the Voluntary Retirement Service (VRS) application submitted by Mr. Sanjoy Deb Rupini, Sr. Assistant, Establishment Section, NIT Agartala.

The VRS of Mr. Sanjoy Dev Rupini, Sr. Assistant, has been accepted and confirmed by the Board as per the extent rule of FR 56 (k) and 48-A.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
