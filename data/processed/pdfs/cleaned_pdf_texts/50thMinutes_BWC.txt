**Minutes of the 50th Building & Works Meeting**
**Held on 20th September, 2022**

The 50th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on 20th September, 2022 at 4:30 PM in the virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Jirania, Agartala, West Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members attended the meeting:

*   Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Director, NIT Agartala
    Chairman, B&WC, NIT Agartala
*   Ms. Veena Dunga, Deputy Secretary (IFI)
    Member, MoE, Govt. of India
*   Prof. (Dr.) Richi Prasad Sharma, Professor, C.E.
    Member, Department, NIT Agartala
*   Prof. (Dr.) Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty, Dean (P&D), NIT Agartala
    Member
*   Er. Sampa Majumder, PWD, Govt. of Tripura
    Member (Representative of Chief Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura)
*   Er. Debasish Pal, Executive Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura
    Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Debasish Bhattacharjee
    Member Secretary, Registrar (I/c), NIT Agartala

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and invited the Dean (P&D) to initiate Agenda-wise discussions.
