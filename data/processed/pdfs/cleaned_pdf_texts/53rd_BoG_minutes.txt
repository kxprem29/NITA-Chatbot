Minutes of the 53rd Meeting of the
Board of Governors
held on November 10, 2020

The 53rd meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on November 10, 2020 at 12 noon in the Virtual Class Room, NIT Agartala, Tripura through online mode.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati: Chairperson of BoG, NIT Agartala and Former Director General (Naval Systems & Materials), DRDO

(2) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma: Director, NIT Agartala

(3) Prof. (Dr.) T. G. Sitharam: Director, IIT Guwahati

(4) Mr. Madan Mohan: Addl. Director General (HE), MoE, Govt. of India

(5) Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral: JS & FA, IFD, MoE, Govt. of India

(6) Mr. U. K. Chakma: Director, DSE, Govt. of Tripura (Representative of State Govt.)

(7) Dr. D. Bhattacharya: Professor, Math Dept., NIT Agartala

(8) Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi): Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Dept., NIT Agartala

(9) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Secretary, Registrar, NIT, Agartala

Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati, Chairperson, Board of Governors of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors. The Chairperson invited the Director and Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 53.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 52nd Meeting of Board of Governors, NIT Agartala.

The Board confirmed the minutes of the 52nd meeting held on September 29, 2020, after necessary corrections regarding the member of the Board nominated by the Central Government.

Item No. 53.2: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 32nd Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on July 31, 2020.

The minutes of the 32nd Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on July 31, 2020, were noted and confirmed by the Board of Governors. However, the MoU with the foreign university is to be taken up as an agenda item in the forthcoming BoG meeting.

Item No. 53.3: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 33rd Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 16, 2020.

The minutes of the 33rd Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 16, 2020, were noted and confirmed by the Board of Governors for immediate action, except Item No. S/33/02, S/33/13, S/33/20, S/33/22 and S/33/24.

Item No. 53.4: To consider the Final Procurement plan (6th Phase) under TEQIP-III.

The procurement plan of Rs. 69,54,200/- and Rs. 3,59,250/- was approved as per TEQIP guidelines.

Item No. 53.6: To consider the recovery of Rs. 18,08,356/- from NCC Ltd. regarding RCC works without using the admixture during the Financial year 2010-11.

The Board approved the recovery of the excess payment of Rs. 18,08,402/- from the pending bills/bank guarantees of NCC Ltd. The minutes are confirmed for implementation.

Item No. 53.7: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Not applicable.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
