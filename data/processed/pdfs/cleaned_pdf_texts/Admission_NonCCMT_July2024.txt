B. Other Eligibility requirements:

1.  Candidates seeking admission in more than one Department will be required to submit a separate application upon payment of the requisite fee.
2.  Conversion from CGPA to percentage or vice-versa given by individual Institute/University will not be considered/allowed. In case both CGPA and percentage are mentioned, only CGPA would be considered.
3.  In case the result of the qualifying degree is awaited, provisional admission is permitted to a student subject to meeting the above minimum qualifying criteria and special eligibility criteria latest by September 30, 2024. In these cases, all exams should have been completed by August 15, 2024. A documentary proof from the Head of the current Institute to that effect in the prescribed format and Mark sheet/ Grade card till the pre-final semester should be uploaded at the time of application. A self-declaration by appearing candidates in the prescribed format should also be uploaded.
4.  An undertaking in the prescribed format should be uploaded by all categories of candidates during application.

C. Instructions for Applicants:

1.  All applicants must apply online through https://admission.nita.ac.in/
2.  The portal is compatible with Mozilla Firefox. Candidates are advised to use the Mozilla Firefox browser only. Candidates are instructed to read the guidelines/instructions in the portal carefully before filling the application.
3.  Multiple programs may be applied for by the applicant. Separate registration and a non-refundable application fee are required for each Department.
4.  After registration, an Application ID (to be used as a login name) and Password will be sent to the email ID provided by the applicant.
5.  Using the login credentials, applicants must log in to the Admission Portal for online application form filling.
6.  Candidates need to fill in the personal details, address details, educational details and upload a photo & signature and other supporting documents.
7.  After filling in all the information, click on Payment Details and Pay the requisite amount.
8.  After Payment, if the amount is deducted from your bank but not reflected in the Admission Portal, candidates need to send an email to nitmisagt@gmail.com.
9.  After payment, if the amount is reflected in the Admission Portal, candidates need to check all the filled information again and finally submit the application & download the Payment & Application form for future reference.
10. Application fees must be paid only through the online payment portal from this site. The requisite non-refundable application fee is Rs. 500/- for GEN/GEN-EWS/OBC candidates and Rs. 300/- for SC/ST/PwD candidates, which has to be submitted through the online payment portal only.
11. After completion and submission of the online form, the filled-in form in PDF format will be generated.

D. The fees payable at the time of admission will be notified on the Institute website in due course of time.

E. Important Note:

1.  All the provisionally selected candidates must fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria as mentioned in section A of this notice. If any candidate is unable to qualify for the minimum eligibility criterion, as per this notification for admission to the M.Tech Programme but considered inadvertently, then his/her candidature will be canceled at a later stage if found ineligible.
2.  A Migration Certificate and Conduct/ Character Certificate are mandatory during provisional admission.

F. Documents (original color scanned) to be uploaded in the portal during application:

1.  10th Mark-sheet and Certificate (Mandatory)
2.  A proof for Date of Birth (Mark-sheet/ Certificate/Birth Certificate etc.) (Mandatory)
3.  12th/Diploma Mark-sheet. (In case there are more than one mark sheet, convert all of them into a single pdf to upload) (Mandatory)
4.  Photo ID as per Govt. of India norms. (Mandatory)
5.  Degree Mark-sheets/grade-cards of all semesters, and up to the pre-final semester for appearing candidates. (In case there are more than one mark sheet, convert all of them into a single pdf to upload) (Mandatory)
6.  Degree / Provisional certificate.
7.  Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS), if applicable, as per GOI format.
8.  In the case of the OBC-NCL/ EWS category, the certificate must be issued by the Competent Authority on or after 01/04/2024.
9.  PwD certificate, wherever applicable. Admission under PwD category will be based on the recommendation of the Medical Board of NIT Agartala.
10. Undertaking (in the prescribed format) for all candidates at Annexure-A (Mandatory)
11. Format of course completion and self-declaration (in the prescribed format) for the appearing candidate, to be uploaded along with the Degree mark-sheets at Annexure-B.
12. Undertaking (in the prescribed format) for appearing candidates only at Annexure-C

NIT Agartala will not be responsible for any failure on the part of the candidates in making payment of the application fee online or for any other reasons.

Application fee will not be refunded if there is no vacant seat in any specialization after admission of students through CCMT-2024.

*   All documents must be in Hindi/ English.
