Minutes of the 52nd (1) (Special) Meeting of the Board of Governors

The Agenda for the 52nd (1) (Special) meeting of the Board of Governors, National Institute of Technology Agartala, was circulated among the following members vide Notification No. F. NITA-28(131-REG)/BOG/20/774-783, dated October 29, 2020. The meeting was convened through circulation.

(1) Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati: Chairperson of BoG, NIT Agartala and Former Director General (Naval Systems & Materials), DRDO

(2) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma: Director, NIT Agartala

(3) Prof. (Dr.) T. G. Sitharam: Director, IIT Guwahati

(4) Mr. Madan Mohan: Addl. Director General (HE), MoE, Govt. of India

(5) Ms. Darshna M. Dabral: JS & FA, IFD, MoE, Govt. of India

(6) Smt. Saumya Gupta: Secretary, Higher Education, Govt. of Tripura

(7) Dr. D. Bhattacharya: Professor, Maths Dept., NIT Agartala

(8) Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi): Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Dept., NIT Agartala

(9) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Secretary, Board of Governors & Registrar, NIT Agartala

No comments were received from any of the members.

Item No. 52(1).1: To consider the Amendments in the Statutes of NITs and IIEST, Shibpur.

The Board approved and adopted the amendments in the Statutes of NITs and IIEST, Shibpur, as per the letter from the Ministry of Education, Department of Higher Education, Govt. of India vide F. No. 35-5/2017 – TS.III, dated October 27, 2020.
