Minutes of the 35th Meeting of the
Board of Governors
held on November 14, 2015
At the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura

The 35th meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was scheduled to be held on November 14, 2015, at 11:00 am in the Conference Hall of NIT Agartala, Tripura. The venue of the meeting was changed to the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala. Accordingly, the meeting was held on November 14, 2015, at 11:00 am in the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Prof. Deepak B. Phatak: Chairperson, BoG of NIT Agartala.
(2) Prof. Gopal Mugeraya: Member, Director of NIT, Agartala.
(3) Shri Sanjeev Kumar Sharma: Director (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India
(Representative of Special Secretary / Additional Secretary / Joint Secretary dealing with Technical Education, Dept. of Higher Education, MHRD).
(4) Shri Jagdish Singh: Secretary to the Government of Tripura, Dept. of Higher Education, Agartala.
(5) Prof. A. Srinivasan: Professor, Dept. of Physics, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Guwahati (nominee of the Director, IIT Guwahati).
(6) Shri Alok Roy Choudhury: Addl. Chief Engineer, PWD, Government of Tripura, Agartala.
(7) Prof. (Mrs.) Uma Bhattacharya: Member, Professor, Dept. of Computer Science & Technology, IEST, Shibpur, West Bengal.
(8) Prof. Richi Prasad Sharma: Member, Professor, Civil Engg. Dept., NIT, Agartala and Registrar (i/c).
(9) Dr. John Deb Barma: Member, Asst. Professor, ME Dept., NIT Agartala.

The Financial Advisor, Department of Higher Education, MHRD, Govt. Of India, could not attend the meeting.

Mr. Sanjeev K. Sharma, Director (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India, attended the meeting through Video Conferencing from his Office, MHRD, Govt. of India, New Delhi.

35th BOG Meeting Minutes
November 14, 2015 / NITA

Prof. Deepak B. Phatak, Chairperson, Board of Governors, NIT Agartala, introduced Shri Jagdish Singh, Secretary to the Government of Tripura, Dept. of Higher Education, Agartala, to all Members of the Board of Governors and welcomed him to the meeting of Board of Governors of NIT Agartala.

Prof. Deepak B. Phatak, Chairperson, Board of Governors, NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all the Members of the Board of Governors.

The Chairperson then invited the Director and the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 35.1: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 18th Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 09, 2015.

The BoG approved the minutes of the 18th Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 09, 2015.

Item No. 35.2: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 19th Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on November 14, 2015.

The BoG approved the minutes of the 19th Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on November 14, 2015.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
