This is to inform all 1st year ITEP (B.Sc. - B.Ed) students, promoted to the second semester, regarding the online semester registration for the upcoming semester. All promoted students are required to complete their registration between 1st and 13th February 2025 by paying the required fee and reporting to the Department (on working days only). Students failing to complete semester registration within the specified dates will be subject to a late registration fine, as follows:

1) Late registration will be permitted until 18/02/2025 (working days only) with a fine of Rs. 5,000/-.

2) Late registration will be permitted until 24/02/2025 (working days only) with a fine of Rs. 10,000/-.

No requests for extension of the registration date will be accepted, nor will waivers of late fees be considered.

a) Students are required to log in to their MIS account at https://mis.nita.ac.in and use the online payment link.

b) Payment through offline or any other mode is not acceptable.

c) Subject registration is mandatory after successful fee payment.

d) Proof of semester registration must be submitted to the Department.

e) Classes will commence on 14/02/2025.
