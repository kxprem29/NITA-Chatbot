The 51st meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on June 24, 2020, at 11:45 am in the Virtual Class Room, NIT Agartala, Tripura.

(3) Mr. Madan Mohan: Addl. Director General (HE), MHRD, Govt. of India

(5) Mr. Saju Vaheed A: Director, DHE, Govt. of Tripura.

(6) Dr. D. Bhattacharya: Professor, Math. Dept., NIT Agartala.

(7) Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi): Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Dept., NIT Agartala.

(8) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Secretary, Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Prof. (Dr.) T. G. Sitharam, Mr. Madan Mohan, Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral, and Mr. Saju Vaheed A attended the meeting through video conference.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Board of Governors, and Director of NIT Agartala presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors. The Chairperson also conveyed his best wishes for everyone's safety during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Chairperson invited the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 51.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 50th Meeting of the Board of Governors, NIT Agartala.

The minutes of the 50th Meeting of the Board of Governors held on January 21, 2020, were confirmed.

Item No. 51.2: To consider the visit of the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

The Board noted the information and appreciated the efforts of the Director, NIT Agartala.

Item No. 51.3: To consider the draft MoA between ILS Hospital and NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the proposal for signing the MoA between ILS Hospital and NIT Agartala as per GFR rules.

Item No. 51.4: Service matter of Shri Sumanta Chakraborty, Ex-Dy. Registrar, in connection with the case filed by him vide case number W.P. (C) 1298/2017, pending before the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura.

Shri Sumanta Chakrabarti joined NIT Agartala on 12.08.2011 as Deputy Registrar, vide appointment letter no. F.NITA.2(166-Estt)/11/2478-82, dated 21st July 2011, from Tripura State Pollution Control Board (A Govt. of Tripura Organization). As per the terms and conditions of the appointment letter, vide clause no. 11 (Retirement Benefits), it is stated that "the Institute has the New Pension Scheme (NPS), Govt. of India, for the employees joining after 01.04.2004. For in-service candidates, appointment under the Institute will be duly supported with pay protection and will be governed by Rules as defined in the FRSR,” which was accepted by Shri. Chakrabarti while joining NIT Agartala. The LPC (Last Pay Certificate) dated 11.08.2011, submitted to NITA by the Tripura State Pollution Control Board (TSPCB) (his previous organization), indicated that he was subscribed to EPF vide EPF Account No. AS/TR/2205/10, which was being maintained by the Commissioner, Employee Provident Fund Organisation, Bholagiri, Agartala, Tripura (Copy enclosed). However, his service condition was changed to GPF, which violates the rules/provisions of the NITSER Act & Statutes and the Govt. of India, and also the terms and conditions of the above-mentioned appointment letter.

Based on the facts, the Board decided that:

(a) Shri Sumanta Chakraorti, who joined NIT Agartala on 12-08-2011, cannot be granted the benefit of GPF as per Clause 29 of NIT Statutes and the appointment letter because he did not have GPF in his previous service at the time of joining NIT Agartala.

(b) The Board decided to keep the actions on hold until the verdict of the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura (W.P (c) 1298/2017).

The above decisions are subject to the verdict of the Writ Petition (C) 1298/2017 filed in the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura.

Item No. 51.6: To consider the recommendation of the minutes of the 34th Finance Committee meeting of NIT Agartala held on October 03, 2019.

Item No. 51.7: To consider the Annual Budget proposal of the Institute for the Financial Year 2020-21.

The Board ratified the decision of the Chairperson, BoG, regarding the Annual Budget proposal of the Institute for the Financial Year 2020-21, subject to the availability of resources. Item No. 51.8: To consider the re-employment of faculty members after superannuation.

The board decided to grant superannuation on the specified date. After superannuation, a contractual extension can be given until the end of the semester. The pay will be fixed after subtracting the pension. The Chairperson, BoG, will grant the extension based on merit.

Item No. 51.9: To consider the issue pertaining to the ongoing TEQIP-III programme at NIT Agartala: Procurement plan of TEQIP.

The 5th Phase Procurement Plan of Rs. 29,50,000.00 (Rupees twenty-nine lakh fifty thousand only) under TEQIP-III, NIT Agartala, has been approved by the Board as per TEQIP rules.

Item No. 51.11: To consider the resignation of faculty members.

Item No. 51.13: To consider signing of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India & the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2020-21 as per the requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Board ratified the MoU between the Department of Higher Education, MHRD, and NIT Agartala.

Item No. 51.14: To consider the action taken report of the external review committee report during 24th and 25th January 2016.

The Board perused and approved the action taken report.

Item No. 51.15: Settlement of pension-related issues of Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar, NIT Agartala.

A draft letter seeking directives from MHRD was approved. The ADG (HE), MHRD, was requested to take up the matter at an appropriate level to resolve the issue. The cheques returned may be deposited in the bank for the time being, and the request of the Registrar to withdraw his GPF may be dealt with as per rules. The Registrar did not participate in the discussion and recused himself.

Item No. 51.16: Consolidated salary for the contractual faculty of NIT Agartala having a Ph.D. degree.

A consolidated salary (fixed) of Rs. 70,000/- is approved for the contractual faculty having a Ph.D. degree. The Board suggested exploring other cases also to grant a consolidated salary. It supersedes the earlier agenda regarding increment for the contractual faculty.

Item No. 51.17: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Not applicable.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
