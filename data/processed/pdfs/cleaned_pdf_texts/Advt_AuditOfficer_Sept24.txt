**NOTICE FOR ENGAGEMENT ON CONTRACT BASIS**

NIT Agartala intends to fill up the following post on a Contract basis through interview, as per details below:

1.  **Name of the Post:** Audit Officer/Audit Advisor
    *   **Maximum Age:** Upto 62 years as on 30-06-2024.
    *   **Monthly Remuneration:** Rs. 50,000/- per month (consolidated). Remuneration will not be a constraint for a deserving candidate. For official tour/visit, TA/DA will be admissible as per rule.
    *   **Place of Posting:** Agartala
    *   **Job Profile:**
        *   a) Audit, review, and evaluate the internal control system of the institute as a whole and assess its adequacy and effectiveness.
        *   b) Perform tasks, consolidate results, and monitor/coordinate with auditors of the institute.
        *   c) Assist the institute in the improvement of internal controls and review financial/administrative procedures.
        *   d) Assist authorities in the interpretation of service rules, CCS rules, and prevailing rules of the institute.
        *   e) Ensure compliance with GFR and other rules & regulations as laid down and are applicable to the Institute.
        *   f) Inspect, examine, and pre-audit bills before submission to the Finance and Accounts Section of the Institute.
        *   g) Oversee pre, internal, statutory, and C&AG audit and ensure compliance with Guidelines.
        *   h) Assist authorities in preparing replies to audit paras.
        *   i) Any responsibility assigned by the Institute authority.
    *   **Contract Tenure:** The contract will initially be for a period of one year and may be considered for further extension up to 3 terms, subject to the requirement of the institute and satisfactory work performance. The original terms & renewal together is limited to 3 years.
    *   **Qualification:**
        *   **Essential:**
            *   Must be a Graduate and a member of "Institute of Chartered Accountants of India".
            *   Good working knowledge of Computer applications and accounting software.
        *   **Desirable:**
            *   Sound knowledge of applicable statutory compliances related to the Education Industry in connection with auditing techniques, internal audit standards issued by ICAI and IIA Certification like Certified Internal Auditor (CIA), Certificate Course on Internal audit by ICAI would be an added advantage.
    *   **Experience:** Holding an analogous post in Level-10 for 05 years or 07 years in Level-9 in the Audit wing of an educational institution of national importance funded by Govt. of India/Central/State Govt. Autonomous bodies/PSU/Central/State University.
