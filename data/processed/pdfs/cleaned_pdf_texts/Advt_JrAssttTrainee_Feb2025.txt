For updates (if any), please visit the institute website: http://www.nita.ac.in

The fixed period internship is open to eligible persons from all castes and categories.

The Institute reserves the right not to enroll any intern if suitable candidates are not found. The Institute may cancel the advertisement in whole or partly without assigning any reason. The Institute also reserves the right to reasonably limit the total number of candidates to be called for Test/Interview. The decision of the Institute in this regard will be final.

Selected candidates shall not have a claim on any regular position and shall not be entitled to any privileges like housing, medical facilities, and other benefits available to regular employees of the Institute.

The shortlisted candidates are required to bring a filled-in Application form along with a recent passport-size photograph. All original documents and a set of self-attested copies of mark sheets and certificates, etc., are to be produced at the time of the interview.

TERMS & CONDITIONS:

The appointment against the above-mentioned position shall be purely on a contract basis on a fixed pay initially for a period of one year and may be considered for further extension up to 3 terms subject to the institute's requirements and satisfactory work performance. The original term & renewal together is limited to 3 years.
