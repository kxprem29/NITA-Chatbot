**Recruitment of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) and Project Assistant for Research Project Funded by Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, under the Core Research Grant (CRG) Scheme**

Applications are invited for the positions of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) and Project Assistant under the sponsored research project. The details of the project and the positions are given below:

**Description of Responsibilities:**

**JRF:**

The primary role of the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) is to assist in research activities under the guidance of the Principal Investigator (PI) or the Co-Principal Investigator. The JRF has to assist the PI and Co-PI in all aspects regarding the execution and successful completion of the project in connection with planning, estimating of tools and equipment, procurement, installation, erection, commissioning, fabrication, and setting up of experimental setups, experimentations, analysis, data collection, data analysis, finding outcomes, publications, conferences, seminars, all documentation of fund utilization viz. preparation of reports and returns etc., and all related works to be assigned from time to time.

**Project Assistant:**

The primary role of the Project Assistant is to assist the PI, Co-PI, and JRF in all aspects regarding the execution and successful completion of the project in connection with planning, estimating, tools, and equipment procurement, installation, erection, commissioning, fabrication, and setting up of experimental setups, experimentations, data collection, conferences, seminars, all documentation of fund utilization viz. preparation of reports and returns, etc., and all related works to be assigned from time to time.
