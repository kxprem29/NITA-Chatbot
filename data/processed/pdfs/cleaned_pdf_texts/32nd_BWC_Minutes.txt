Minutes of the 32nd Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on September 26, 2014

The 32nd meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on September 26, 2014 (Friday) at 6:00 pm in the Conference Room of the NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

*   Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, : Chairman, B&WC,
    Director, N.I.T., Agartala. NIT, Agartala
*   Sri Asit Bhaumik, : Member
    Addl. Chief Engineer / Representative of
    PWD (Civil), Govt. of Tripura.
*   Sri Kajal Banik, : Member
    Executive Engineer (Elect.),
    Electrical Inspectorate/ Representative of
    PWD, Govt. of Tripura.
*   Dr. Sujit Kumar Pal, : Member
    Dean (P & D), N.I.T. Agartala.
*   Dr. Richi Prasad Sharma, : Member
    Professor, Civil Engg. Department.
    NIT Agartala.
*   Mr. Sumanta Chakrabarti, : Member Secretary
    Registrar (I/C), N.I.T., Agartala.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and requested the Registrar (I/C) and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

Item No. 32.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 30th & 31st Building & Works Committee Meeting held on December 09, 2013 & February 07, 2014 respectively.

The minutes of the 30th & 31st Building & Works Committee Meeting held on December 09, 2013 & February 07, 2014 respectively, in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, were circulated to all the members and no modifications/ suggestions/ comments were received. The minutes of the 30th & 31st Building Works Committee of NIT Agartala were confirmed.
