NOTICE

This is to inform all faculty members, staff, and students of NIT Agartala that NIT Agartala will celebrate the 78th Independence Day on 15th August 2024. The programme schedule is given below:

| Sl. No. | Event                         | Date          | Time    | Venue                            |
|---------|-------------------------------|---------------|---------|----------------------------------|
| 1       | National Flag Hoisting        | 15.08.2024    | 8:00 AM | SWAMI VIVEKANANDA KNOWLEDGE PARK |
| 2       | National Flag Hoisting and March Past | 15.08.2024    | 8:30 AM | ABV Sports Complex               |
| 3       | Address by the Hon'ble Director, NITA | 15.08.2024    | 8:40 AM | ABV Sports Complex               |
| 4       | Cultural Program               | 15.08.2024    | 8:50 AM | ABV Sports Complex               |

It is also informed that the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign, under the aegis of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, will be celebrated across the nation from 13th to 15th August 2024. All faculty, staff, and students of the institute are encouraged to hoist the Indian National Flag on their premises during the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign and to upload selfies with the Tiranga on the website www.harghartiranga.com.

All faculty members, staff, and students of NIT Agartala are requested to participate in the program as per the mentioned schedule and make it a grand success.
