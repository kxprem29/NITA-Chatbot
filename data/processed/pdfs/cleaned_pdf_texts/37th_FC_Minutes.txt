**Minutes of the 37th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on September 29, 2020**

The 37th meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on September 29, 2020, at 11:00 am in the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati
Chairperson of BoG & FC, NIT Agartala and Former Director General (Naval Systems & Materials) of DRDO

(2) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma
Director of NIT Agartala

(3) Mr. Madan Mohan
Addl. Director General (HE), MHRD, Govt. of India

(4) Mr. D. K. Singh
Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD
(Representative of JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India)

(5) Dr. D. Bhattacharya
Professor, Math Dept., NIT Agartala

(6) Dr. Govind Bhargava
Member Secretary of FC and Registrar of NIT Agartala

Dr. S. C. Sati, Chairperson of BoG & FC; Mr. Madan Mohan, ADG, MHRD; and Mr. D. K. Singh, Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD attended the meeting through video conference.

The Chairperson of the Finance Committee, NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members. The Chairperson then invited the Director and Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

**Item No. 37.1:** Confirmation of the Minutes of the 36th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on June 24, 2020.

The minutes of the 36th meeting of the Finance Committee held on June 24, 2020, were confirmed.

**Item No. 37.2:** Action taken on the decision of the 36th Finance Committee meeting held on January 21, 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the action taken.

**Item No. 37.3:** To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as on September 09, 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute.

**Item No. 37.4:** Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted the present status of the TEQIP-III project of NIT Agartala.

**Item No. 37.5:** To consider the Physical & Financial Statement of the ongoing construction works/projects.

The Finance Committee considered the Physical & Financial statement of ongoing construction works/projects of NIT Agartala.

**Item No. 37.6:** To consider the Audit Fee in respect of Transaction/Accounts Audit of National Institute of Technology, Agartala for the years 2013-14 to 2018-19.

The Finance Committee approved the proposal. Henceforth, fees raised by AG (Audit) will be paid with the approval of the Director, NIT Agartala.

**Item No. 37.7:** To consider Amendment in the guidelines for utilization of Cumulative Professional Development Allowance (CPDA).

The Finance Committee approved the amendment in the guidelines of CPDA.

**Item No. 37.8:** To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 44th Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee approved the recommendation of the 44th Building & Works Committee meeting held on November 15, 2019.

**Item No. 37.9:** To consider the proposal of Fee and User Charges for July-December semester of 2020.

The Finance Committee approved and confirmed the proposal and authorized the Director to take decisions for charging fees and other user charges in consultation with the committee of Deans, and the decision may be informed to the Board.

**Item No. 37.10:** To consider the pensionary benefits extended to the superannuated employees of NIT Agartala during the year 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the pensionary benefits extended to the superannuated employees of NIT Agartala during the year 2020. The Finance Committee also advised that these are purely administrative issues and need not be put up in the Finance Committee.

Not applicable.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
