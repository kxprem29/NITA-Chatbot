The Chairperson invited the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

The minutes of the 47th Meeting of the Board of Governors held on June 26, 2019, were confirmed after the incorporation of comments from JS & FA MHRD and Under Secretary (NIT) MHRD with regard to Item No. 47.28. Reference to the letter of MHRD vide no. F. 35-5/2018-TS III dated 4th April 2019 should also be incorporated at Item No. 47.15 with regard to Recruitment Rules for Non-Teaching positions.

Item No. 48.2: Actions Taken on Decisions of the 46th BOG Meeting of NIT, Agartala held on February 13, 2019

Actions taken on the decisions of the 46th BoG meeting were noted.

Item No. 48.3: To Consider Implementation of Strengthening of Administration — Periodic Review of Central Government Employees under Fundamental Rule (FR) 56 (j)/ (l) and Rule 48 of CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972

The Board adopted the proposal for its implementation for periodic review of Central Government Employees for strengthening of administration under Fundamental Rule (FR) 56 (j)/ (l) and Rule 48 of CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972. The decision is confirmed.

Item No. 48.4: To Consider Fixation of License Fee of Different Buildings to be Charged by NIT Agartala for Use of Office Buildings, Academic Buildings, Market Complex by Contractors/Agencies/Shopkeepers etc.

The Board approved and confirmed the recommendations of the committee.

Item No. 48.5: To Consider the Recommendation of the Selection Committee Meeting held during August 26-27, 2019 at Conference Room of International Guest House, NIT Agartala for Recruitment of Assistant Registrar and Assistant Librarian

Item No. 48.6: International Conference on Applied Mathematics and Computational Intelligence (ICAMCI-2020) at Department of Mathematics during 19-20th March 2020

The Board approved the proposal without any financial expenditure from the sanctioned Ministerial fund.

Item No. 48.7: To Consider the Assets & Liabilities Dues for an Amount of Rs. 56,49,968 to the Govt. of Tripura

The Board appreciated the efforts to resolve the long-pending matter and approved to pay Rs. 56,49,968/- to DHE, Government of Tripura to settle the assets & liabilities dues. However, it was opined that a letter can be sent to MHRD to get the position cleared.

Item No. 48.8: To Consider the Recommendation of the Minutes of 33rd Finance Committee Meeting of NIT Agartala held on June 26, 2019

The Board approved the minutes of the 33rd Finance Committee Meeting of NIT Agartala held on June 26, 2019.

Item No. 48.9: Absorption of Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar w.e.f. 05-05-2017 and Counting of his Past Service for the Purpose of Pension, Leave etc. as per CCS Rule 1972

The Board declined the proposal and made it clear that the absorption at NIT Agartala is in violation of NIT statutes/Recruitment rules. The Board directed the Registrar to get his Superannuation cleared from his parent department and inform NIT Agartala within a month so that his services for the remaining period could be decided till the age of 62 years or for a total tenure of 5 years, whichever is earlier.

The BoG decided that his lien in his parent Organization may be treated as extended up to 31.07.2019 (the date of his superannuation in his parent organization), and his pension contribution and leave salary contribution during the period of his service at NIT Agartala till the date of his superannuation in his parent organization may be transferred to his parent organization. The BoG also decided that w.e.f. 1.8.2019, his remuneration as Registrar NIT Agartala may be re-fixed at his last pay drawn minus pension, in compliance with DoPT OM No. 3/3/2016-Estt (Pay II) dated 1.05.2017 as amended from time to time. Also, in view of Rule 55-A of CCS (Pension) Rules 1972, he shall not be eligible to draw Dearness Relief on Pension during the period of his re-employment as Registrar, NIT Agartala. During the proceedings of this discussion, the Registrar went outside and did not participate.

Item No. 48.10: International Workshop on Advanced Seismology, Seismic Hazard and Earthquake at NIT Agartala

The Board approved the proposal without any financial expenditure from the sanctioned Ministerial fund.

Item No. 48.11: Absorption of Mr. Sunil Das, Estate Engineer at NIT Agartala

The proposal of the direct absorption from the State Services was declined due to the absence of provision in Recruitment rules/NIT Statutes.
