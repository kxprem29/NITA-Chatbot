Minutes of the 36th Meeting of the
Finance Committee
held on June 24, 2020

The 36th meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on June 24, 2020, at 11:00 am in the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma: Chairperson of Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala.

(2) Mr. Madan Mohan: Addl. Director General (HE), MHRD, Govt. of India

(3) Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral: JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India.

(4) Dr. D. Bhattacharya: Professor, Math. Dept., NIT Agartala.

(5) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Member Secretary of FC and Registrar of NIT Agartala.

Mr. Madan Mohan and Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral attended the meeting through video conference.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson of the Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members. The Chairperson also conveyed his best wishes for everyone's safety during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 36.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 35th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on January 21, 2020.

The minutes of the 35th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on January 21, 2020, were confirmed after deliberation.

Item No. 36.2: Action taken on the decision of the 34th Finance Committee meeting held on October 03, 2019.

The Finance Committee noted the action taken.

Item No. 36.3: To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as on May 30, 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute. On page 61, "Grant received during 2019-2020" may be read as "Grant received during 2020-21."

Item No. 36.4: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted the present status of the TEQIP-III project of NIT Agartala.

Henceforth, the Faculty In-charge, TEQIP will provide detailed figures and development updates to the Finance Committee.

Item No. 36.6: To consider the Annual Budget proposal of the Institute for the Financial Year 2020-21.

The Finance Committee perused and ratified the decision of Chairperson, BoG & FC with regard to the Annual Budget proposal, subject to the availability of resources. The issue of delegation of power was raised by one of the members. NIT Agartala will examine the NITSER Act & Statutes, and the matter will be apprised accordingly.

Item No. 36.7: To consider freezing of Dearness Allowance to the employees & Dearness Relief to pensioners at current rates until July 2021 and to authorize NIT Agartala to implement further DA orders as declared by the Govt. of India.

The Finance Committee approved the proposal for freezing DA to employees and DR to pensioners and authorized the Director of NIT Agartala to implement further DA orders as and when the Govt. of India notifies them.

Item No. 36.8: To consider the transfer of GPF amount with interest from LICI Silchar to the NITA Staff Benefit Trust Account.

The agenda is deferred and will be put up again after gathering information from different CFTIs.

Item No. 36.9: To consider signing a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India & the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2020-21 as per the requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Finance Committee ratified the decision to sign the MoU between the Department of Higher Education, MHRD, Govt. of India, and the National Institute of Technology Agartala.

Item No. 36.10: To consider the employer's contribution to the NPS Account from 14% to 10% of Basic Pay and Allowance.

The status quo of March 2020 will be maintained until communication is received from the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India.

Item No. 36.11: To consider the MACP to Late Bhusan Sarkar, Retired Group-C employee of NIT Agartala.

The agenda is withdrawn.

Item No. 36.12: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Not applicable.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
