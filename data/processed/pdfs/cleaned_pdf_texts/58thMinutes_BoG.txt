Item No. 58.2: To consider the Reservation in the Post of Associate Professor and Professor for direct recruitment at NIT Agartala.

NIT Agartala published rolling advertisements on its website vide Notification No. F. NITA. 2 (521-ESTT)/2022/Vol. III/734, dated: 22-04-2022 and No. F. NITA. 2 (545-ESTT)/2022/1159, dated: 11-04-2022. The last date for submission of the online application form for the first cycle is 30th June 2022. Already a good number of applications have been received. Now, due to the Implementation of Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers' Cadre) Act, 2019, a new reservation roster is to be prepared by the Institute following Government Policy. In this respect, a committee constituted by the Institute prepared a Cadre and Category wise distribution of the total sanctioned faculty positions (presently occupied posts and vacant posts), and the same was placed before the Board. Representatives from the Bureau of MoE and the Integrated Finance Division (IFD) of the Ministry in principle accepted but suggested certain points to be looked at. After deliberation over those points in consultation with the experts, the committee members prepared a modified self-explanatory cadre wise and category wise break-up of posts (Annexure - A), for kind consideration of the Board. During the confirmation of the 57th BoG meeting minutes, the Board deliberated on the category wise and cadre wise break-up of the post and approved the same in compliance with the Central Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers' Cadre) Act, 2019.

Item No. 58.3: Demand Notice U/s 147 r.w.s 144 issued by the Income Tax Department for A.Y. 2013-14 to 2017-18 against NIT Agartala amounting to Rs. 39,49,64,310/-.

The matter was discussed elaborately, and the Board decided that the matter be defended while hiring a legal counsel and all the channels be explored.

Item No. 58.4: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 58.4.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 57th and 57th-A (Special) Meeting of the Board of Governors, NIT Agartala.

The minutes of the 57th and 57th-A (Special) Board of Governors meeting were circulated among the Members, and no comment was received from the Members of the Board of Governors. The minutes of the 57th and 57th-A (Special) meeting of the Board of Governors were confirmed by the Board.

The Board also opined that the filling of the Registrar position should be taken up on priority.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
