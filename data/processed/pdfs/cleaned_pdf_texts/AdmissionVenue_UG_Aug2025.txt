How to reach NIT Agartala:

1.  NIT Agartala Campus is approximately 59 minutes (26.5km) away from the Maharaja Bir Bikram Kishore Manikya Airport, Agartala. Public vehicles are available from the airport to reach the campus.

2.  NIT Agartala Campus is approximately 7 minutes (3.2 km) away from Jirania railway station and 37 minutes (21.1 km) away from the Agartala (Badharghat) railway station. Public vehicles are available from both the railway stations to reach the campus.
