**NOTICE**

This is for the general information of all first-year UG, PG, and Dual Degree students of NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala who wish to stay in the hostel. They are required to deposit the requisite Hostel Fee and Mess Charges for the Odd Semester (Session 2024-25) through the MIS Online Payment mode. Use the login ID and password provided in the Provisional Admission Letter of NIT Agartala to log in to your MIS account. The Provisional Admission Letter is generated after completion of physical verification.

(1) Details of hostel fees are given in Table A.

**TABLE A: Details of Hostel Fee**

| Eligibility | Hostel Occupancy | Initial Fee (₹) | Hostel Fee (₹) | Mess Advance (₹) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| UG (First Year) | Double (Limited) | 5000* | 2000 | 6200 | 23445** |
| PG (First Year) | Single (All) | 5000* | 2000 | 7200 | 23445** |

**Note:**

*   ₹5000 is refundable after completion of the course.
*   ₹23445 is the Mess Advance.

Due to limited vacancy in the girls’ hostel, girls whose home state is other than Tripura will be given priority. Girls from Tripura must apply through the link given below. Eligible students will be notified on a later date.

[https://forms.gle/wQ1b87quPkpRXdTK7](https://forms.gle/wQ1b87quPkpRXdTK7) (B.Tech and Dual Degrees BSMS, BTMT, and PG)

(2) Eligible students, as per Table A, should pay the one-time hostel initial fee (₹7000) through their MIS account using the procedure mentioned below after physical reporting:

Login to MIS Account → Online Payment → SBI EPAY → Common Payment Interface → Select Receipt Type → Hostel Initial Fee → Pay

(3) After paying the initial hostel fee, students should submit the hostel fee (₹29645 for Double Occupancy and ₹30645 for Single Occupancy) through their MIS account using the procedure mentioned below:

Login to MIS Account → Online Payment → SBI EPAY → Common Payment Interface → Select Receipt Type → Hostel Fee → Pay

(4) After paying the hostel fee, proceed to room allotment in the MIS using the procedure mentioned below:

Hostel → Transaction → Automatic Room Allotment → Allot Room

(5) At the hostel office of RNT/Gargi Hostel, produce the soft copies of the receipts (generated in steps 2 & 3) worth ₹35845 and ₹36845, the hostel room allotment letter (generated in step 4), along with the filled hostel application form in hard copy.

(6) For any queries related to the hostel, students can contact hostel wardens and supervisors.

**RULES AND REGULATIONS OF HOSTELS, NIT AGARTALA**

1.  Keep calm and quiet to maintain peace and harmony inside the hostel and mess.
2.  The allotted room/floor/corridor/toilet must be kept neat and clean for healthy living for all individuals.
3.  Personal laptops without external speakers, mobile phones, and power banks are only allowed for use. The use of any other electrical appliances for any reason is strictly prohibited, and a penalty of ₹5000/- will be imposed.
4.  Ragging (verbal/physical/psychological) is a crime and is strictly prohibited on campus and off-campus. Punishment for raggers will be as per the orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India.
5.  Fighting among each other in a group or as an individual is a punishable offense under any circumstances. ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty against each individual for each case, and further disciplinary action will be imposed with immediate effect.
6.  Cooking is not allowed inside the hostel rooms, and ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty for each event against each individual.
7.  Using any kind of motor vehicle is strictly prohibited in the campus premises. If caught, respective bikes/scooters will be seized until the end of the owner's and rider(s)' academic program.
8.  Smoking/chewing tobacco/consumption of alcoholic drinks or supplying any of these items to others is strictly prohibited in hostel/campus premises, and ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty for each individual.
9.  No hosteller is allowed to take any article/utensils from the mess to outside, not even to the hostel room, and ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty against each individual for each case.
10. Any unauthorized accommodation by the hosteller for friends/visitors/relatives in the hostel room is strictly prohibited, and ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty against each individual. In the case of Gargi Hostel, only lady guardians are allowed to visit the boarders on Saturday, Sunday, and Institute Holidays only during 12:30 PM to 3:30 PM for a duration of 30 minutes/as permitted by the concerned hostel authority, after submitting a photocopy of government-issued identity proof.
11. Photograph pasting/drawing/writing on the wall of the room/furniture/door/bathrooms/common area of the hostel is strictly prohibited, and ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty for each case against each individual.
12. The allotted room of the hostel cannot be interchanged/altered under any circumstances without prior approval of the competent authority; otherwise, ₹5000/- will be imposed as a penalty against each individual for each alteration, and further disciplinary action may be taken. Hostel administration reserves the right to shift residents from one room to another room of the same hostel to maintain discipline at the hostel and campus premises.
13. Do not write your name/roll number/department on the room door because your room will be used further by others in the following years.
14. Birthday celebrations inside the hostel room are strictly prohibited.
15. Hostellers are not allowed to remain absent from the hostel at night without prior permission from the Chief Warden/Warden/Asst. Warden of that hostel. If found, disciplinary action will be initiated with immediate effect.
16. Using the roof of any hostel/mess by hostellers/anyone for any reason is strictly prohibited.
17. Boys’ hostellers are not allowed to stay outside the hostel from 8:00 PM to 4:30 AM for any reason except medical issues. Girls’ hostellers are not allowed to stay outside the hostel from 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM for any reason except medical issues.

18. During summer and winter vacations, girls’ hostellers are not allowed to stay outside the hostel from 5:00 PM to 7:00 AM for any reason except medical issues.

Prior approval from the competent authority has to be taken in advance for staying in the hostel during the vacation period.
20. For proper supervision of the regular latecomers at the hostel, a late entry register will be maintained, and after 3 late entries against any hosteller, one warning letter may be issued to him/her. Despite the warning letter, if the student is late, then appropriate penalty can be imposed upon the student.
21. Intentional damage of any item in the hostel room/floor/corridor is the occupant's liability to get it repaired/replaced. Moreover, damage to hostel property such as doors, windows, electrical fittings, toilet fittings, glass panes, etc., will lead to disciplinary action.
22. Misuse of water, electricity, and other facilities is liable to disciplinary action.
23. Hostellers are solely responsible for keeping valuable belongings with them. Hostel administration shall not be liable for loss/damage of such valuable items.
24. Mess timings must be strictly adhered to as follows:

    Breakfast: 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM
    Lunch: 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM
    Snacks: 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM (For Gargi Hostel Only)
    Dinner: 7:30 PM to 9:30 PM

    Food will not be served before or after the prescribed timing. Sometimes, unintentional delays may happen in preparing food and getting ready in the mess by the mess staff. Keep patience and obey the mess rule for this occurrence.
25. Do not waste food in the mess. Take all you want, but eat all you take.
26. Any sort of complaint for electrical/civil/carpentry/plumbing maintenance must be reported in the complaint register of the respective hostel.
27. Playing outdoor games inside the hostel is strictly prohibited.
