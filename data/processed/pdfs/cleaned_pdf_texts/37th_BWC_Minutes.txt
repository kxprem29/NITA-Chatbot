Minutes of the 37th Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on September 06, 2016

The 37th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on September 06, 2016, at 6:00 pm in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and requested the Registrar (I/C) and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

Item No. 37.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 36th BWC Meeting held on 28th June, 2016.

The minutes of the 36th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on 28th June, 2016, in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura, were circulated to all the members. No comments were received from any of the members. So, the minutes of the 36th Building & Works Committee were confirmed.

Item No. 37.6: To consider the extra item for the work “1500 boys hostel at NIT Agartala”.

The BWC has approved the proposal for the execution of PVC water stops and brick-on-edge flooring as extra items at a cost of Rs. 1,80,996.00 + Rs. 3,85,388.00 = Rs. 5,66,384.00.

Item No. 37.7: To consider the proposal for the construction of a Children's Park near the residential block, Horticulture, and land development work (in front of the Academic Building) at NIT Agartala at a tentative cost of Rs. 62,40,285.00.

The Building & Works Committee suggested that the estimate be re-casted, showing the minimum needs, and may be placed before the next BWC for approval prior to awarding the work to the CPWD against the MOU of H4B (2 blocks) at NIT Agartala.

Item No. 37.8: To consider the proposal for the construction of “Revamping of lakes, water harvesting, and beautification of water bodies at NIT Agartala at a tentative cost of Rs. 1500.00 lakh.

The Building & Works Committee suggested that, upon obtaining the DPR along with the detailed estimate from CPWD, the same may be placed in the next BWC for consideration.

Item No. 37.9: To consider the award of work “Up-gradation of the internal main road with both side footpath including side drain, electric post, bus stop, sitting arrangement at a certain interval & conduit from main gate-1 to auxiliary gate-2 via housing and boys hostel & auxiliary gate-2 to main gate-1 and auditorium to existing play ground (old) at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 12,62,33,747.00.

The Institute invited a 2nd call for tenders vide PNIT No.23(164)/NITA/Estate/2016-17/2139-43, dated: 24-06-2016. In response, 6 (Six) tenders were received and opened by the duly constituted Institute Tender Evaluation Committee on 18-07-2016.

On scrutiny of the tenders, it was found that the tenders of (1) Sri Jahar Sur Choudhury & (2) Sri Tapan Saha, Contractors, fulfilled the technical criteria. Thereafter, the financial bids of the said two tenderers were opened on 22-07-2016 by the duly constituted tender evaluation committee and found that Sri Jahar Sur Choudhury, Contractor, was the 1st Lowest with a quoted rate of Rs. 12,16,37,422.00.

The BWC considered the proposal for onward transmission to the F.C and BOG for approval.

Item No. 37.10: Any other item with the permission of the chair.

There was no other item for discussion.
