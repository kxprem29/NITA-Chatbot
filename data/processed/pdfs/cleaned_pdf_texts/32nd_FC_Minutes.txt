Minutes of the 32nd Meeting of the Finance Committee
held on February 13, 2019

The 32nd meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala was held on February 13, 2019, at 11:30 am in the Conference Room of the NIT Transit House, New Delhi.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Prof. H.K. Sharma: Chairperson, Finance Committee, NIT Agartala.
(2) Mr. Madan Mohan: DDG (HE), MHRD, Govt. of India
[Representative of Joint Secretary (dealing with NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India]
(3) Mr. D.K. Singh: Dy. Secretary (FD), MHRD, Govt. of India
[Representative of Joint Secretary & Financial Advisor, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India].
(4) Prof. S. C. Saha: Professor, NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee: Asst. Professor, NIT Agartala.
(6) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Member Secretary, Finance Committee and Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Prof. H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting. He welcomed all members of the Finance Committee.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 32.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 31st Meeting of the Finance Committee held on August 09, 2018.

The minutes of the 31st Finance Committee meeting held on August 09, 2018, were confirmed after deliberations on the comments submitted by the Under Secretary to the Govt. of India, MHRD, representative of Additional Secretary (TE), MHRD, vide letter F. No. 1-2/2018-TS.II, dated: 4th September 2018. Agenda Item No. 31.9 of the 31st Finance Committee meeting needs to be reviewed. The Finance Committee authorized the Director of NIT Agartala to review the user charges at any time in the future, as per the need, and the same may be brought to the attention of the Finance Committee.

Item No. 32.2: Action taken on the decision of the 30th Finance Committee held on March 27, 2018.

The Finance Committee noted the actions taken.

Item No. 32.3: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 40th Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The recommendations of the 40th BWC meeting were accepted.

Item No. 32.4: To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as on January 31, 2019.

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute.

Item No. 32.5: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted the status of the TEQIP-III project.

Item No. 32.6: To consider the Physical & Financial Statement of the ongoing construction works/projects.

The Finance Committee noted the Physical & Financial statement of the ongoing construction works/projects.

Item No. 32.7: To consider the Separate Audit Report on the accounts of the Institute for the financial year 2017-18.

The Finance Committee considered the report and opined that the observations of the CAG are to be reported in the forthcoming Finance Committee meeting.

Item No. 32.8: To consider some Maternity Benefits for the women employees of NIT, Agartala, including contractual women employees.

The Finance Committee adopted the Maternity Benefits for the women employees of NIT Agartala.

Item No. 32.9: TDS liabilities during the financial year 2007-08 to 2010-11.

The Finance Committee noted the actions of the IT Department and the actions taken by the Institute, and also agreed with the legal opinion. The Finance Committee further instructed that THCB be communicated with again, and in case a reply from THCB is not received this time also, actions are to be taken against the officials concerned.

Item No. 32.10: To consider the Delegation of Administrative and Financial Power with regard to LTC issues for Teaching and Non-Teaching staff (regular and contractual) of NIT Agartala to the Dean (FW) and Registrar.

The Finance Committee accepted the proposal.

Item No. 32.11: To consider the Delegation of power to the Dean (Faculty Welfare), NIT Agartala, to sanction CPDA expenses to faculty members of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee accepted the proposal.

Item No. 32.12: Income Tax benefit against the donation to NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee approved the proposal of forming a TRUST to receive donations/funds/contributions.

Item No. 32.13: Admission to Ph.D. scholars shall be 50% of the sanctioned intake of the faculty members.

The Finance Committee agreed to the proposal.

Item No. 32.14: To consider user charges of the students to meet the minimum expenses for facilities of Bharat Ratna Atal Bihari Vajpayee Sports Complex.

The Finance Committee approved the user charges of the Sports Complex of Rs. 1000/- per semester.

Item No. 32.15: To consider the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India & the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2019-20 as per requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Finance Committee considered the MoU for 2019-20.

Item No. 32.16: To consider the sitting fee being paid to Members of different Committees of NITA.

The Finance Committee approved the sitting fee of Rs. 5000.00 for all the members attending the Statutory meetings (BoG, FC & BWC) and other official meetings. The Finance Committee superseded all the earlier decisions.

Item No. 32.17: To consider the deployment of TSR personnel on security measures of NIT Agartala.

To strengthen the security of NIT Agartala, the salary of nineteen (19) personnel (18 nos. of TSR Security Guards + 1 no. of Supervisor) are approved. However, the Finance Committee suggested that the same number of Security Guards may be reduced/may not be recruited, if further requirement existed, from an outsourcing company in a phased-wise manner.

Item No. 32.18: To consider the revised emoluments and guidelines on service conditions for research personnel engaged in the R&D programme of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee adopted the revised emoluments and guidelines.

Item No. 32.19: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 32.19.1: To consider the enhancement of the consolidated salary of the Medical Officer (Contractual) engaged in the Medical Unit of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee approved the enhancement of salary.

Item No. 32.19.2: To consider the Annual Report for the year 2017-18 of the Institute.

The agenda was clubbed with Item No. 32.7.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
