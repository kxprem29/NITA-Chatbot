Minutes of the 35th Meeting of the
Finance Committee
held on January 21, 2020

The 35th meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on January 21, 2020, at 11:00 am in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, New Delhi.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Prof. H. K. Sharma : Chairperson, Finance Committee, NIT Agartala.
(2) Shri D. K. Singh : Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD.
(Representative of JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India)
(3) Shri Indrajit Kuri : Under Secretary, MHRD.
(Representative of Joint Secretary (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India)
(4) Prof. S.C. Saha : Professor, NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. Govind Bhargava : Member Secretary, Finance Committee and Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee, Associate Professor, NIT Agartala, could not attend the meeting.

Prof. H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Finance Committee, and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting. He welcomed all the members of the Finance Committee.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate a discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 35.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 34th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on October 03, 2019.

The minutes of the 34th Finance Committee meeting held on October 03, 2019, were confirmed after deliberation. The decision of the 48th BoG meeting regarding the re-employment of Dr. Govind Bhargava as Registrar is to be incorporated.

Item No. 35.2: Action taken on the decision of the 33rd Finance Committee meeting held on June 26, 2019.

The Finance Committee noted the action taken.

Item No. 35.3: To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as on January 09, 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute.

Item No. 35.4: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted the present status under the TEQIP — III project.

Item No. 35.5: To consider the Physical & Financial Statement of the ongoing construction works/ projects.

The Finance Committee noted the Physical & Financial statement for ongoing construction works/ projects of the Institute.

Item No. 35.6: To consider the Separate Audit Report on the accounts of the Institute for the financial year 2018-19.

The Finance Committee approved the Separate Audit Report for the year 2018-19. However, the committee was of the opinion that the reply submitted to CAG along with the observation are to be henceforth brought to the notice of the FC.

Item No. 35.7: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 43rd Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted Item Nos. 43.1 to 43.14, 43.17 and 43.19 to 43.23 of the 43rd Building & Works Committee. The recommendations of the 43rd Building & Works Committee meeting held on June 21, 2019, with regard to Item Nos. 43.15, 43.16, 43.18, and 43.24.1 were accepted and recommended to be placed before the BoG.

Item No. 35.8: Adoption of the guidelines for settlement of claims for compensation as enunciated in various judgments of the Hon’ble Supreme Court in case of death/ permanent incapacitation due to unintended/ unforeseen occurrences during maintenance, operation, and provisioning of public services.

The guidelines of DoT vide Memorandum No. 36-11/2015-SR, dated 5th December 2016, and the Institute committee are approved. However, point no. 2 of the institute’s committee report may be read as, the guidelines will be applicable for all employees who are not covered under Group Insurance. Superannuated employees from any sector will not be covered under this scheme.

Item No. 35.9: To consider GPF balance in respect of Dr. Vidyut Dey, Assistant Professor, Production Engineering Department.

The Finance Committee noted the proposal and suggested recovering the interest from Dr. Vidyut Dey during the period of withholding the amount/ fund.
