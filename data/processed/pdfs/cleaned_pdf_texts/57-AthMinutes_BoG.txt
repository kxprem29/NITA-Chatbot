Minutes of the 57th-A (Special) BOG Meeting / NIT Agartala
Through Circulation / NIT Agartala

NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AGARTALA
WEST TRIPURA, INDIA
Dated: 24.05.2022

Minutes of the 57th-A (Special) Meeting of the Board of Governors.

The 57th-A (Special) meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was convened through circulation vide Notification No. NITAgartala/EC(Acad)/2022/GoG/05 dated: 24.05.2022. The meeting was convened through circulation.

The following members accorded their consent to the agenda:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Chairperson of BoG and Director, NIT Agartala.
(2) Mrs. Saumya Gupta, Joint Secretary (NITs), MoE, Govt. of India.
(3) Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral, JS & FA, IFD, MoE, Govt. of India.
(4) Mr. Brijesh Pandey, Secretary, Dept. of Higher Education, Govt. of Tripura.
(5) Prof. (Dr.) T.G. Sitharam, Director, IIT Guwahati.
(6) Prof. A.K. Chakrabarti, Professor, Electrical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala.
(7) Dr. Dipak Kr. Das, Registrar (i/c), NIT Agartala.
(8) Dr. (Mrs.) Sumita Deb, Assistant Professor, Electrical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala.
(9) Prof. (Dr.) D. Bhattacharjee, Professor and Registrar (I/C), NIT Agartala.

Item No. 57.1-A: To consider signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India & the National Institute of Technology, Agartala for the year 2022-23 as per requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Board decided to approve the matter related to the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Govt. of India and National Institute of Technology Agartala for signing as per requirement of GFR 229 (xi) for the year 2022-23 as per suggestions received from MoE, Govt. of India, Department of Higher Education, IFD vide letter F. No. 3-9/2021-IFD (Pt.), dated: 01.04.2022.

(Prof. (Dr.) D. Bhattacharjee) (Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma)
Registrar (I/C), NIT Agartala Chairperson of BoG and Director,
NIT Agartala
