NOTICE FOR ENGAGEMENT ON CONTRACT BASIS

NIT Agartala intends to fill up the following post on a Contract basis through an interview, as per details below:

1. Name of the Post: Estate Engineer
   Maximum Age: Upto 62 years as on 30-06-2024.
   Monthly Remuneration: Rs. 50,000/- per month (consolidated). Remuneration will not be a constraint for a deserving candidate. TA/DA will be admissible as per rules for official tour/visit.
   Place of Posting: Agartala
   Job profile:
     i) Responsible for maintaining/managing/developing/providing technical support for campus physical infrastructure.
     ii) Implementation and follow up action on the policy matters of the institute.
     iii) Any other task(s) as assigned by the authorities of the institute from time to time.
   Contract Tenure: The contract will initially be for a period of one year and may be considered for further extension up to 3 terms, subject to the requirement of the institute and satisfactory work performance. The original term & renewal together is limited to 3 years.
   Qualification & Experience: 1st Class Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering/Electrical Engineering.
   Desirable: Five years relevant experience working with CPWD norms and practices in the following areas:
   Planning, Design, estimating, development of specifications, preparation and submission of complete tender process, quality control and monitoring of works, verification of contractor's bills etc. Campus infrastructure planning and execution of works, good communication and writing skills.
   A Master's Degree in the relevant experience areas will be considered as a value addition.

2. No. of Post: 01 (One)

3. Eligible candidates interested in appearing for the interview should send their CV/application in the prescribed format available on the institute website, along with self-attested certificates in a Single PDF for proof of education/experience/age etc., via email to establishment@nita.ac.in, latest by 04:00 PM of 16-09-2024.

   (Shortlisted candidates will be informed by return email indicating the tentative date & time to attend the interview.)

4. The appointment against the above-mentioned position shall be purely on a contract basis on a fixed pay for a fixed duration, initially for a period of one year and may be considered for further extension up to 3 terms, subject to the requirement of the institute and satisfactory work performance. The original term & renewal together is limited to 3 years.
