**NOTICE**

The National Institute of Technology Agartala and the Indian Institute of Information Technology Agartala will celebrate the 77th Independence Day on 15th August 2024. A March Past by the students is one of the main events to honor the day.

All undergraduate (UG) students, both boys and girls, of NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala are requested to report to the Sports Complex from 6th August 2024 at 5:00 PM for march past practice. All Gymkhana members should coordinate the students. Students are directed to attend the practice sessions sincerely.
