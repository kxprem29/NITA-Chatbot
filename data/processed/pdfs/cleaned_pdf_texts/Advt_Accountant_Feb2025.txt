**NOTICE FOR ENGAGEMENT ON CONTRACT BASIS**

Applications are invited for engagement on a contractual basis for the post of Accountant (Contractual).

1.  **Name of the Post:** Accountant (Contractual)

    *   **Maximum Age:** Retired persons up to 62 years of age as on 31-12-2024.
    *   **Monthly Remuneration:** Rs. 25,000/- per month (consolidated).
    *   **TA/DA:** For official tour/visit, TA/DA will be admissible as per rules.
    *   **Place of Posting:** NIT Agartala
    *   **Contract Tenure:** The contract will initially be for a period of one year and may be considered for further extension up to 3 terms, subject to the institute's requirements and satisfactory work performance. The original term and renewals together are limited to 3 years.

    **Qualification:**

    *   **Essential:** Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from a recognized University/Institute and proficiency in Excel, Computer word processing & spreadsheets.
    *   **Desirable:** English proficiency in speaking & writing. Other computer skills.
    *   **Experience:** Persons with Accounts-cum-administrative training, holding analogous posts in Central/State Govt. /Autonomous bodies/ PSU/Central/State Universities for at least a period of 5 years. Retired persons from Govt./PSU/Autonomous bodies/ PSU/Central/State Universities holding similar nature of work will be preferred.

2.  **No. of Posts:** 02 (two). The number of vacancies may increase/decrease if required.

Eligible candidates interested in appearing for the interview should send their application addressed to the Registrar, NIT Agartala, mentioning the following: (1) Post applied for, (2) Name (in Block letters), (3) Father’s/Husband’s Name, (4) Present & Permanent address, (5) Date of birth and Age (as on 31-12-2024), (6) Email address, Mobile No., (7) Whether SC/ST/OBC/PH/Ex-S, (8) Educational qualification, (9) Experience (if any), (10) Category, (11) Other Information (if any), (12) Whether all the terms & conditions of the advertisement are agreed upon or not (YES/NO). This information, along with self-attested photocopies of all certificates for proof of education/experience/age etc., should be sent in a single PDF file via email to establishment@nita.ac.in latest by 25-02-2025.

Shortlisted candidates will be informed by return email, indicating the tentative date & time to attend the interview. The shortlisted candidates are required to bring a copy of the filled-in application form, a recent passport-size photograph, all original documents, along with a set of self-attested copies of the documents, at the time of interview for verification.

For updates (if any), please visit the institute website http://www.nita.ac.in

The original term and renewals together are limited to 3 years.
