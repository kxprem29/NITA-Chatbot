**Special Eligibility Requirements for all Departments:**

GEN/GEN-EWS/OBC category candidates must obtain 60% marks or 6.5 CGPA (on a 10-point scale) in the subject at the B.Sc. level, and 55% marks or 6.0 CGPA (on a 10-point scale) in the subject at the B.Sc. level for SC/ST/PwD category candidates.

**Special Eligibility Criteria:**

*   **M.Sc. in Chemistry:** Chemistry as a subject in all the years/semesters in B.Sc. The duration of the B.Sc. program should not be less than 3 (three) years.

*   **M.Sc. in Mathematics & Computing:** Mathematics as a subject in all the years/semesters in B.Sc. The duration of the B.Sc. program should not be less than 3 (three) years.

*   **M.Sc. in Physics:** Physics as a subject in all the years/semesters in B.Sc. The duration of the B.Sc. program should not be less than 3 (three) years.

**B. Other Eligibility Requirements:**

1.  Candidates seeking admission to more than one Department will be required to submit separate applications upon payment of the requisite fee.
2.  Conversion from CGPA to percentage or vice-versa given by individual Institute/University will not be considered/allowed. In case both CGPA and percentage are mentioned, only CGPA would be considered.
3.  In case the result of the qualifying degree is awaited, provisional admission is permitted to a student subject to meeting the above minimum qualifying criteria and special eligibility criteria latest by September 30, 2024. In these cases, all exams should have been completed by August 15, 2024. A documentary proof from the Head of the current Institute to that effect in the prescribed format and Mark sheet/ Grade card till the pre-final semester should be uploaded at the time of application. A self-declaration by appearing candidates in the prescribed format should also be uploaded.
4.  An undertaking in the prescribed format should be uploaded by all categories of candidates during application.

**Important Note:**

*   All the provisionally selected candidates must fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria as mentioned in section A of this notice. If any candidate is unable to qualify for the minimum eligibility criterion, as per this notification for admission to the M.Sc. Programme but considered inadvertently, then his/her candidature will be canceled at a later stage if found ineligible.

*   Migration Certificate and Conduct/Character Certificate are mandatory during provisional admission.
