**Notice Regarding Credit Transfer**

This is to inform all students of NIT and HIT Agartala that they are required to register on both the ABC and NAD portals through the following links: https://www.abc.gov.in/ and https://nad.digilocker.gov.in/, respectively. This registration is necessary to generate their ABC and NAD IDs, which must then be shared using the form accessible via the following link or by scanning the QR code provided:

https://forms.gle/IQV686XqzaLTvTsB9

Once the IDs are updated in the MIS, students can apply for credit transfer through the ABC portal.

All credit transfer requests from students should be submitted using the attached form. Upon submission of the form, duly endorsed by the HoDs, the Credit Transfer Request will be executed.

Students cannot opt for credit transfer of more than 10% of the total credits required for their course.

Students with backlogs are not eligible to apply for credit transfer.

Students must maintain the required attendance for the courses they have opted for in the host institute.
