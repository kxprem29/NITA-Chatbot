**Minimum Qualification for admission to Ph.D programme:**

A) Candidates for admission to the Ph.D programme in Engineering Departments must satisfy one of the following criteria:

I. Master's Degree in Engineering/ Technology or equivalent in an appropriate area with a minimum CGPA of 6.5 out of 10-point grading system or equivalent 60% of marks, from a recognized Institute or University.

II. B.E/ B.Tech/ B.Pharm or similar discipline graduates, from an Institute/ University with average NIRF ranking within 100 (Engineering category) over the last 3 years, and Institutes of National Importance, with an academic record of 70% or 7.5 CGPA in a 10-point scale, can be considered for direct admission to the PhD programme. Such candidates must have a valid GATE/ NET score card tenable for the year of admission.

III. Master's Degree in relevant science discipline with a good academic record and of exceptional merit are eligible for the relevant engineering discipline with a minimum CGPA of 6.5 out of 10-point grading system or more or at least 60% of marks, from a recognized Institute or University. However, for availing scholarship from MHRD (now MoE), a GATE score is mandatory.

**Categories of Scholars under Section- A**

1.  **Full-time with scholarship:** Candidates under this category will be eligible for fellowship as per MHRD (now MoE) Institute norms. A GATE score is a mandatory criterion for this category of scholars for making themselves eligible for receiving a scholarship during the tenure of the Ph.D.

    Fellowship will be awarded to the scholars who are admitted under this category subject to availability as stipulated by MHRD (now MoE). The award and/ or renewal of the fellowship will be as per the guidelines issued by MoE and the Institute, from time to time.
2.  **Full time self-financed scholars:** Candidate selected for admission under this category will not be entitled to any scholarship from the Institute. A GATE score is not a mandatory criterion for this category. However, eligibility norms are essential as per section A(1) of this notification.

B) Admission to the Ph.D programme in Science Departments must satisfy one of the following criteria:

I. Master's degree in Sciences with a good academic record and with a minimum of 65% marks (or equivalent or 6.5 CGPA in a 10-point grading system) or 60% of marks (6.0 CGPA out of 10-point grading system) with a GATE score or UGC/CSIR-NET/NBHM tenable for the year of admission. Preference will be given to GATE/NET qualified candidates.

II. Master's degree in Engineering/Technology or equivalent is eligible with a good academic record with a minimum CGPA of 6.5 out of 10-point grading system or equivalent or 65% of marks. Preference will be given to GATE-qualified candidates.

III. BE/ B.Tech. B.Pharm or similar discipline graduates, from an Institute/ University with average NIRF ranking within 100 (Engineering category) over the last 3 years, and Institutes of

C) Admission to the Ph.D programme in Humanities & Social Sciences under the Department of Management, Humanities & Social Sciences:

Scholars for admission to the Ph.D. programme in Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS) must have a Master's degree in a relevant discipline with a minimum of 60% marks or equivalent or 6.5 CGPA in a 10-point grading system or 55% or 6.0 CGPA in a 10-point scale with a UGC/CSIR-NET/NBHM tenable for the year of admission. The relevant discipline of research will include English; Economics; Commerce; Finance; Foreign Trade; Business Management; Geography; Rural Management & Development; Sociology and others as per the requirement of the Department, which will be notified from time to time. Preference will be given to NET-qualified candidates.

D) Admission to the Ph.D programme in Management under the Department of Management, Humanities & Social Sciences:

MBA/P.G. Diploma in Management of 2-year duration with 60% marks or a CGPA of 6.5 in a 10-point scale. Preference will be given to GATE / NET qualified candidates.

Candidates admitted to the MHSS Department for the Ph.D programme under the Full-time with scholarship category will be eligible for an Institute scholarship, based on availability and decisions of the competent authority from time to time. The eligibility condition for Scholarship/ Fellowship for candidates having an MBA degree is 70% marks. The award and/ or renewal of the fellowship will be as per the guidelines issued from time to time.

E) Institute Employees/Research Scholars under External Registration/ Research scholars under the part-time category:

For Research Scholars of these categories, the minimum educational qualifications are the same as prescribed in A to D above for admission to the Ph.D. programme in the respective categories. However, a GATE score or CSIR/ UGC JRF or Lectureship/ NBHM/ JMET/ CAT/ AIMA or equivalent qualification as applicable for regular full-time research scholars may not be required in these cases.

F) Admission to the Ph.D programme for foreign scholars:

Foreign nationals can only register as regular full-time scholars. Foreign nationals with degrees from Indian Universities will be treated at par with Indian nationals for admission purposes. Foreign nationals with foreign degrees must meet the minimum educational requirements as given in A to D above and equivalent to an Indian Master's degree in the relevant disciplines. In addition, they should have a valid GRE/ GMAT /GATE /JMET/ CAT/ XAT/ MAT/ ATMA/ UGC or CSIR/NET and a score in TOEFL/ IELTS or equivalent examination. This category of scholars are not eligible for any financial assistance from the Institute during the entire tenure of the Ph.D programme.

**Selection Procedure:**

On submission of the online application, the candidates fulfilling eligibility conditions as per section A to D may be provisionally short-listed by the respective departments. The provisionally short-listed candidates will be required to appear for an online test (30 marks) to be conducted by the Institute. The test will comprise MCQ-type questions, covering the UG and PG levels (specialization wise at PG level). The candidates qualifying the test will be allowed to appear for an online interview. Provisional selection for admission to the Ph.D programme may be made strictly on the basis of the academic performance of the candidates, publication, and availability of seats.

*   GATE/NET qualified candidates may be exempted from the written test.
*   Admission and Academic fees will be displayed on the Institute website at the appropriate time.
*   For all hostel-related matters, candidates may contact the Office of the Chief Warden.
