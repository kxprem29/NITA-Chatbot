**Advertisement for Junior Research Fellow**

Principal Investigator (PI): Dr. Paramvir Singh, Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, NIT Agartala.

The interview will be held on April 4, 2023, at 11:00 am in online mode.
