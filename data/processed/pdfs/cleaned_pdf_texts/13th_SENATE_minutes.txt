Item No. 5/13/14: Review of existing programs at UG and PG Levels.

After review of all existing programmes at UG and PG levels the SENATE decided as follows:

BS-MS and BT-MT started in the Institute from the year 2013-14 would be allowed to continue for 1(one) year. After that the matter would be reviewed and an appropriate decision on the continuance of the programmes would be taken.

All the 4 (four) schools opened for running M.Tech and Ph.D programme would be merged immediately "i.e. before the starting of next academic session" with the Department as indicated against each below:

School of Hydro Informatics - Civil Engineering
School of Seismic Science & Engineering - Civil Engineering
School of Automotive Engineering - Mechanical Engineering
School of Energy Studies - Electrical Engineering

The matter regarding running of some specializations in M.Tech courses would be reviewed considering the infrastructure, manpower availability and the number of students admitted, as per decision of 28th BoG meeting.

One faculty of Hydro Informatics Department, Dr. Mrinmoy Majumder, Assistant Professor would now onwards work in the Civil Engineering Department and his designation would be Assistant Professor in Civil Engineering Department.
