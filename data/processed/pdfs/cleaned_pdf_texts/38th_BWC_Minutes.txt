Minutes of the 38th Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on March 15, 2017

The 38th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on March 15, 2017, at 6:00 pm in the Conference Room of the NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

* Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director, N.I.T., Agartala: Chairman, B&WC, NIT, Agartala
* Sri Asit Bhaumik, TES Gr-I, Chief Engineer / Representative of PWD (Civil), Govt. of Tripura: Member
* Sri Kajal Banik, Superintending Engineer (Elect.), Office of the Chief Engineer/ Representative of PWD, Govt. of Tripura: Member
* Dr. Ratul Das, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala: Member
* Dr. Richi Prasad Sharma, Professor, Civil Engg. Department, NIT Agartala: Member
* Dr. Debasish Bhattacharya, Registrar (I/C), N.I.T., Agartala: Member Secretary

The Director (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India, could not attend the meeting.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and requested the Registrar (I/C) and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

Item No. 38.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 37th BWC meeting held on 6th September, 2016.

The minutes of the 37th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on September 6, 2016, in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura, were circulated to all the members. The Building & Works Committee also confirmed the modified minutes of the 37th Building & Works Committee meeting, incorporating the suggestion under agenda item No. 35.2(6) (Sl. No. 37.2(6)) to award the work to the CPWD as a deposit work, canceling the earlier decision regarding the necessity of a comparative study with other swimming pools. The Minutes of the 37th Building & Works Committee of NIT, Agartala, were confirmed.
