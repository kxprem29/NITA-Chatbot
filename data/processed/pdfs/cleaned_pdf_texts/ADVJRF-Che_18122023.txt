**Recruitment of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) for the research project funded by NTFP-NCE, Govt. of Tripura**

Applications are invited for the post of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) under the sponsored research project. The details of the project and the post for recruitment are given below:

Title of the Project: Microbial assessment for developing inoculums for artificially infecting agarwood tree for sustainable agar cultivation in Tripura and the north-east region

Funding Agency: NTFP-NCE, Govt. of Tripura

Project duration: 04 years

Principal Investigator: Dr. Srimanta Ray, Dept. of Chemical Engg.

Emolument:

*   (for candidate without NET/GATE qualification or equivalent): INR 15,500/- Year 1&2 and INR 17,500/- Year 3 onwards
*   (for candidate with NET/GATE qualification or equivalent): INR 31,000/- Year 1&2 and INR 35,000/- Year 3 onwards

Essential Qualifications: First Class Master's Degree in Life Science/ Biotechnology/ Microbiology/ Botany/ Biochemistry or any other allied subjects with hands-on skill/knowledge of Microbial isolation and characterization and Molecular Biology/ Biotechnological Techniques.

Desirable Qualifications:

1.  Candidates with experience in skill/knowledge related to plant biotechnology/culture will be preferred.
2.  Preference will be given to applicants with publications in Scopus/SCI indexed journals in the related field.
3.  Preference will be given to applicants with experience in the related field.
4.  Candidates with NET/GATE/Equivalent qualification will be preferred.
5.  Willingness for field visits and travelling, if needed.

Description of responsibilities: Developing inoculums for artificially infecting agarwood tree for agar cultivation.

Date of Interview: 15.01.2024

Mode: Online (Reporting Time and Link will be informed by email)

Selection Process: Presentation followed by Interview

Venue: NIT Agartala, National Institute of Technology Agartala

**Terms and Conditions:**

1.  The position is purely on a temporary basis and will be governed by the Administrative rules and conditions imposed by the Research & Consultancy Cell, NIT Agartala.
2.  Candidates, before appearing for the interview, shall ensure that they are eligible for the position they intend to apply for.
3.  Interested candidates should send their (i) filled application including photograph and signature, (ii) annexures referred to in the application, (iii) scanned copies of educational qualifications, (iv) research publications, (v) experience, (vi) skills acquired related to the advertisement, and (vii) statement of interest for the position latest by 10.01.2024. Applications received on or after the due date but till the date of interview may also be considered, if the application is found suitable for the project by the PI.
4.  The application should be sent (as a single PDF) to Dr. Srimanta Ray, Department of Chemical Engineering, National Institute of Technology (NIT), Agartala, Tripura, India through e-mail at srimanta.chemical@nita.ac.in
5.  No TA/DA is permissible for appearing in the interview and joining in case of selection. The Institute reserves the right to cancel the process at any point of time without assigning any reason.
6.  Candidates appearing through online mode shall bring all documents submitted online, along with the original degree(s)/certificate(s) and experience certificate(s) at the time of joining for verification.
7.  Only shortlisted candidates will be informed through email provided in the application form for the interview, upon selection engagement letter will be sent through the email.
8.  The applicant will be responsible for the authenticity of information, documents, and photographs submitted.
9.  The Institute reserves the right to cancel the recruitment without assigning any reason.
10. Appointment will be temporary in nature and as per the Institute terms and conditions. The continuation of appointment will be based on satisfactory annual performance appraisal. The temporary engagement can be terminated without assigning any reason.
11. The position is co-terminus with the project duration. In the event that the JRF wishes to leave before completing the tenure, he/she will be required to submit at least two months' notice from the planned date of leaving the project.
12. The candidate selected for the above position will have the option to register for the PhD program at the Department of Chemical Engineering, National Institute of Technology (NIT) Agartala.
