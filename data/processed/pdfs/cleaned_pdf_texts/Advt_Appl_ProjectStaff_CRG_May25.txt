**Recruitment of Project Assistant/Junior Research Fellow (JRF)/Senior Research Fellow (SRF)/Research Associate (RA) for Research Project Funded by SERB-DST, Govt. of India, under the Core Research Grant (CRG) Scheme**

Applications are invited for the post of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) under the sponsored research project. The details of the project and the post for recruitment are given below:

**Title of the Project:** Development and Assessment of Seismic Performance of Affordable Earthquake Resistant Interlocking Block for Earthen Masonry Houses

**Funding Agency:** SERB-DST, Govt. of India, under the Core Research Grant (CRG) Scheme

**Project Duration:** 3 Years (1 year completed)

**Principal Investigator:** Prof. (Dr.) Rajib Saha / Dr. Manu S Nadesan (Co-PI)

**Monthly Emolument:** Rs. 37,000 per month for the first two years and Rs. 42,000 for the third year

**Essential Qualifications:** Post Graduate Degree in a relevant Professional Course (Civil Engineering, Construction Engineering, Civil & Environmental Engineering, Applied Mechanics, Material Science & Engineering, and allied disciplines) selected through a process described by any one of the following:

A. Scholars selected through National Eligibility Tests – CSIR-UGC NET including Lectureship (Assistant Professorship) and GATE.

B. Selection process through national-level examinations conducted by Central Government Departments and their Agencies and Institutions such as DST, DBT, DAE, DOS, DRDO, MoE, MHRD, ICAR, ICMR, IIT, IISc, IISER, etc.

(As per DST Office Memorandum: DST/PCPM/Z-06/2022 dated 26th June, 2023)

**Description of Responsibilities:**  Carry out research work on the research project “Development and assessment of seismic performance of affordable earthquake-resistant interlocking block for earthen masonry houses,” as directed by the members of the Project Implementation Committee (PIC) and Project Review Committee (PRC). Maintain records of results in presentable form (soft and hard copy).  Track contingencies/consumables required for the work. Assist the PI in preparing various reports related to the research work. Carry out any other departmental work as directed by the PI.

**Date of Interview:** 26-05-2024 (Monday) at 11:00 A.M. in the Department of Civil Engineering, NIT Agartala (Offline Mode).
