NOTICE

This is to inform all concerned that the 17th Convocation of the National Institute of Technology Agartala will be held on 18th January, 2025 (Saturday) at NIT Agartala, to confer degrees upon the graduates of various programmes (UG, Dual Degree, PG, and Ph.D) for the Academic Year 2024-25.

A-1. Online Registration

1. Online registration is mandatory for all degree recipients intending to participate in the Convocation ceremony.
2. Graduates are required to log on to the online registration portal: https://mis.nita.ac.in. The registration portal will be active from 02/12/2024 to 02/01/2025.
3. A DigiLocker ID must be provided during registration. Individuals without a DigiLocker ID are required to register on the DigiLocker portal first. No registration fee is required, as the fee has already been paid.

A-2. Dress Code:

1. Please note that the following dress code must be strictly adhered to by all degree recipients on the day of the Convocation. Failure to comply will result in denial of entry to the Convocation area.

Boys: White Kurta Pajama, preferably cotton.
Girls: Off-white Salwar Suit, preferably cotton.

A-3. Help Desk:

For any technical issues during online registration, please contact Mr. Pramathesh Banik at nitmisagt@gmail.com or by phone at 0381-2548537, between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM (on working days).

All are advised to visit the Institute website regularly for further updates related to the Convocation.
