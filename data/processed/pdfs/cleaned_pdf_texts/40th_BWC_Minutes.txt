NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, AGARTALA

Minutes of the 40th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on March 27, 2018

The 40th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on March 27, 2018, at 10:30 AM in the Conference Room of Shastri Bhavan, New Delhi, India.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

* Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Chairman, B&WC, Director, N.I.T., Agartala.
* Dr. S. C. Saha, Professor, Mechanical Engg. Department, NIT Agartala - Member
* Mr. K Rajan, Under Secretary, Higher Education, MHRD, Govt. of India - Member
* Mr. D.K. Singh, Under Secretary, Higher Education, MHRD, Govt. of India - Member
* Mr. A. K. Singh, Under Secretary, Higher Education, MHRD, Govt. of India - Invitee Member
* Dr. Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala - Member
* Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar, N.I.T., Agartala - Member Secretary

Shri Asit Bhaumik, Chief Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura, and Shri Kajal Banik, Superintending Engineer (Electrical), Govt. of Tripura, could not attend the meeting.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and invited the Registrar and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

Item No. 40.3: To inform the status of the various projects which were already approved by the Chairman BWC and subsequently ratified by the 38th BWC against agenda item 38.3.

The BWC noted the status of work in respect of item no. 38.3 (Sl. No. 1 to 12) of the 38th BWC meeting.

Item No. 40.4: To inform the status of the various projects which were already approved by the Chairman BWC and subsequently approved by the 38th BWC against agenda item 38.4 to 38.8.

The BWC noted the status of work in respect of Item no. 38.4 to 38.8 (Sl. No. 1 to 9) of the 38th BWC meeting.

Item No. 40.5: To consider the estimate for the work of “Construction of boundary wall for KV field along with drain at NITA” at a cost of Rs. 26,72,992.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal and recommended placing the proposal in the next FC and BoG meeting for necessary administrative approval and expenditure sanction.

Item No. 40.6: To consider the estimate for the work of “Providing and fixing of aluminum partition wall in 2nd Floor (HSS Dept.) and 3rd Floor (SOM Dept.) of Central Library Building and a separate approach for the building” at NITA, at a cost of Rs. 18,07,669.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal.

Item No. 40.7: To consider the estimate for the work of “Providing and fixing of a partition wall at different locations of Civil Engineering Dept. (SOM Building) at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 36,06,559.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal and recommended placing the proposal in the next FC and BoG meeting for necessary administrative approval and expenditure sanction.

Item No. 40.8: To consider the estimate for the work of “Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of internal electrification works in the School of Management building under Civil Engineering Department at NIT Agartala complex” at a cost of Rs. 9,28,345.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal.

Item No. 40.9: To consider the estimate for the work of “Construction of approach road for workshop building and CE lab in front of ECE building at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 6,28,136.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal.

Item No. 40.10: To consider the estimate for the work of “Extension of medical unit at Raima Hostel, NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 12,13,006.00/Balance work.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal.

Item No. 40.11: To consider the estimate for the work of “providing a parking area and approach for the Mathematics Department at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 8,73,784.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal.

Item No. 40.12: To consider the extra item for the work “1500 boys hostel at NIT Agartala.”

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal of extra items for 1500 capacity boys hostel amounting to Rs. 7,22,37,000 for completion of the fire fighting works.

Item No. 40.13: To consider the estimate for the work of “Construction of 100 Capacity Research Scholar Hostel at NIT Agartala (G+6)” at a cost of Rs. 25,00,00,000.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal and recommended placing the proposal in the next FC and BoG meeting for necessary administrative approval and expenditure sanction subject to the Budget provision in FY 2018-19.

Item No. 40.14: To consider the estimate for the work of “Construction of 1500 Capacity open air theatre at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 17,00,00,000.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal and recommended placing the proposal in the next FC and BoG meeting for necessary administrative approval and expenditure sanction subject to the Budget provision in FY 2018-19.

Item No. 40.15: To consider the proposal of Rainwater harvesting facilities at NIT Agartala.

The Building & Works Committee recommended deferring the project for the time being.

Item No. 40.16: To consider for administrative approval and expenditure sanction of the estimate for the work of “Extension of LT Over Head line for H3 Residential Quarters complex at NIT Agartala campus” at a cost of Rs. 3,93,675.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
