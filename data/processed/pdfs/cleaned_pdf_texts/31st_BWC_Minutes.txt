Minutes of the 31st Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on February 07, 2014

The 31st meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on February 07, 2014 (Friday) at 10:30 am in the Conference Room of the NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

* Prof. Gopal Mugeraya: Chairman of B&WC, Director, NIT Agartala
* Shri Asit Bhaumik: Member, Addl. Chief Engineer, PWD (R & D) (Representative of PWD, Govt. of Tripura)
* Shri Kajal Banik: Member, Executive Engineer (Elect.), Electrical Inspectorate (Representative of PWD, Govt. of Tripura)
* Dr. Ajoy Kr. Chakraborty: Member, Dean (P&D), NIT Agartala
* Dr. R. P. Sharma: Member, Professor, NIT Agartala
* Dr. S. K. Pal: Invitee, Advisor to the Director, Civil Engg. & Construction Works.
* Shri Sumanta Chakrabarti: Member Secretary, Registrar (I/C), NIT, Agartala

The representative of MHRD could not attend the meeting.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and requested the Registrar (I/C) and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

Item No. 31.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 30th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on December 09, 2013.

The minutes of the 30th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on December 09, 2013, in the Conference Room of NIT Agartala (Transit House) were circulated to all the members, and no modifications, suggestions, or comments were received. The minutes of the 30th Building Works Committee of NIT Agartala were confirmed.

Item No. 31.2: Action taken on the decision of the 30th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on December 09, 2013, in the Conference Room of NIT Agartala.

The Building & Works Committee noted the action taken report.

It was informed to the BWC that, as suggested by Mr. Rajesh Singh, Director NITs, in the 30th BWC meeting held on 09/12/2013, a decision was taken to include one member from CPWD Agartala in the BWC of NIT Agartala. Accordingly, NIT Agartala invited the Chief Engineer, CPWD Agartala, to be a member of the BWC of NIT Agartala and requested them to attend the 31st BWC Meeting to be held on 07/02/2014. In response, the CPWD conveyed that, as a policy decision of the Directorate, officers of CPWD would not be participating as members of the BWC Meetings for works not executed by the CPWD and expressed their inability to depute any CPWD Officer to attend the BWC Meeting at NIT Agartala, which was communicated by Mr. M. Balachandran, Superintending Engineer (P cum ACC) office of the Chief Engineer, NEZ-II, GPRA Complex, Gandhigram, Agartala, vide letter no. 23(6)(1)/NEZ-11/2014/109, dated 28/01/2014.

Item No. 31.3: To consider the award of the work "Construction of 1500 Capacity Boys’ Hostel at NIT Agartala" to the lowest qualified bidder, M/S NCC Limited, at a cost of Rs. 1,52,42,54,361.00 (Rupees One Hundred Fifty-Two Crore Forty-Two Lakh Fifty-Four Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty-One only).

With the recommendation of the BWC in its 29th Meeting (item No. 29.9) held on June 17, 2013, in the 22nd Meeting (item No. 22.8) of the FC held on June 18, 2013, and the 26th Meeting (item No. 26.16) of the BOG held on June 18, 2013, a tender for the work "Construction of 1500 Capacity Boys’ Hostel at NIT Agartala" was invited vide no. F. No. 23(101)/NITA/Estate/2012-13/5019-23, dated 12/08/2013, but subsequently, the tender was cancelled due to administrative reasons.

The tender was again published vide no. F. No. 23(101)/NITA/Estate/2012-13/9446-50, dated 20/12/2013, in 7 (seven) all India and local newspapers, namely: (1) Dainik Sambad, (2) Syandan Patrika, (3) Daily Deshar Katha, (4) Tripura Times, (5) Assam Tribune, (6) Indian Express (All Editions), and (7) The Telegraph (All Editions) on 23/12/2013, as well as on the website http://www.tenders.gov.in. The last date for receiving and opening the tender was 21.01.2014. In response, three bids were received from (1) M/S Larsen & Toubro Limited, (2) M/S Simplex Infrastructures Limited, and (3) M/S NCC Limited. A tender opening, evaluation, & recommendation committee was constituted vide no. F.No.23(101)/NITA/Estate/2012-13/12016-27, dtd. 20/01/2014, comprising the following members: (1) Dr. Sujit Kumar Pal, Advisor to the Director, Civil Engineering & Construction Work, (2) Dr. Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty, Dean (P&D), (3) Dr. Rup Narayan Ray, Associate Professor, Elect. Engg. Dept., (4) Dr. Manish Pal, Associate Professor, Civil Engg. Dept., (5) Dr. Sima Ghosh, Associate Dean (P&D), (6) Mr. Gopi Nandan Dey, Assistant Professor, Civil Engg. Dept., (7) Mr. Abhik Majumder, Assistant Professor, Mech. Engg. Dept., (8) Sri. Subhendu Chaudhuri, Estate Engineer, (9) Sri. Asit Baran Bhattacharya, (Accounts Officer), and (10) Sri. Paresh Ch. Das, Estate Engineer (Civil). The committee opened the technical bids on 21/01/2014 at 3:30 pm and found all the bids technically qualified. After obtaining approval from the competent authority, the price bids were opened on 27/01/2014 at 3:00 pm. A comparative statement has been prepared. The rates (without service tax) offered by (1) M/S Larsen & Toubro Limited is Rs. 204,60,40,705.00 (Rupees Two Hundred Four Crore Sixty Lakh Forty Thousand Seven Hundred and Five only) (76.39% above the estimated cost), (2) M/S Simplex Infrastructures Limited is Rs. 196,88,46,422.00 (Rupees One Hundred Ninety-Six Crore Eighty-Eight Lakh Forty-Six Thousand Four Hundred and Twenty-Two only) (69.73% above the estimated cost), and (3) M/S NCC Limited (L1) is Rs. 152,42,54,361.00 (Rupees One Hundred Fifty-Two Crore Forty-Two Lakh Fifty-Four Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty-One only) (31.40% above the estimated cost). The bid of M/S NCC Limited has been found to be the lowest at a cost of Rs. 152,42,54,361.00 (Rupees One Hundred Fifty-Two Crore Forty-Two Lakh

Fifty-Four Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty-One only) at 31.40% above the estimated cost (put to tender).

The rates quoted by the lowest bidder have been analyzed for justification by the Estate Section and found to be reasonable, also in consideration of rates for similar nature of works received in the near past. The tender opening, evaluation & recommendation committee scrutinized all the bids and recommended the lowest bid of M/S NCC Limited at a cost of Rs. 1,52,42,54,361.00 (Rupees One Hundred Fifty-Two Crore Forty-Two Lakh Fifty-Four Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty-One only) for the award of work.

After thorough discussion on rate justification and considering all aspects of trends on price escalation on major construction materials and labor, the BWC arrived at a decision to award the work to M/S NCC Limited at a cost of Rs. 1,52,42,54,361.00 (Rupees One Hundred Fifty-Two Crore Forty-Two Lakh Fifty-Four Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty-One only).

Item No. 31.4: To consider approval of the estimate for the work "Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of 15 (fifteen) lifts in Administrative Building 1 & 2, School of Management, International Guest House, Academic Block (ECE Deptt. & DoNER), Central Library, and Director’s Bungalow at NIT Agartala" at a cost of Rs. 2,86,51,140.00.

The detailed estimate submitted by the Estate Engineer for the work "Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of 15 (fifteen) lifts in Administrative Building 1 & 2, School of Management, International Guest House, Academic Block (ECE Deptt. & DoNER), Central Library, and Director's Bungalow at NIT Agartala" at a cost of Rs. 2,86,51,140.00 has been examined by the Building & Works Committee.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal with a total estimated cost of Rs. 2,86,51,140.00 (Rupees Two Crore Eighty-Six Lakh Fifty-One Thousand One Hundred and Forty Only) for inviting tenders and decided that the makes should be restricted to 5 (five) vendors, namely (1) OTIS, (2) Thyssen Krupp, (3) Johnson, (4) Kone, and (5) Mitsubishi.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the chair.
