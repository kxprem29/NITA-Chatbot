**NOTIFICATION**

**Subject: Admission to Kokborok (Tripura/Tipra) Certificate Course for the Academic Session 2024-2025**

Applications are invited from eligible candidates for admission to the Kokborok (Tripura/Tipra) Certificate Course (2 semesters) for the Academic Session 2024-2025 at NIT Agartala.

1.  The intake capacity is 50 students, primarily comprising teaching and non-teaching staff, and students of NIT Agartala. However, external students may be admitted if seats remain vacant.

2.  Admission will be granted to students meeting the requisite qualifications on a first-come, first-served basis, according to merit.

3.  Applications will be received from 05.07.2024 to 25.07.2024. The Application Form is provided in Annexure – I and must be submitted to the office of the undersigned. Selected applicants will be notified on the institute website as soon as possible. Selection, Admission, and Registration processes will be completed between 26.07.2024 and 31.07.2024. Classes will commence from 03.08.2024 onwards.

4.  The admission fee for the course is Rs. 1000.00 (Rupees one thousand only) per semester (exempted for regular students of NIT Agartala). The fee should be paid in favor of “REGISTRAR, NIT AGARTALA” via a crossed Demand Draft payable at SBI, NIT Agartala Branch, OR deposited into the Institute A/c. No. 30369892838 through online transfer. The payment receipt must be submitted along with the application form.

**Minimum Educational Qualification for Admission to the Programme:**

1.  The minimum required educational qualification is H.S. (10+2) pass with at least 40% marks.

2.  Selection will be based on marks obtained in H.S. (10+2) and higher degrees, if applicable. An entrance exam may be conducted if required (for external applicants only).

3.  This notification is issued with the approval of the competent authority.
