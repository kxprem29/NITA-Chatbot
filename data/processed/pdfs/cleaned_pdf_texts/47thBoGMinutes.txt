The 47th meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on June 26, 2019 at 12 noon in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, New Delhi.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson of BoG and Director, NIT Agartala

(2) Smt. Darshna M. Dabral, JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India

(3) Shri K. Rayan, US, MHRD, Govt. of India
(Representative of the Special Secretary / Additional Secretary / Joint Secretary dealing with Technical Education, Dept. of Higher Education, MHRD)

(4) Prof. Sharad Gokhale, Dean of Infrastructure, Planning & Management, IIT Guwahati (nominee of the Director, IITG)

(5) Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha, Member, Professor, ME Dept., NIT Agartala

(6) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee, Member, Assoc. Professor, EE Dept., NIT Agartala

(7) Dr. Govind Bhargava, Secretary, Registrar, NIT, Agartala

Prof. Sanghamitra Bandyopadhyay, Director, Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, Prof. Vijayalakshmi Ravindranath, IISc, Bangalore, and The Principal Secretary to the Govt. of Tripura, Dept. of Higher Education, Agartala, could not attend the meeting.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Board of Governors, and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors.

The Chairperson invited the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 47.23: To consider the work of “Special Repair, Retrofitting and Upgradation of Dhalar Hostel building at NIT Agartala” for Rs. 93,94,302.00

Item No. 47.24: To consider the work of “Upgradation of 2-storey building besides Knowledge Centre at NIT Agartala” for Rs. 60,77,405.00

A built-up and incomplete structure is lying idle adjacent to the Knowledge Centre (library building) since the last few years. A committee was made to look at the efficacy of the structure and suggested its use. The committee recommended making use of the existing incomplete infrastructure for the laboratories/classrooms for the students. The committee also opined that the built-up infrastructure will be deteriorated and a good amount of money will be wasted if not taken into consideration on priority.

In view of the above, the Board accepted and confirmed the recommendations of the 33rd Finance Committee meeting vide Item No. 33.16.

Item No. 47.28: Absorption of Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar w.e.f. 05-05-2017 and counting of his Past Service for the purpose of Pension, Leave etc. as per CCS Rule 1972

A case with respect to this recruitment had been filed in the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura, Agartala. Therefore, the important facts with regard to the case will be gathered/collected and placed before the Board in the ensuing meeting. The Under Secretary (NIT), D/o Higher Education, MHRD, communicated that the appointment of the Registrar has not been carried out by the Institute as per the Recruitment Rules issued by MHRD/NITSER Council. There is no provision for the appointment of Registrar on a regular basis in the RRs and also absorption in the NITs. It may also be noted that in the First Statutes of NITs under Clause 21 (1), it is clearly stated that "The Registrar shall be appointed for a fixed term of not exceeding five years on deputation or contract basis."

Item No. 47.29: Any other issue with the permission of the Chair

Item No. 47.29.1: To consider the issues mentioned below pertaining to the ongoing TEQIP-III program at NIT Agartala

The Board approved and confirmed the second phase Procurement Plan under TEQIP-III as per TEQIP rules and also ratified and confirmed the Governance Self Review (GSR) for TEQIP-III.

Item No. 47.29.2: To consider the reallocation of Faculty positions, vacancies, and reservation at NIT Agartala

The Board approved and confirmed the report of the Committee regarding the reallocation of faculty positions. However, reservation is to be applied as per Govt. of India norms.

Item No. 47.29.3: To fill up the posts of Deputy Registrar and Assistant Registrar on deputation basis

The Board approved and confirmed the filling up of one post of Deputy Registrar on Deputation. The board also approved filling up two posts of Assistant Registrars on Deputation if the posts are not filled up in the recent ongoing recruitment process.
