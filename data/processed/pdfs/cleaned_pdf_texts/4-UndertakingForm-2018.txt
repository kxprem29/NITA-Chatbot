I, ____________________________ (name), son/daughter/wife of Shri _______________________, residing at _____________________________, have been admitted to the Ph.D. programme on _____________________ in the department of ____________________________ during the academic session ______________________. I am thankful to the institute for awarding me the Institute scholarship of Rs. __________ per month for Ph.D. in Engineering/ Technology / Science for the academic year _______, and I hereby undertake the following:

1.  I am not selected for any regular appointment/service and I am registering for the Ph.D. programme with the intention of completing it.

2.  I am not and will not be in receipt of any salary or other financial benefits from any source, except the MHRD/Institute Scholarship during the period of my study at the Institute.

3.  I will obtain prior permission from the institute before appearing in any examination conducted by other Institutions/Universities/Public bodies.

4.  I will not apply for or accept any job without obtaining prior permission from the Institute.

5.  I will undertake to dedicate 8 hours per week to teaching-related activities within the department, such as laboratory demonstrations, tutorials, evaluation of test papers, seminars, symposia, etc., and/or to academic research projects of the faculty members of the department, as assigned to me by the Head of the Department.

6.  I understand that the continuation of the scholarship for each semester is contingent on: (1) my satisfactory performance during the preceding semester(s) in the discharge of my responsibilities in teaching and research assignments; and (2) my satisfactory academic performance and progress during the preceding semester in my registered degree programme, as assessed by the doctoral committee, according to the procedure laid down by the Senate.

7.  I also understand that the scholarship will be terminated at any time if my performance in the assigned duties or the progress in my research work is judged to be unsatisfactory.
