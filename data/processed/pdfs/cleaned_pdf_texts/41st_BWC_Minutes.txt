Minutes of the 41st Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on September 04, 2018

The 41st meeting of the Building & Works Committee of National Institute of
Technology, Agartala was held on September 04, 2018 at 5:00 P.M. in the Board Room of
NIT Agartala Campus, Jirania, Agartala.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee
(B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

1. Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Chairman, B&WC,
Director, NIT, Agartala.
2. Dr. S.C. Saha
Professor, Mechanical Engg. Department, Member
NIT Agartala.
3. Dr. Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty,
Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala. Member
4. Sri Kajal Banik, Superintending Engineer, PWD,
Govt. of Tripura: Member
5. Dr. Govind Bhargava, Member Secretary
Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and invited the
Registrar and Dean (P&D) to initiate Agenda-wise discussions.

Item no. 41.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 40th BWC meeting held on 20th
March, 2018.

The minutes of the 40th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on March 27,
2018 at 10.30 A.M. in the Conference Room of Shastri Bhavan , New Delhi, India, were
circulated to all the members. The Building & Works Committee confirmed the minutes of
40th Building & Works Committee meeting.

Item No. 41.2: Action taken on the decision of the 39th Building & Works
Committee Meeting held on 27th July, 2017 in the Conference
Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.
