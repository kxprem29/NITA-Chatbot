**Item No. 42.8:** To consider the estimate for the work of “Extension of height of the boundary wall at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 1,82,91,026.00

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal and recommended placing the proposal in the next FC and BoG meeting for necessary administrative approval and expenditure sanction.

**Item No. 42.9:** “Design, Supply, Construction, Erection, trial run and commissioning of water treatment plant of capacity 2.0 MLD and construction of overhead reservoirs, pipe laying work, construction of pump house including site development and boundary wall, electrical and mechanical component at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 1458.00 lakh

The BWC discussed the issue and suggested inviting PWD (DWS Department), Govt. of Tripura. The DWS department has the expertise to render such services. It was also discussed that a modified IRP (Iron Removal Plant) may be installed for removing iron and other unwanted dissolved minerals from the groundwater without installing a treatment plant separately, as a treatment plant needs recurring expenses and extra manpower which may be avoided by exploiting modern technology. Therefore, it was decided to take a detailed estimate from DWS, which may be placed in the next BWC meeting for approval.
