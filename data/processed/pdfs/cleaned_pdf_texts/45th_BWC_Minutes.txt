Minutes of the 45th Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on 13th July 2020

The 45th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on 13th July 2020 at 4:00 P.M. in the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala Campus, Jirania, Agartala.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members attended the meeting:

*   Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Director, NIT, Agartala - Chairman, B&WC, NIT, Agartala
*   Sri D.K. Singh, Under Secretary, MHRD, Govt. of India (Attended through Skype) - Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala - Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Sujit Kumar Pal, Civil Engineering Department, NIT Agartala - Member
*   Sri Alok Chakraborty, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura (representative of Chief Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura) - Member
*   Sri Kajal Banik, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura - Member
*   Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar, NIT, Agartala (Attended through Skype) - Member Secretary

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and invited the Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.
