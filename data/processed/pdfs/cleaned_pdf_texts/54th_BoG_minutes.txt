Minutes of the 54th Meeting of the
Board of Governors
Held on January 13, 2021

The 54th meeting of the Board of Governors of National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on January 13, 2021, at 11:50 am in the Virtual Class Room, NIT Agartala, Tripura, through online mode.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati, Chairperson of BoG, NIT Agartala and Former Director General (Naval Systems & Materials), DRDO
(2) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director, NIT Agartala.
(3) Prof. (Dr.) T. G. Sitharam, Director, IIT Guwahati
(4) Mr. Madan Mohan, Addl. Director General (HE), MoE, Govt. of India
(5) Mrs. Darshana M. Dabral, JS & FA, IFD, MoE, Govt. of India
(6) Mr. Saju Vaheed A, Director, DHE, Govt. of Tripura.
(7) Dr. D. Bhattacharya, Professor, Math. Dept., NIT Agartala.
(8) Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi), Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Dept., NIT Agartala.
(9) Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar, NIT Agartala.

Dr. Subhash Chandra Sati, Chairperson, Board of Governors of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors. The Chairperson invited the Director and Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 54.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 52nd (Special) Meeting and 53rd Meeting of the Board of Governors, NIT Agartala.

The minutes of the 52nd (Special) and 53rd Meetings of the Board of Governors were confirmed.

Item No. 54.2: To consider Special Cash Package equivalent in lieu of Leave Travel Concession Fare for Central Government Employees during the block 2018-2021.

The Board approved the Special Cash Package equivalent in lieu of Leave Travel Concession Fare for the regular employees of NIT Agartala during the block 2018-2021.

Item No. 54.3: To consider the proposal for acceptance of the lowest bidder for the work "Extension of height of existing boundary wall at NIT Agartala".

Based on the recommendation of the 46th Building & Works Committee meeting and 38th Finance Committee meeting, the Board approved the additional expenditure of Rs. 30,67,857.00 (Rupees thirty lakh sixty-seven thousand eight hundred fifty-seven only) from the Institute grant and advised that all norms/guidelines/procedures be followed. The acceptance of the lowest bidder should be processed by Institute level authorities as per applicable government/institute rules.

Item No. 54.4: To consider the proposal for "Construction of 200 capacity Girls' hostel at NIT Agartala, Tripura for an amount of Rs. 16,77,41,197.00" (Deposit Work).

The Board approved the proposal. The Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Govt. of India, has accorded administrative approval in principle for Rs. 6,30,00,000.00 (Rupees six crore thirty lakh only). The remaining amount of Rs. (16,77,41,197.00 - 6,30,00,000.00) = Rs. 10,47,41,197.00 (Rupees ten crore forty-seven lakh forty-one thousand one hundred ninety-seven only) was sanctioned from the IRG of the Institute.

Item No. 54.5: To consider the proposal for "HVAC, Stage Craft, Stage Light, Sound System, Projector System, Sitting Arrangement in the Seminar Hall at NIT, Agartala" (Deposit Work).

The proposal was approved by the Board for an amount of Rs. 11,63,10,000.00 (Rupees eleven crore sixty-three lakh ten thousand) from IRG and institute grant.

Item No. 54.6: To consider the proposal for "Water supply arrangement at NIT Agartala by the way of constructing Ground Water Treatment Plant including construction of Deep Tube Wells, Ground Level Reservoir, Raw water Rising Main, Clear Water Rising Mains and other allied works etc. complete in all respect (Deposit work)".

The Board advised that the matter be placed in the next BoG meeting along with justification due to the increase of estimated cost from Rs. 9,03,24,485.00 (Rupees nine crore three lakh twenty-four thousand four hundred eighty-five) to Rs. 11,96,79,000.00 (Rupees eleven crore ninety-six lakh seventy-nine thousand).

Item No. 54.7: To consider the single bid for the work: Upgradation of the 2-story building besides the Knowledge Centre at NIT Agartala".

Based on the recommendation of the 46th Building & Works Committee meeting and 38th Finance Committee meeting, the Board approved the additional fund of Rs. 12,43,495.00 (Rupees twelve lakh forty-three thousand four hundred ninety-five only) from the Institute grant/IRG, while consideration of the single bid should be processed by the Institute level authorities within the permissible government/institute rules.

Item No. 54.8: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 45th Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the recommendations of the 45th Building & Works Committee meeting held on July 13, 2020.

Item No. 54.9: To consider the recommendation of the minutes of the 36th Finance Committee meeting of NIT Agartala held on June 24, 2020.

The Board approved the recommendations of the minutes of the 36th Finance Committee meeting of NIT Agartala held on June 24, 2020.

Item No. 54.10: To consider the mapping of Designation to the Non-Teaching staff (regular) of NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the recommendation of the DPC conducted on 24-07-2020 with regard to the mapping of designation without any financial implication.

Item No. 54.11: To consider the MoU between NIT Agartala and Istituto Nazionale Di Oceanografia E Di Geofisica Sperimentale - OGS, Sgonico, Italy.

The Board approved the proposal for signing of the MoU between National Institute of Technology, Agartala, and Istituto Nazionale Di Oceanografia E Di Geofisica Sperimentale - OGS, Sgonico, Italy. The Board also suggested sending the progress of all the MoUs signed by NIT Agartala to the Chairman.

Item No. 54.12: To consider contractual faculty members serving the institute for interview.

The Board suggested re-advertising the faculty positions as the advertisement was issued in the month of November 2019.

Item No. 54.13: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Not applicable.

The Board of Governors opined that while approvals/sanctions for various plans/funds/activities are accorded as above, the necessary compliance of government rules/institute procedures towards execution of the above action points needs to be ensured by appropriate institute level authorities in line with applicable laid down procedures.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.

(Prof. (Dr.) D. Bhattacharya) (Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma)
Registrar Chairperson of BoG and Director
