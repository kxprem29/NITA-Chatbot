Minutes of the 33rd Meeting of the
Finance Committee
held on June 26, 2019

The 33rd meeting of the Finance Committee of National Institute of Technology, Agartala was held on June 26, 2019 at 10:30 am in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, New Delhi.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. H. K. Sharma: Chairperson, Finance Committee, NIT Agartala.
(2) Mrs. Darshna M. Dabral: JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India.
(3) Mr. K. Rajan: US, MHRD, Govt. of India [Representative of Joint Secretary (dealing with NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India].
(4) Prof. S. C. Saha: Professor, NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee: Assoc. Professor, NIT Agartala.
(6) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Member Secretary, Finance Committee and Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Prof. H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Finance Committee and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting. He welcomed all members of the Finance Committee.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 33.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 32nd Meeting of Finance Committee held on February 13, 2019.

The minutes of the 32nd Finance Committee meeting held on February 13, 2019, were confirmed after deliberations. To avoid complications due to earlier orders/decisions of the Institute with regard to payment of a sitting fee of Rs. 5000.00 for attending meetings or other official affairs, the decision taken vide Agenda Item No. 32.16 of the 32nd Finance Committee will supersede all earlier decisions.

Item No. 33.2: Action taken on the decision of the 31st Finance Committee meeting held on August 09, 2018.

The Finance Committee noted the action taken report. The FC was apprised about the Legal opinion with regard to action taken on TDS liabilities vide agenda 31.12, in which the Legal Counsel advised that it would not be wise to go for litigation because of the deposition of money to the Central Government.

Item No. 33.3: To consider the Annual Budget proposal of the Institute for the Financial Year 2019-20.

The Finance Committee accepted and confirmed the Annual Budget of the Institute for the Financial Year 2019-20.

Item No. 33.4: To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as on June 15, 2019.

The Finance Committee noted the financial status of the Institute.

Item No. 33.5: Present Status of TEQIP-III Project of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted the status of the TEQIP-III project.

Item No. 33.6: To consider the Physical & Financial Statement of the ongoing construction works/projects.

The Finance Committee noted the physical & financial statement of the ongoing construction works/projects.

Item No. 33.7: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 41st Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee accepted the recommendations of the minutes of the 41st Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

Item No. 33.8: To consider the Medical Attendance Guidelines for NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee suggested following the CS (MA) guidelines for medical benefit to the regular employees of NIT Agartala. The Finance Committee also approved the list of the referral hospitals, approved by the Govt. of Tripura vide No. F. | (9)-Secy/ HFW/ 13 (1), dt. 08-06-2016 (Annexure — A).

Item No. 33.9: To consider the adoption of Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for the funds received from individuals/ Private Bodies/ Non-Government Organizations for giving Memorial Awards/ Fellowships.

The Finance Committee approved the adoption of the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP).

However, the Finance Committee suggested opening a Current Account with Collect Facility, so that the donor may donate directly to this account.

Item No. 33.10: To consider the Stipend of Rs. 5000.00 to be given to Mr. Amit Debnath, Research Scholar of CSE Department as per the order passed by the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura vide WA 49 of 2018.

The Finance Committee ratified the decision and suggested issuing an Advisory Memo to the members/chairman of the Doctoral Committee.

Item No. 33.11: To consider user charges for the students to meet the minimum expenses of the facilities at NIT Agartala w.e.f. 2019-20 Academic year.

The Finance Committee noted the information.

Item No. 33.12: National Pension System (NPS), the monthly contribution of the employer @ 14% of the Basic Pay plus DA w.e.f. 01-04-2019.

The Finance Committee approved the adoption of a contribution to NPS from 10% to 14% in respect of the Employer's share, in line with the Office Memorandum of the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India vide F. No. 13-4/ 2019-TC, dated: 16-04-2019.

Item No. 33.13: To consider consolidated fees for the conference and hearing of Sr. Advocate for defending NIT Agartala at the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.

The Finance Committee ratified the decision.

Item No. 33.14: To consider the work of “Special Repair, Retrofitting and Upgradation of the Library Building at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs. 71,55,558.00.

Due to earthquakes, cracks have developed in different parts of the Building, and the structure is lying idle without any use. Therefore, to make use of the structure in the interest of the students and to prevent it from further deterioration, Special Repair, Retrofitting, and Upgradation of the Library Building is essential and is to be undertaken immediately.

The Finance Committee, therefore, approved the estimated cost of Rs. 71,55,558.00 (Rupees Seventy-One Lakh Fifty-Five Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty-Eight) for the works and recommended placing it before the Board.

Item No. 33.15: To consider the work of “Special Repair, Retrofitting and Upgradation of Dhalai Hostel building at NIT Agartala” for Rs. 98,94,302.00.

This hostel building is old, and therefore gradual deterioration has taken place in the building over time. To keep this building functional for the use of students, special repair, retrofitting, and up-gradation is urgently needed.

The Finance Committee approved the estimated cost of Rs. 98,94,302.00 (Rupees Ninety-Eight Lakh Ninety-Four Thousand Three Hundred and Two) for the works and recommended placing it before the Board.

Item No. 33.16: To consider the work of “Upgradation of a 2-storey building besides the Knowledge Centre at NIT Agartala” for Rs. 60,77,405.00.

A built-up and incomplete Structure is lying idle adjacent to the Knowledge Centre (library building) for the last few years. A committee was formed to look at the efficacy of the structure and suggested its use. The committee recommended making use of the existing incomplete
