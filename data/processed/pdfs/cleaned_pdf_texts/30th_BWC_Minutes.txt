**Item No. 30.32.1:** To consider the estimate for the work “Special repair of Gomati Hostel during the year 2013-14 at NIT Agartala”, at a cost of Rs. 50,21,082.00.

Due to the sad demise of Sri Rahul Kumar, a 2nd-year student of ECE Department, who was residing in Gomati Hostel, a total student unrest took place at the NIT Agartala campus from December 04, 2013, onwards. Students gheraoed the administration and demanded the immediate renovation of all three old Hostels viz. Gomati, Dhalai, and Howrah Hostels at par with the new Hostel (Aryabhatta) within a month's time. The District Administration also intervened in this matter and advised the Institute authority to accept one of the demands to renovate all three old Hostels namely Gomati, Dhalai, and Howrah within this time. The decision of the renovation of three old hostels viz. Gomati, Dhalai, and Howrah was taken in presence of the District Magistrate, Superintendent of Police, and other state government Officials to subside the agitation of the students.

The BWC members discussed in detail the prevailing situation of student unrest and the commitment given by the Institute authority and District Administration. The BWC also decided to give the work to M/s NCCL, a resourceful Contractor who has been working on other construction works of the NIT Agartala campus and has the potential to complete the work within the time frame as mentioned above.

Considering the emergent situation, the BWC approved the execution of the works by M/S NCCL as extra items / deviated quantities of works on the agreement of Administrative Block I & II and Seminar Hall at NIT Agartala, with the following conditions at Gomati Hostel only as an exceptional case:

1.  Execution of items in the estimate at the rates covered in the existing agreement (Rs. 39,32,218/-)
2.  For other items, the rates may be finalized by giving a percentage above the schedule rate as per the existing agreement (Rs. 10,11,367/-)
3.  Items not borne on the schedule may be executed on the basis of market rate analysis (Nil)

The Estate Engineering Section prepared the revised estimate as per the advice and the guidelines of the BWC which comes to Rs. 49,45,585.00. The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal at a cost of Rs. 49,45,585.00.
