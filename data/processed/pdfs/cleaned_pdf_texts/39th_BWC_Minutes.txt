Minutes (modified) of the 39th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on July 27, 2017

The 39th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on July 27, 2017, at 6:00 pm in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

*   Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya: Chairman, B&WC, Director, NIT Agartala.
*   Sri Asit Bhaumik: Member, TES Gr-I, Chief Engineer / Representative of PWD (Civil), Govt. of Tripura.
*   Sri Kajal Banik: Member, Superintending Engineer (Elect), Office of the Chief Engineer / Representative of PWD, Govt. of Tripura.
*   Dr. Ratul Das: Member, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala.
*   Dr. Rishi Prasad Sharma: Member, Professor, Civil Engg. Department, NIT Agartala.
*   Dr. Govind Bhargava: Member Secretary, Registrar, N.I.T., Agartala.

The Director (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India, could not attend the meeting.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and requested the Registrar and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

The minutes of the 38th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on March 15, 2017, in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura, were circulated to all the members. No comments have been received from any member. Therefore, the Minutes of the 38th Building & Works Committee of NIT, Agartala, are hereby confirmed.
