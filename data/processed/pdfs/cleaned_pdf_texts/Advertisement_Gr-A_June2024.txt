**QUALIFICATION AND EXPERIENCE**

**01. DEPUTY REGISTRAR**

Essential:

Educational Qualification & Experience:

Master’s degree in any discipline with at least 55% marks or its equivalent grade in the UGC point scale with a good academic record from a recognized University/Institute.

Experience:

(i) 9 years’ experience as Assistant Professor in the AGP of Rs. 6,000/- and above with 3 years’ experience in educational administration, or
(ii) Comparable experience in research establishment and/or other institutions of higher education, or
(iii) 5 years of administrative experience as Assistant Registrar in the GP of Rs.5400/- or equivalent.

Desirable:

(i) Qualification in area of Management/Engineering/Law.
(ii) Experience of working in E-Office system.
(iii) A Chartered or Cost Accountant degree or diploma for the post of Deputy Registrar (Finance and Accounts) or Deputy Registrar (Internal Audit).
(iv) Administrative Experience (equivalent to that of Assistant Registrar) earned in Industry, Government, PSU, Banks and Higher Autonomous Educational Institutes will be recognized.

**02. ASSISTANT REGISTRAR**

Pay Level-10, Pay of Rs. 56,100/- per month

Essential:

Educational Qualification & Experience:

Master’s degree in any discipline with at least 55% marks or its equivalent grade in the CGPA/UGC point scale with a good academic record from a recognized University/Institute, or Employees serving as Superintendent (SG-l)/Private Secretary (NFG) in PB-2 with GP Rs.5400/- with at least 2 years regular service as Superintendent (SG-II)/Private Secretary (NFG) in PB-2 with GP Rs.4800/- with at least 5 years regular service with Master’s degree.

Desirable:

(i) Qualification in area of Management/Engineering/Law.
(ii) Experience of working in E-Office system.
(iii) A Chartered or Cost Accountant for the post of Assistant Registrar (Finance and Accounts).

**03. EXECUTIVE ENGINEER (CIVIL ENGINEER)**

Pay Level-10, Pay of Rs. 56,100/- per month

Essential:

Educational Qualification & Experience:

B.E/B.Tech in Civil Engineering with first class or its equivalent grade in the CGPA/UGC 7 point scale with a good academic record from a recognized University/Institute or Employees of the Institute with at least five years regular service as Assistant Engineer (SG-II) in PB-2, Grade Pay of Rs. 4800/- or with at least 2 years regular service as Assistant Engineer (SG-I) in PB-2, Grade Pay of Rs. 5400/-.

Desirable:

Experience in handling large construction projects, knowledge of Computer-aided Design (CAD) and latest Management Technology/other relevant software applications.

**04. SCIENTIFIC / TECHNICAL OFFICER**

Pay Level-10, Pay of Rs. 56,100/- per month

Essential:

Educational Qualification & Experience:

(i) B.E/B. Tech/M.Sc. in relevant field or MCA Degree with first class or equivalent grade (6.5 in 10 point scale) and consistently excellent academic record; or

(ii) Employees of the Institute serving as Technical Asst. (SG-II) for at least 5 years with Grade Pay Rs. 4800/- or Technical Asst. (SG-I) with Grade Pay Rs. 5400/- in PB-2 with two years regular service in the Institute.

Desirable:

a) Work experience in relevant field, e.g. maintenance of Scientific equipment, system administration, software development in fabrication and support to research.
b) Candidates with Ph.D. in the relevant field shall be preferred.

**GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS AND INFORMATION**

7.  Incomplete applications are liable to be rejected.
8.  All qualifications, experiences, and age limits will be considered as on or before 02/08/2024 (Closing date of online application form).

If the University/Boards award Grades/CGPA, the same will have to be indicated in equivalent percentage of marks as per the norms adopted by the University/Boards. In the absence of the same, the candidature will not be considered.

13. Original documents along with one set of self-attested copies will have to be produced at the time of the test/interview for verification.
14. Applications received online on or before the closing date will only be considered for scrutiny and selection.
15. Mere fulfillment of minimum qualifications and experience does not entitle any candidate to be called for a test/interview. A shortlisting criterion may be set higher than the minimum advertised.
16. The Institute reserves the right to call all the eligible candidates or shortlisted candidates for an interview/test after screening. The Institute may conduct a screening test if the number of applications received is large. No interim communication on the status of the application will be entertained.
17. Experienced and/or meritorious candidates may be granted a higher starting pay on the recommendation of the selection committee.
18. Canvassing in any manner would entail disqualification of the candidature.
19. The names of the shortlisted candidates will be displayed on the Institute website. Besides, all information regarding the selection test, interview schedule, etc., will also be provided through the Institute website only. The Institute will not be responsible in any manner if a candidate fails to visit/access the website in time. 20. The date for determining the eligibility of candidates in every respect, i.e., qualifications, experience, and preferred age limit, etc., shall be considered as of the closing date, i.e., the last date of submission of the online application form, failing which they may be debarred from the recruitment process.
21. The Institute shall retain completed online application data for non-shortlisted candidates only for three months after completion of the recruitment process.
22. Candidates who wish to apply for more than one post should apply separately for each post in the prescribed manner with the requisite fee for each post.
23. Candidates applying for direct recruitment and Deputation shall submit separate applications for each mode and pay the fee separately.
24. Failure to remit the fee as per instructions will render the application rejected. Fees once paid shall not be refunded under any circumstances.
25. The decision of the Institute in all matters will be final. No correspondence/interim inquiries will be entertained from the candidates in connection with the process of selection/interview, including reasons for not being called for an interview. Any dispute regarding the selection/recruitment process will be settled through Arbitration, failing which the matter can be appealed in the Hon’ble High Court of Tripura.
26. The reservation policy is applicable as per Govt. of India norms.
27. The Institute may set norms higher than the minimum eligibility criteria while shortlisting. This shall be binding on all the applicants.
28. Any subsequent changes notified in recruitment rules or guidelines shall be applicable accordingly in this recruitment.

30. Particulars and criteria in respect of each post are attached at Annexure-A.
31. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the test/interview or both.
32. For the post of Scientific/Technical Officer, the relevant fields in the recruitment rules include Computer Science and Engineering/MCA Degree.
33. The waiting list may be operated within a period of one year from the date of the interview.

**How to apply:**

Step-1: Register to create a User ID and Password for the online application on the Institute website (www.nita.ac.in). Fill out the online application form completely and submit it online.

Step-2: You can save before final submission to edit/review any field. No change is permissible after submission.

Step-3: After submission of the form, the payment gateway will open for fee payment if applicable (not applicable for women and PwD candidates). Follow the instructions carefully for fee payment. Without fee payment, the application will not be accepted/considered.

Step-4: Take a printout of the completed application form, paste a recent color passport-size photograph, put your signature in the specified spaces, and preserve it safely. Shortlisted candidates will be required to submit the same while reporting for the selection test/Interview.
