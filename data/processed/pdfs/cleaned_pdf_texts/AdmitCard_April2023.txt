NOTICE

This is to inform all UG, PG, and Dual Degree (Intermediate & Final Year) students appearing for the Even Semester End Term Examination scheduled to begin on 20th April 2023, that the Admit Cards for the said Examination can be printed from their respective MIS Accounts as per the schedule mentioned below:

| Sl. No. | Name of the Examination                                        | Examination Starting Date | Duration of downloading Admit card from student’s respective MIS Account |
|---------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1       | Even Semester End Term Examination of UG, PG and Dual (Intermediate & Final Year students) | 20th April 2023           | 17th April to 19th April 2023                                                |

No student will be able to print their Admit Card before or after the above mentioned schedule.

All students are hereby instructed to bring their Admit Cards to the Examination Hall when appearing for the examination and obtain the invigilator's signature at the designated place.

Students without their respective Admit Cards will be debarred from appearing in the Examinations.
