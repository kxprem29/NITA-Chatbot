Minutes of the 49th Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on 15th March 2022

The 49th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala was held on 15th March, 2022 at 10:00 A.M. in the Virtual Class Room of NIT Agartala, Jirania, Agartala, West Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee, NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members attended the meeting:

*   Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Director, NIT Agartala - Chairman, Building & Works Committee, NIT Agartala
*   Prof. (Dr.) Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala - Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Richi Prasad Sharma, Professor, Civil Engineering Department, NIT Agartala - Member
*   Mr. Indrajit Kuri, Under Secretary, Ministry of Higher Education, Govt. of India - Member
*   Er. Sampa Majumder, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura (representative of Chief Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura) - Member
*   Er. Debashis Paul, Executive Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura - Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Debasish Bhattacharya, Registrar(i/c), NIT Agartala - Member Secretary

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and invited the Dean (P&D) to initiate Agenda-wise discussions.
