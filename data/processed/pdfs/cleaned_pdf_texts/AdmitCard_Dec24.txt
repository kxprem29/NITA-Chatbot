NOTICE

This is to inform all students of the 1st Semester UG, DUAL & PG programs appearing for the End Term Odd Semester Examination, scheduled to begin on 13th December 2024, that the Admit Cards for the said examination can be printed from their respective MIS accounts as per the schedule mentioned below:

| Sl. No. | Name of the Examination                                     | Starting Date   | Duration of Downloading Admit Card from Student’s Respective MIS Account |
|---------|----------------------------------------------------------|-----------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1       | End Term Odd Semester Examination (1st Sem UG, DUAL & PG) | 13th December 2024 | 10th December to 12th December 2024                                     |

No student will be able to print their Admit Card before or after the above-mentioned schedule.

All students are hereby instructed to bring their Admit Card to the Examination Hall each day of the examination and obtain endorsement from the invigilator at the designated place. Any student who fails to bring their Admit Card on any day of the examination will be required to obtain a duplicate Admit Card for the remaining days. Duplicate Admit Cards can be downloaded from their respective MIS accounts upon payment of a fine of Rs. 200/-.

Students without their Admit Cards will be debarred from appearing in the Examinations.
