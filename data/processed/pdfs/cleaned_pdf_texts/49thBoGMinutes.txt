NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AGARTALA
WEST TRIPURA, PIN 799046

Minutes of the 49th Meeting of the
Board of Governors
held on November 09, 2019

The 49th meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on November 09, 2019, at 10:30 am in the Board Room of NIT Agartala, Tripura.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson of BoG and Director, NIT Agartala
(2) Mr. D. K. Singh, Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD (Representative of the JS & FA, IFD MHRD, Govt. of India)
(3) Prof. Sharad Gokhale, Dean of Infrastructure Planning & Management, IIT Guwahati (nominee of the Director, IITG)
(4) Mr. Saju Vaheed A, Director, Higher Education, Govt. of Tripura (Representative of the Principal Secretary to the Govt. of Tripura, Dept. of Higher Education, Agartala)
(5) Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha, Member, Professor, ME Dept., NIT Agartala
(6) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee, Member, Asso. Professor, EE Dept., NIT Agartala
(7) Dr. Govind Bhargava, Secretary, Registrar, NIT Agartala

Prof. Sharad Gokhale and Mr. D. K. Singh attended the meeting through Skype.

The Special Secretary / Additional Secretary / Joint Secretary dealing with Technical Education, Dept. of Higher Education, MHRD, could not attend the meeting.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Board of Governors, and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors.

The Chairperson invited the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 49.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 48th Meeting of the Board of Governors, NIT Agartala

The minutes of the 48th Meeting of the Board of Governors, held on October 03, 2019, were confirmed after the incorporation of comments from the Dy. Secretary (Finance), IFD, MHRD, with regard to Item No. 48.9, and the following line was added: “During the proceedings of this discussion, the Registrar went outside and did not participate.”

Item No. 49.2: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 29th Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on February 27, 2019

The Board approved the agenda numbered as S/29/14, S/29/15, S/29/17, S/29/21, and S/29/24 of the 29th Senate meeting minutes. However, regarding agenda no. S/29/14, pertaining to the opening of a multidisciplinary center, the Members opined that the Institute can apply separately to the Ministry of HRD for a grant.

Item No. 49.3: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 30th Senate meeting of NIT Agartala held on September 27, 2019

The Board approved the minutes of the 30th Senate meeting held on September 27, 2019. The agenda no. S/30/32 does not involve any financial liability on the part of the Institute, and the necessary permission was acquired before signing the agreement. In agenda no. S/30/A/03, the original number of seats can be restored as per the demand of the course.

Item No. 49.4: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 31st Senate meeting of NIT Agartala scheduled on November 06, 2019

The Board approved and confirmed the minutes of the 31st Senate meeting held on November 06, 2019.

Item No. 49.5: Any other issue with the permission of the Chair

Not applicable.
