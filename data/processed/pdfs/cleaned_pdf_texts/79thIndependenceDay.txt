NOTICE

This is to inform all faculty members, staff, and students of NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala that NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala will jointly celebrate the 79th Independence Day on 15th August 2025. The program schedule is given below:

| Sl. No. | Event                    | Date          | Time    | Venue                      |
|---------|--------------------------|---------------|---------|----------------------------|
| 1       | Tiranga Rally            | 13.08.2025    | 7:00 AM | ABV Sports Complex         |
| 2       | National Flag Hoisting   | 15.08.2025    | 8:00 AM | SWAMI VIVEKANANDA KNOWLEDGE PARK |
| 3       | National Flag Hoisting and March Past | 15.08.2025 | 8:30 AM | ABV Sports Complex         |
| 4       | Address by the Hon'ble Director NITA | 15.08.2025 | 8:40 AM | ABV Sports Complex         |
| 5       | Cultural Program         | 15.08.2025    | 8:50 AM | ABV Sports Complex         |

It is also informed that the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign under the aegis of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav will be celebrated across the Nation from 2nd to 15th August 2025. All faculty, staff, and students of the institute are encouraged to hoist the Indian National Flag on their premises during the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign and to upload selfies with the Tiranga on the website www.harghartiranga.com.

All faculty members, staff, and students of NIT Agartala and IIIT Agartala are requested to participate in the program as per the mentioned schedule and make it a grand success.
