Minutes of the 30th Meeting of the
Finance Committee
held on March 27, 2018

The 30th meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on March 27, 2018, at 11:30 am in the Chamber of Joint Secretary & Financial Advisor (IFD), First Floor, ‘C’ Wing, MHRD, New Delhi.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma: Chairperson and Member of Finance Committee,
Director of NIT Agartala.

(2) Mrs. Darshna M. Dabral: Financial Advisor (IFD), MHRD, Govt. of India.

(3) Mr. Sanjeev K. Sharma: Director (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India
(Representative of Joint Secretary (dealing with NITs), Dept. of Higher Education, MHRD).

(4) Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha: Member, Professor, NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee: Member, Asst. Professor, NIT Agartala.
(6) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Member Secretary, Finance Committee and
Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

The Chairperson informed the Finance Committee that, as per the provision of Statutes, two faculty members of the Institute, i.e., (1) Prof. (Dr.) S. C. Saha, Mechanical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala, and (2) Dr. Subhadeep Bhattacharjee, Asst. Professor, Electrical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala, are members of the Finance Committee. This information was also ratified by the Finance Committee.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson of the Finance Committee of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Finance Committee. He thanked them for attending the meeting.

The Chairperson then invited the Registrar to initiate the discussion on the agenda items.

Item No. 30.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 29th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on March 24, 2017.

The Finance Committee (FC) confirmed the minutes, which were placed before the FC after incorporating the comments related to Item Nos. 29.6, 29.9, 29.12.1, 29.12.3, 29.12.6, 29.12.7, 29.12.8, 29.12.9, and 29.12.10, received from NIT Division, MHRD, vide letter F. No. 1-5/2017 TS.1II, dated: April 06, 2017.

Item No. 30.2: To consider the Annual Accounts and the Audit Report on the accounts of the Institute for the financial year 2016-17.

The FC considered the Annual Accounts and the Audit Report on the accounts of the Institute for the financial year 2016-17 and opined that the discrepancies noted by CAG Tripura should be addressed in the Annual Report to be laid down in Parliament to avoid any delay.

Item No. 30.3: TDS liabilities of Rs. 53,74,319 for payments made to Tripura Housing and Construction Board, Agartala, during the financial years 2007-08 to 2010-11.

The Finance Committee viewed the matter seriously and decided that:

1. An investigation should be carried out to fix the responsibility for not deducting TDS, and a report should be submitted.

2. Legal opinion should be sought to initiate legal proceedings against THCB. The FC observed that the penal interest will keep increasing; therefore, the Penal Interest of Rs. 53,74,319 may be paid to the IT department immediately, as the IT department has already provided relief from Rs. 3.55 Cr to Rs. 1.53 Cr.

The decision is confirmed for implementation.

Item No. 30.4: Implementation of Credit policy of Higher Education Funding Agency (HEFA) — When required in future.

The FC adopted HEFA for implementation, and the decision is confirmed.

Item No. 30.5: To consider signing an MOU between NITA and MHRD, Govt. of India, as per requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Finance Committee approved the MOU drafted between NITA and MHRD and suggested submitting it to the MHRD with suggested modifications. The decision is confirmed for implementation.

Item No. 30.6: To ratify the decision of the Chairman (FC) regarding the Budget for F.Y. 2017-18.

The FC ratified the decision of the Chairman (FC) regarding the Budget for the year 2017-18.

Item No. 30.7: Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 30.7.1 To approve the guidelines for payment of TA/DA to students for participation in various outside activities.

The FC ratified the guidelines for the payment of TA/DA to students. However, the Finance Committee decided that students may be allowed to participate in various outside activities normally by train in Sleeper Class. In exceptional/emergent cases, students can be allowed to travel by Air with the approval of the Director on a case-by-case basis.

Item No. 30.7.2: To consider the guidelines for some activities related to TEQIP-III and for Financial Assistance to the Faculty/ Staff/Students of NITA from the TEQIP-III fund.

The Finance Committee ratified the decision taken by the Chairman (FC) for Financial Assistance to the Faculty/ Staff/ Students of NITA from the TEQIP-III fund. However, the students may be allowed to participate in various outside activities normally by train in Sleeper Class. In exceptional/emergent cases, the students can be allowed to travel by Air with the approval of the Director on a case-by-case basis.

Item No. 30.7.3 To receive information regarding the Implementation of the 7th CPC for Employees of NITA receiving pay as per the 6th CPC Pay Scale (Pay Band and GP).

The Finance Committee noted the information.

Item No. 30.7.4 To receive information regarding Annual Pay enhancement matters of contractual employees (for faculty members and non-teaching employees) of NIT Agartala.

The information was noted by the FC.

Item No. 30.7.5 To receive information regarding Honorarium to Non-official members of the Board of Governors and Finance Committee of NIT Agartala.

The information was noted by the FC.

Item No. 30.7.6 To consider the Financial status of the Institute as of March 15, 2018.

The Finance Committee noted the Financial Status of the Institute.

Item No. 30.7.7 To consider various recommendations and decisions taken by the Building & Works Committee at its 40th meeting held on March 27, 2018.

The Finance Committee suggested putting up recommendations in the next FC meeting.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
