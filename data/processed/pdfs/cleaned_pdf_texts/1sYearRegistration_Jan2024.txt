NOTICE

Sub: Registration for 1st year (2nd Semester) UG, Dual Degree, PG and Ph.D programmes for Even Semester (2023-24 session).

1. The students of 1st year (2nd Semester) UG, Dual Degree, PG and Ph.D programmes are hereby informed to pay their semester fee in full to be eligible for subject registration during the Jan - Jun 2024 semester. Students are instructed to pay their semester fees based on the Type mentioned in Table-1 and then register themselves in MIS.

2. The requisite fees for the programmes, semesters and type are mentioned in Table 2 & 3.

3. The semester fees must be paid using the online payment portal at https://mis.nita.ac.in. The link will be available from 16/01/2024. Students are advised to use the Username and Password of their Institute MIS account (Online Payment --> SBI E-Pay Tuition Fee).

4. After completing the payment (Sl. No. 1), students will be allowed to register for subjects provisionally through the same online portal (Academic Registration --> Semester Registration). All required subjects are to be selected by each student before clicking on the submit button to complete their provisional registration and print 3 (three) sets of the provisional registration slip. All three registration slips are to be signed physically by the respective students and concerned faculty advisor/supervisors of the students/scholars and submitted to the UG/PG coordinators of the respective Departments. One signed copy of the registration slip will be handed over to the concerned student later.

5. Those who have paid excess fee against the Institute fee in the previous semester, their excess fee will be adjusted against the Institute fee in the current semester.

6. Please find the following link to pay the remaining fees by the students who have not submitted the balance fee of the 1st semester: mis.nita.ac.in (Online Payment --> SBI Epay --> Backdated Payment Interface).

7. Students availing loan through bank/other sources may also transfer their Semester fees from their respective bank through NEFT in favour of Registrar, NIT Agartala, Account No. 30369892838, IFSC Code SBIN0011491 and are instructed to update the details of their loan, the transaction details and upload relevant supporting documents in their MIS account (Academic Admission --> Upload Document). The details of the payment are to be filled in and uploaded in the Google Form - https://forms.gle/iasMStUATZUhvuAHB.

8. After completion of the above steps, students need to mandatorily report physically to their Faculty Advisor and/or UG/PG Coordinator (as applicable) of respective Department. As per the following schedule, the Faculty Advisor and/ or UG/PG Coordinator(s) will then verify the fee payment/ subject registration on their MIS portal and complete the semester registration process for the student. The dates for physical registration are given below:

    a. 16th to 22nd January, 2024 (4:00 pm) without fine.
    b. Physical registration from 22nd to 25th January, 2024 (4:00 pm), will incur a fine of Rs. 5,000/- as Penalty.
    c. Beyond 25th January, 2024, a fine of Rs. 10,000/- will be imposed as penalty within 5 working days.

9. Regarding hostel-related matters, students may contact the Office of the Chief Warden.
