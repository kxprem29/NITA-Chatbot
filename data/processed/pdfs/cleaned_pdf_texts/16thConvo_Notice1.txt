**16th CONVOCATION NOTICE**

The 16th Convocation of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, will be held on Saturday, 4th November, 2023, at NIT Agartala for conferring degrees to the graduates of the Academic Session 2022-23.

On behalf of the National Institute of Technology Agartala, we invite all degree recipients (UG, Dual Degree, PG, and Ph.D.) to attend the 16th Convocation Ceremony for receiving their degrees. This notice details the registration process for the event.

**A-1. Registration**

To participate in the 16th Convocation, all degree recipients must register through the online registration portal (mis.nita.ac.in). Click on the convocation portal, which will be active from 4th September, 2023, to 12th October, 2023. It is mandatory to mention your DigiLocker ID during registration. Individuals without a DigiLocker ID need to register on the DigiLocker portal first. No registration fee is required, as the fee has already been paid.

To access the Online Registration Portal, all degree recipients are requested to follow this link: https://mis.nita.ac.in.

**A-2. Distribution of Robes:**

Robes will be distributed to registered recipients only on the day prior to the Convocation, i.e., on 3rd November, 2023, from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM in the respective Departments. No robes will be issued after this time. Degree recipients will not be allowed to enter the Convocation Hall/premises and will receive their degrees in absentia if they do not have a robe.

Please note that degree recipients who are unable to complete their online registration may collect their degree certificate in absentia from the Examination Section during working hours after the Convocation.

**A-3. Rehearsal:**

The Convocation programme rehearsal is scheduled for 3rd November, 2023, at 3:30 PM. Only degree recipients who have registered and collected robes within the aforementioned deadline will be allowed to participate in the rehearsal and the main event. Except for the rehearsal, no degree recipients will be allowed to enter the venue during the Convocation.

**A-4. Help Desk:**

For any technical issues during online registration, please contact Mr. Pramathesh Banik at nitmisagt@gmail.com or by phone at 0381-2548537, between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM (on working days).

All are requested to visit the Institute website regularly for the latest updates.
