NOTIFICATION

Subject: Admission in MBA Programme under the Department of Management, Humanities & Social Sciences of NIT Agartala for the 2025-26 Academic Session.

Applications are invited from eligible candidates intending to pursue a 2-year full-time Residential MBA programme in the Department of Management, Humanities & Social Sciences of NIT Agartala, from the July-December 2025 session.

NIT Agartala follows the Reservation Policy as per Govt. Of India Norms.

Candidates willing to apply for admission to the programme at NIT Agartala are instructed to follow the below-mentioned instructions and the time schedule very sincerely:

1.  Visit the online portal of NIT Agartala at admission.nita.ac.in and register by clicking "Apply Now."
2.  Enter and fill in the information in the following tabs in the portal:
    a) Personal Details
    b) Address Details
    c) Educational Details
    d) Photo and Signature
    e) Upload document in support of Date of Birth (A single PDF of size less than 500 Kb)
    f) Experience certificates and certificates of Extra-Curricular Activities
    g) Online Payment (a non-refundable application fee of Rs. 500/- for SC/ST/PwD and Rs. 1000/- for other applicants, through the online portal only).
3.  After completing the activities mentioned in Sl. No. 1 & 2, the candidates are also required to send the hard copy of the offline application form containing relevant information along with all relevant documents to the MHSS department of NIT Agartala.

    The following documents are required:

    a) Valid score card of CAT/MAT/CMAT/GMAT (Scanned Copy of Original).
    b) Proof of date of birth (Scanned Copy of Original 10th Admit Card/Certificate).
    c) Mark sheet(s) of X (Scanned Copy of Original).
    d) Mark sheets(s) of XII/Diploma (Scanned Copy of Original).
    e) Photo ID proof, as per guidelines of Govt. of India (Scanned Copy of Original).
    f) Mark sheets of all semesters of Qualifying Degree (Scanned Copy of Original). If the final result of the qualifying degree is awaited, an undertaking must be uploaded as per the prescribed format at Annexure-A (Final Year Appearing Certificate duly signed by authority).
    g) Pass Certificate of Qualifying Degree, wherever applicable (Scanned Copy of Original).
    h) SC/ ST/ PwD/ OBC-NCL/ EWS certificate (if and whichever applicable) (Scanned Copy of Original). The date of issue of the OBC-NCL/ EWS Certificates must be as per guidelines of GoI (Issued on or after 01/04/2025). If not available at the time of the submission of application, in that case, the candidates may submit it through email to the department at the time of the Group Discussion & Personal Interview Process.
    i) Experience certificates and certificates of Extra-Curricular Activities, if any.

Note:

a.  Multiple documents, wherever applicable, must be uploaded as a single PDF.
b.  Migration and Conduct/Character Certificates are mandatory for provisionally selected Candidates during admission.
c.  All Certificates/documents should be in Hindi/English only.

4. Last date for receiving the Online Application Form is 25/04/2025 (Friday) till 3 PM, and the last date for receiving the hard copy of the Application Form is 05/05/2025 (Monday).

Eligibility: Graduates of a full-time regular course in Engineering discipline with 60% of marks [CGPA/CPI of 6.0 in 10 point scale] and for any other disciplines with 55% of marks [CGPA/CPI of 5.5 in 10 point scale] with a valid score either in CAT/CMAT/GMAT/MAT/ or Institute Level Test (which will be conducted by NIT Agartala). A relaxation of 5% in marks or 0.5 in CGPA/CPI in 10 point scale in graduation marks may be extended to the SC/ST candidate. Final year appearing candidates may also apply.

Institute Level Test for MBA Admission-2025 will be conducted by NIT Agartala through online mode.

Group Discussion and Personal Interview process (GD & PI) will be held in online mode.

Applicants, who have valid CAT/CMAT/GMAT/MAT scores, are not required to appear in the Institute Level Test (ILT). Either a valid score of CAT/CMAT/GMAT/MAT or Institute Level Test (ILT) (which will be conducted by NIT Agartala) will be considered for the Personal Assessment (PA) process.

The decision of NIT Agartala regarding the date for PI & Institute Level Test will be final.

No request for changes for dates regarding PI & Institute Level Test will be entertained.

All the prescribed format of certificates needs to be downloaded from the official website of NIT Agartala.

The online portal for application will be closed as per schedule.

Applications of the candidates may be rejected if supporting testimonials, etc., are not uploaded properly. Applications may also be rejected if any misleading documents or wrong or incomplete information are given.

The fees payable at the time of admission will be notified on the Institute website in due course of time.

All selected candidates must stay in the institute hostel.

For all hostel-related matters, candidates may contact the office of the Chief Warden. Contact details are available on the Institute website.
