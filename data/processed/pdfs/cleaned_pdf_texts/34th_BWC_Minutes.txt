**Minutes of the 34th Building & Works Committee Meeting**
held on August 24, 2015

The 34th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on August 24, 2015 (Monday) at 6:00 pm in the Conference Room of the NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura. The minutes of the 34th Building & Works Committee meeting were placed before the 26th & 27th Finance Committee meetings. The Committee suggested some corrections/modifications. Incorporating those suggestions, the minutes were placed before the 35th Building & Works Committee meeting held on February 10, 2016, where, after lengthy discussion on the suggestions proposed by the Finance Committee, the minutes of the 34th Building & Works Committee meeting, incorporating those suggestions, were confirmed.

The confirmed minutes of the 34th Building & Works Committee meeting are as follows:

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

*   Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya: Chairman, B&WC, Director, NIT, Agartala
*   Sri Asit Bhaumik: Member, TES Gr-I Addl. Chief Engineer / Representative of PWD (Civil), Govt. of Tripura
*   Sri Kajal Banik: Member, Superintending Engineer (Elect), Electrical Inspectorate/ Representative of PWD, Govt. of Tripura.
*   Dr. Debasish Bhattacharya: Member, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala.
*   Dr. Richi Prasad Sharma: Member, Professor, Civil Engg. Department, NIT Agartala
*   Mr. Sumanta Chakraborti: Member Secretary, Registrar (I/C), NIT, Agartala.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and requested the Registrar (I/C) and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

**Item No. 34.1:** Confirmation of the minutes of the 33rd Building and Works Committee held on February 23, 2015

The minutes of the 33rd Building & Works Committee Meeting held on February 24, 2015, in the Conference Room of the NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura, were circulated to all the members. **Item No. 34.3:** To consider various construction activities already approved by the Chairman, BWC, for ratification by the BWC.

The Building & Works Committee (BWC) ratified the action taken in awarding works mentioned in the respective items under agenda item No. 34.3.

**Item No. 34.4:** To consider the estimate for “Construction of Central Lecture Hall Complex having 27 Class Rooms at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs.70,52,95,723.00.

The Building & Works Committee was apprised about the proposal. BWC noted that the meeting of the Scrutiny Committee comprising the experts Dr. Virendra Kumar Paul and Dr. Lakshman Nandagiri has been scheduled to be held on September 16, 2015.

**Item No. 34.5:** To consider the total tentative deviation (including extra items, substitute items, etc. in totality) in the work of Construction of Sports Complex at NIT Agartala at a cost of Rs. 4,87,99,551.00.

This item was discussed in detail, and it was opined that more information regarding the amount of deviated structural steel/polycarbonate/reinforcement steel, RCC & brick work, the rate in excess of 30%, is to be decided by the Institute after discussion with the contractor. After this exercise, a special meeting may be convened to discuss this agenda item. M/S NCC Limited is to be contacted to hold a discussion regarding the negotiation of rates in respect of structural steel, polycarbonate sheet, reinforcement, brick work & RCC, for the amount in excess of 30% of the BOQ.

**Item No. 34.6:** To consider an estimate for the “Electromechanical work in the swimming pool of the Sports Complex at NIT Agartala”, at a cost of Rs.4,47,84,869.00

Based on guidance by the statutory Finance Committee of NIT, Agartala, BWC felt the necessity of a comparative study with other such Swimming Pools. It was decided to reconsider the proposal in the next meeting.

**Item No. 34.7:** To consider approval for the work “Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of 15 (fifteen) lifts in Administrative Building 1 & 2, School of Management, International Guest House, Academic Block (ECE Deptt. & DoNER), Central Library, and Director’s Bungalow at NIT Agartala at a cost of Rs.2,86,51,140.00.

BWC decided that a “Revised Estimate” should be prepared excluding the lift in the Director's Bungalow, and the proposal was accordingly deferred.

**Item No. 34.8:** To consider the estimate for “Maintenance of Auditorium Hall during the year 2014-15/SH: Repairing of stage and roof” at a cost of Rs.7,42,801.00

The Building & Works Committee ratified the proposal.

**Item No. 34.9:** To consider the estimate for “Maintenance of Academic building (DONER) during the year 2014-15/SH:-Providing of moveable partition wall, false ceiling for Room No. NB-207 & water supply distribution arrangement for room no.: NB-208 to NB-211 of the new Academic Building and Hall no.: 04 of workshop no.: 02” at a cost of Rs. 4,01,144.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal for the work order already issued at a cost of Rs. 3,14,900.00 to Utpal Chakraborty, contractor.

**Item No. 34.10:** To consider the estimate for “Construction of Sentry post and internal path way with seating arrangement at Gargi Hostel at NIT Agartala” at a cost of Rs.6,39,428.00

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal to issue a work order in favour of Shree Guru Enterprise at a cost of Rs. 6,96,756.00, which is 5.8% above the estimated cost.

**Item No. 34.11:** Schedule of rate to be followed while estimating — Delhi Schedule of Rate or Tripura Schedule of Rate.

The Building & Works Committee approved that:

1.  Henceforth, estimates should be prepared based on DSR 2014. For those items which are not available in DSR, the latest TSR will be followed.
2.  The estimates which have already been prepared or are under process before taking the above decision, DSR 2012 is to be followed.
