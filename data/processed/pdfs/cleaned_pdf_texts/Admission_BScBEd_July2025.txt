III. Minimum Eligibility Requirements:

a) The candidate should pass Class XII Board Examination (10+2) or its equivalent examination securing a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate (best five subjects, including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics) from a recognized Board/Council.

AND

b) To be eligible, it is mandatory that the candidate should have studied and passed Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics subjects in Class XII Board Examination (10+2) or its equivalent examination.

AND

c) The candidate should be qualified in NCET-2025, conducted by NTA and must have appeared for the Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics tests as the domain subjects in the NCET 2025 conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

AND

d) State of eligibility (HS or OS):

i. State of eligibility means the state from where the candidate has passed Class XII (or equivalent) examination. It is to note that the state of eligibility DOES NOT depend upon the native place or the place of residence of the candidate.

IV. Selection Procedure:

i. Scrutiny of applications based on the number of available seats (category-wise) and eligible candidates in order of merit of the score obtained in the NCET-2025.

ii. Fulfillment of Minimum Eligibility requirements as per section III.

iii. A merit list will be generated by NIT Agartala based on the score obtained in NCET-2025.

Note:

1) In case of any tie, the candidate older in age will be placed above the candidate who is younger in age. If the tie-breaking criterion fails to break the tie, then higher marks in the total marks obtained in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry papers in 12th/Higher secondary level will be considered as tie-breaker.

2) In cases where a qualifying board issues only letter grades without providing the corresponding percentage of marks, the candidate must obtain and submit a certificate from the board indicating the equivalent percentage at the time of seat acceptance. In case of failure to provide such certificate, the decision of NIT Agartala will be final and binding.

3) Preference may be given to the candidates who have opted NIT Agartala while applying for NCET 2025.

4) Inclusion in the NCET 2025 scores does not guarantee admission. Admissions will be made purely on merit, based on NCET scores, and subject to seat availability at NIT Agartala.

5) All admission offers made during the seat allotment process shall be considered provisional until the candidate fulfills all admission requirements and deadlines as stipulated by NIT Agartala.

V. Online application:

All applicants must apply online at https://admission.nita.ac.in/

The portal is compatible with Mozilla Firefox. Candidates are advised to use Mozilla Firefox browser only.

Candidates should apply through online mode only. A non-refundable application fee of Rs. 1000/- for OP/OBC-NCL/EWS candidates and Rs. 500/- for SC/ST/PwD candidates has to be submitted through the online payment portal only.

Offline, handwritten, or printed applications will not be entertained.
