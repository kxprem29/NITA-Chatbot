Minutes of the 36th Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on June 28, 2016

The 36th meeting of the Building & Works Committee of National Institute of Technology, Agartala was held on June 28, 2016 at 6:00 pm in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

NIT Agartala.

* Dr. Debasish Bhattacharya, Member Secretary
Registrar (I/C), N.I.T., Agartala.

The Director (NITs), MHRD, Govt. of India could not attend the meeting.

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and requested the Registrar (I/C) and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

Item no. 36.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of 35th BWC meeting held on 10th
February, 2016 in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House,
Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

The minutes of the 35th Building & Works Committee Meeting held on 10th February 2016 in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura, were circulated to all the members. No modifications/suggestions/ comments were received. The Minutes of the 35th Building & Works Committee of NIT, Agartala was confirmed.

Item No. 36.2: Action taken on the decision of the 35th Building & Works
Committee Meeting held on 10th February, 2016 in the Conference
Room of NIT Transit House, Kunjaban, Agartala, Tripura.

The Building and Works Committee (BWC) noted the action taken report with the following observations:

[A] Ref. Sl No. 2, in respect of Item No. 35.2(2) : Regarding Upgradation of the internal main road at a cost of Rs. 12,62,33,747.00.

The BWC ratified the action taken by the Institute for inviting the 2nd call of tender.

[B] Ref. Sl No. 10, in respect of Item No. 35.2(10) : Regarding Design, Supply, Construction, Erection, trial run and commissioning of water treatment plant of capacity 2.0 MLD and construction of overhead reservoirs at a cost of Rs. 1458.00 lakhs.

Agenda will be placed in the next BWC meeting with proper justification along with detailed estimate as emphasized on priority basis by the BWC, considering its importance.

[C] Ref. Sl. No. 19, in respect of Item No. 35.9 : Regarding Supply of electrical materials for Kendriya Vidhyalaya at a cost of Rs. 80,715.00.

The BWC opined that the proposal for the supply of electrical materials may be referred to the Purchase Section of NIT Agartala.

[D] Ref. SL No. 22, in respect of Item No. 35.12 : Regarding Providing and fixing of GI exposed pipe instead of PPR pipe at 1500 boy's hostel.

In respect of providing and fixing of GI exposed pipe instead of PPR pipe, it was suggested that the Institute should act as per approved BOQ.

[E] Ref. SL No. 23, in respect of Item No. 35.13 : Regarding substitute item for fitting fixing of aluminium frame instead of sal wood frame in the work construction of 1500 capacity boys hostel.

In respect of fitting fixing of aluminium frame instead of sal wood frame, it was suggested that the Institute should act as per approved BOQ.

The Building & Works Committee (BWC) ratified & confirmed the action taken in awarding works mentioned in the respective items under agenda item no. 36.3.
