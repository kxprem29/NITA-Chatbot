Minutes of the 50th Meeting of the
Board of Governors
held on January 21, 2020

The 50th meeting of the Board of Governors of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on January 21, 2020, at 12:00 hours in the Conference Room of NIT Transit House, New Delhi.

The following members were present at the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma: Chairperson of BoG and Director, NIT Agartala.

(2) Shri D. K. Singh: Dy. Secretary (Fin), MHRD.
(Representative of the JS & FA, IFD, MHRD, Govt. of India)

(3) Shri Indrajit Kuri: Under Secretary, MHRD.
(Representative of Special Secretary / Additional Secretary / Joint Secretary dealing with Technical Education, MHRD, Govt. of India)

(4) Prof. Sharad Gokhle: Dean of Infrastructure, Planning & Management, IIT Guwahati (nominee of the Director, IITG).

(5) Shri B. Das: Dy. Residence Commissioner, Govt. of Tripura.
(Representative of the Principal Secretary to the Govt. of Tripura, Dept. of Higher Education, Agartala)

(6) Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi): Member, Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Dept., NIT Agartala.

(7) Dr. Govind Bhargava: Secretary, Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson, Board of Governors and Director of NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors.

The Chairperson invited the Registrar to initiate discussions on Agenda items.

Item No. 50.1: Confirmation of the Minutes of the 49th Meeting of Board of Governors, NIT Agartala.

The minutes of the 49th Meeting of the Board of Governors, held on November 09, 2019, were confirmed after deliberation.

Item No. 50.4: To consider the proposal to recruit two Technical personnel (on contract) for the MIS, NITA.

The BoG approved the recruitment of two technical personnel on a contract basis, in addition to the sanctioned posts of Non-Teaching staff.

Item No. 50.5: To consider the proposal to recruit a Counsellor for the students of NIT Agartala.

The BoG ratified the recruitment of a Counsellor at NIT Agartala, in addition to the sanctioned posts of Non-Teaching staff.

Item No. 50.6: To consider the recommendations of the minutes of the 43rd Building & Works Committee meeting of NIT Agartala.

The Board noted Item Nos. 43.1 to 43.14, 43.17, and 43.19 to 43.23 of the 43rd Building & Works Committee. The recommendations of the 43rd Building & Works Committee meeting held on June 21, 2019, with regard to Item Nos. 43.15, 43.16, 43.18, and 43.24.1 were approved.

Item No. 50.7: To consider the decision of temporary engagement of different Teaching and Non-Teaching employees at NIT, Agartala on a contractual basis.

The Board noted the information.

Item No. 50.8: To consider the Guidelines for engagement of Visiting Faculty at NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the guidelines for the engagement of Visiting Faculty. Since the student ratio has taken a quantum jump as compared to the sanctioned faculty strength, the engagement will be made additional to the sanctioned posts.

Item No. 50.9: To consider the Concordance Tables in respect of pensioners of NIT Agartala.

The Board noted the status and decided to process the matter at the Institute level; separate approval in this regard is not required.

Item No. 50.10: To consider the nomination of a Member to the Board of Governors.

The Board ratified the nomination of Dr. Soma Nag (Nandi), Assistant Professor, Chemical Engineering Department, NIT Agartala, as a Member of the Board of Governors.

Item No. 50.11: Discontinuation of Interviews at Junior Level Posts carrying the Grade Pay of Rs. 4600/- or less [up to Level 7 in 7th CPC].

The Board adopted the guidelines in accordance with instructions issued by DoPT vide their Office Memorandum No. 39029/01/2013-Estt (B)-Part dated December 29, 2015, which has been communicated by the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India vide letter F. No. 35-4/2016-TS.III, dated: December 11, 2019.

Item No. 50.12: Adoption of the guidelines for settlement of claims for compensation as enunciated in various judgments of Hon’ble Supreme Court in case of death/ permanent incapacitation due to unintended/ unforeseen occurrences during maintenance, operation, and provisioning of public services.

The guidelines of DoT vide Memorandum No. 36-11/2015-SR, dated: December 5, 2016, and the Institute committee are approved. However, point no. 2 of the Institute’s committee report may be read as: the guidelines will be applicable for all employees who are not covered under Group Insurance. The superannuated employees from any sector will not be covered under this scheme.

Item No. 50.13: To consider the issue mentioned below pertaining to the ongoing TEQIP-III program at NIT Agartala.

The Board approved the 4th phase Procurement Plan of Rs. 49,89,440.00 (Rupees forty-nine lakh eighty-nine thousand four hundred forty only) under TEQIP-III as per rules.

Item No. 50.14: To consider the report of the external review committee for academic audit that visited NIT Agartala during October 21-22, 2019.

The Board perused and approved the report. The Action Taken Report of the last external review committee may be put up in the forthcoming BoG meeting.

Item No. 50.15: Any other issue with the permission of the Chair.

Item No. 50.15.1: To consider the Annual Report for the year 2018-19 of the Institute.
The Board approved and confirmed the Annual Report 2018-19 prepared by the Institute, incorporating the accounts of the Institute for 2018-19 audited by the Indian Audit and Accounts Department, Office of the Accountant General (Audit), Tripura.

The meeting ended with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
