**NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AGARTALA**

**Minutes of the 39th Meeting of the Finance Committee held on July 29, 2021**

The 39th meeting of the Finance Committee of the National Institute of Technology Agartala was held on July 29, 2021, at 4:00 pm in the Virtual Class Room, NIT Agartala, Tripura, via online mode.

The following members were present in the meeting:

(1) Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Chairperson of BoG & FC and Director, NIT Agartala.
(2) Mr. Anil Kumar, Director (F. I), MoE, Govt. of India
(3) Mr. Indrajit Kuri, US (IIT), MoE, Govt. of India
(4) Dr. D. Bhattacharya, Professor, Math. Dept., NIT Agartala.
(5) Dr. Govind Bhargava, Member Secretary & Registrar, NIT, Agartala.

The Chairperson of the Finance Committee, NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting and welcomed all members of the Board of Governors. The Chairperson requested the Registrar to initiate discussion on the agenda items.

**Item No. 39.1:** Confirmation of the Minutes of 38th and 38th-A (Special) Meeting of the Finance Committee.

The Minutes of the 38th and 38th-A (Special) Finance Committee meetings were confirmed.

**Item No. 39.2:** Action taken on the decisions of the 37th Finance Committee meeting held on September 29, 2020.

The Finance Committee noted the actions taken on various decisions of the 37th Meeting of the Finance Committee.

**Item No. 39.3:** To receive information on the Financial Status of the Institute as on July 15, 2021.

The Finance Committee noted the detailed financial status of the Institute as of July 15, 2021.

**Item No. 39.4:** To consider signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India & the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2021-22 as per requirement of GFR 229(xi).

The Finance Committee ratified the decision for signing the MoU between the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India & the National Institute of Technology Agartala for the year 2021-22 as per the requirement of GFR 229(xi) and recommended that it be placed before the Board for approval.

**Item No. 39.5:** Present Status of the TEQIP-III project of NIT Agartala.

The Finance Committee noted the present status of the TEQIP-III project.

**Item No. 39.6:** To consider the Physical & Financial statement of the ongoing construction works/projects.

The Finance Committee noted the Physical & Financial statement for the ongoing construction works/projects of NIT Agartala.

**Item No. 39.7:** To consider the recommendation of the DPC for granting financial upgradation under the MACP scheme to the nine non-teaching staff of NITA.

The Finance Committee considered the recommendations of the DPC for implementation of the MACP guidelines for the following non-teaching staff:

(1) Mr. Pradip Majumder, Jr. Assistant.
(2) Mrs. Uma Saha, Attendant.
(3) Mr. Nepal Debnath, Attendant.
(4) Mr. Sukhen Saha, Jr. Assistant.
(5) Mr. Bishu Debbarma, Attendant.
(6) Mrs. Brihalata Debbarma, Attendant.
(7) Mr. Jahar Debnath, Attendant.
(8) Mr. Dilip Debbarma, Attendant.
(9) Mrs. Sonalaxmi Debbarma, Attendant.

The Finance Committee recommended that the matter be placed before the Board.

**Item No. 39.8:** To consider the proposal for "water supply arrangement at NIT Agartala by way of constructing a Ground Water Treatment Plant including construction of Deep Tube wells, Ground Level Reservoir, Raw water Rising Main, clear water Rising Mains, and other allied works etc. complete in all respects (deposit work)".

The Finance Committee recommended sanctioning the expenditure for an additional amount of Rs. 2,93,54,515.00 (Rupees two crore ninety-three lakh fifty-four thousand five hundred and fifteen only) [calculated as Rs. 11,96,79,000.00 - Rs. 9,03,24,485.00] to award the work to PWD (DWS), Govt. of Tripura, as deposit work. The Finance Committee also recommended placing the matter before the Board for administrative approval & expenditure sanction of Rs. 11,96,79,000.00 (Rupees eleven crore ninety-six lakh seventy-nine thousand) from the available Institute grant and/or IRG of the Institute.
