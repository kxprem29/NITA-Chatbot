Minutes of the 43rd Building & Works Committee Meeting
held on 21st June, 2019

The 43rd meeting of the Building & Works Committee of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on 21st June, 2019 at 4:00 P.M. in the Board Room of NIT Agartala Campus, Jirania, Agartala.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT, Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members were present:

* Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Chairman, B&WC, Director, NIT, Agartala
* Dr. S. C. Saha, Professor, Mechanical Engg. Department, Member, NIT Agartala
* Dr. Partha Pratim Sarkar, Dean (P & D) i/c, Member, NIT Agartala
* Sri Alok Chakraborty, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura (representative of Chief Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura), Member
* Sri Kajal Banik, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura, Member
* Dr. Govind Bhargava, Registrar, NIT, Agartala, Member Secretary

The Chairman welcomed all the members present in the meeting and invited the Registrar and Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions.

The comments of MHRD sent vide letter no. F No. 1-4/2017 - TS.III, dated 20th June 2019 were also deliberated.
