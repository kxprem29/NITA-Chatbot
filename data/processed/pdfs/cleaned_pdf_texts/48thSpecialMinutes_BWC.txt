Minutes of the 48th (Special) Building & Works
Committee (Special) Meeting
Held on 21st December 2021

The 48th (Special) meeting of the Building & Works Committee (Special) of the National Institute of Technology, Agartala, was held on 21st December 2021 at 4:00 PM in the Board Room of NIT Agartala Campus, Jirania, Agartala.

Prof. (Dr.) H. K. Sharma, Director and Chairman of the Building & Works Committee (B&WC), NIT Agartala, presided over the meeting.

The following members attended the meeting:

*   Prof. (Dr.) H.K. Sharma, Chairman, B&WC, Director, NIT Agartala
*   Mr. Anil Kumar, Director (Finance), MoE, Govt. of India, Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Ajoy Kumar Chakraborty, Dean (P & D), NIT Agartala, Member
*   Er. Sampa Majumder, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura (representative of Chief Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura), Member
*   Er. Kajal Banik, Superintending Engineer, PWD, Govt. of Tripura, Member
*   Prof. (Dr.) Debasish Bhattacharya, Registrar (I/C), N.I.T., Agartala, Member Secretary

The Chairman welcomed all the members present at the meeting and invited the Dean (P&D) to initiate agenda-wise discussions. Mr. Anil Kumar, Director (Finance), MoE, Govt. of India, attended the meeting online.

The Building & Works Committee confirmed the minutes of the 47th Building & Works Committee meeting as no comment was received from any member of the BWC.

Item No. 48.2: To consider the estimate for the work of "Construction of proposed Lecture Hall Complex at NIT Agartala up to ground floor (With future provision of G+2)" for an amount of Rs. 33,48,76,684.00

The Building & Works Committee approved the proposal and recommended placing the same before the FC and BoG for necessary Administrative Approval and Expenditure Sanction for Rs. 33,48,76,684.00 for the creation of a Lecture Hall Complex under the EWS Scheme. It is going to be a deposit work for CPWD; therefore, the estimate and plan can be changed accordingly as per the suggestion of the executing agency (CPWD). Additional funds beyond the sanction accorded by the Ministry will be borne by the Institute grant/IRG of the Institute.

Item No. 48.3: To consider the estimate for the work of "construction of partition wall and repairing of water supply & sanitary items for the Chemical Engineering Department at NIT Agartala (Old Library building)," for an amount of Rs. 24,12,618.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved and confirmed Technical Sanction, Administrative Approval, and Expenditure Sanction for the estimated amount of Rs. 24,12,618.00.

Item No. 48.4: To consider the estimate for the work of "construction of 1 (one) no. Bore well, including pipelines for Gargi Hostel at NIT Agartala," for an amount of Rs. 2,53,808.00.

The Building & Works Committee approved and confirmed Technical Sanction, Administrative Approval, and Expenditure Sanction for the estimated amount of Rs. 2,53,808.00.

Item No. 48.5: To consider the estimate for the work of "Internal Electrification works for the Chemical Engineering Department at NIT Agartala," for Rs. 7,95,489.00.
