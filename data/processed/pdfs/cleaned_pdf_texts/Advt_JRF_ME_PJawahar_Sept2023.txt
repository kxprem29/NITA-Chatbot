**Recruitment of Junior Research Fellow for Research Project Funded by Science and Engineering Research Board, Department of Science & Technology, Government of India**

Applications are invited for the post of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) under a sponsored research project. The details of the project and the post are given below:

**Title of the Project:** Friction Stir Welding of Aluminum Alloy (Al 6101) and Copper Alloy (C11000) for Busbar Application by Novel Hybrid Interlayer Approach

**Funding Agency:** Science and Engineering Research Board, Department of Science & Technology, Government of India

**Project Duration:** 18 July 2023 to 17 July 2026

**Principal Investigator:** Dr. P. Jawahar
Assistant Professor
Mechanical Engineering Department
NIT Agartala

**Monthly Emolument:** Rs. 31,000/- Plus HRA

**Essential Qualifications:** B.Tech / M.Tech in (Mechanical Engineering / Manufacturing Engineering / Production Engineering / Material Science Engineering / equivalent). Candidates with CSIR-UGC NET / GATE qualification will be preferred.

**Description of Responsibilities:** Perform experiments on Friction Stir Welded joints and characterize the properties of welded joints.

**Date of Interview (online):** 29th September 2023 (tentative)
