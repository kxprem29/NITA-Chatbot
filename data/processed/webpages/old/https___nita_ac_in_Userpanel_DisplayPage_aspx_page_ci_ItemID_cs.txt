Computing & ICT Unit
Office of  Vehicle Section
The Computing & ICT Unit of NIT Agartala manages the network services, website and other domain related activity in the Institute.
Some major tasks being looked after by the Computing & ICT Unit are:
Providing the internet services to the Institute.
Providing the institute email services for employees and research students of the Institute.
Hosting and managing the Institute website.
Running all useful and essential services like naming services, DHCP services, firewall, web application services, etc to the institute for different purposes.
Providing the facility of Net based installation of major operating systems and other software to the Institute.
Providing the support to the technical team of the companies visiting for campus placement of students.
