The Department of Computer Science and Engineering of National Institute
 of Technology Agartala offers undergraduate and postgraduate 
programme  where students are 
encouraged to acquire knowledge with an integrated thinking 
about education, research & development, application and training. 
The students are provided knowledge to understand the fundamental of 
basic sciences and humanities, inter disciplinary knowledge, innovation,
 creative problem solving abilities, intellectual honesty and 
professional ethics, capacity for team work in inter disciplinary, 
national and international environment.  Since its inception in 1999, the department
 has always been recognized for excellence.
The first undergraduate batch rolled out in the year 2003.
Programmes:
This Institute offers courses of study and facilities for research 
leading to following programmes under Computer Science & 
Engineering department.
