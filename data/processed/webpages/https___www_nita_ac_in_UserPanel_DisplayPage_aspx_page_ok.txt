Funding Opportunities for UG/PG students -Texmin COE NIT Agartala Mining Project Proposals
We are delighted to announce the formal establishment of a
Memorandum of Agreement (MoA)
for the
"TEXMiN–NIT Agartala Mining Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) Centre of Excellence (CoE)"
at
(Ref:
TEXMiN/NITA/COE/24-25/372
).
Under this initiative, a total budget of
₹120.00 Lakhs
for four years with yearly budget of
30.0 lakhs
has been sanctioned to support the development of
CPS-based solutions
aligned with the
TEXMiN mandate
, focusing on the following thrust areas:
Technology Development (TD)
Innovation & Entrepreneurship (IE)
This four-year collaboration aims to drive
transformative technological advancements
in the mining sector.
We are pleased to share the finalized formats for the following key components under the CoE:
Technology Development Projects
Pre-Incubation Support for Students
Undergraduate Fellowships
All documents have been prepared in accordance with
TEXMiN’s operational norms and guidelines
. For further details, please refer to the attached MoA.
To ensure transparency and adherence to best practices,
at least one representative from TEXMiN will be included in the selection process
.
Important Note:
The last date for submission of proposals under the above-mentioned schemes is
31st August 2025.
