Research Area :
Materials Science (Thin films & Nanoscience)
Molecular aggregates/nanostructures: Development of nanoscale molecular aggregates (J-aggregates/H-aggregate/excimer) and nanostructures (nanowires/nanorods) onto ultrathin films using LB/LbL technique that have potential applications toward optoelectronic devices and possess non linear optical activity.
Organic electronics: Development of switching memory devices using organic molecules and sensors using conducting polymers. 
Organo-clay hybrid film: Development of hybrid films to stabilize organic films and control over molecular organization on the ultrathin films at nanoscale.
FRET based sensor: To identify different FRET pairs and apply them for sensor applications.
